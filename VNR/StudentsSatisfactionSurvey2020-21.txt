Report on Students Satisfaction Survey (SSS) for the A.Y. 2020-21
VNRVJIET is the institute which got embedded with the inherent closed loop system by which it corrects
itself through the feedbacks/surveys on continuous basis. Students Satisfaction Survey (SSS) is one among
such surveys. 35% of the students have responded to the survey. The organisation of survey, guidelines,
questionnaire, and the results are presented as follows.
Student Satisfaction Survey
(Includes the questionnaire on Teaching -Learning Evaluation, Infrastructure and Facilities)
Guidelines for Students
To impart quality education and provide competent resources to the society, stakeholder’s inputs
create a cutting edge in achieving quality.
Our institute VNR VJIET being on the top amongst the private engineering colleges with
accreditation in NBA, NAAC, ISO strives to reach high altitudes with continuous inputs especially from
the major stakeholder’s, i.e you: our students. Your genuine input and suggestions will take it to the greater
heights. Please join us in reaching this goal and setting up a benchmark in the society in creating & nurturing
world’s best engineers.

• All questions should be compulsorily attempted.
• Each question has five responses, choose the most appropriate one.

IV. Quality Questionnaire
Provide observation / suggestions to improve the overall teaching – learning experience in
your institution.
More than 80% of students expressed their satisfaction about Teaching Learning process.
Concerning to the fairness in evaluation, 86% students expressed good satisfaction levels. More
than 85% students are happy with the course design and the material supplied during online
classes. According to the opinion of 75% students, overall online classes were well organized
Regarding the efforts made by the teachers and institute to inculcate soft skills, life skills and
employability skills, 78% students expressed their utmost satisfaction.
