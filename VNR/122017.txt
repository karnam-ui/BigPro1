4) Two innovations from VNRVJIET were selected after national level screening and
showcased in Indian pavilion in International Innovation Fair (WIIF) in China held between
23rd to 25th November 2017.Supported by International Federation of Inventors’
Associations (IFIA) and cosponsored by Association of Inventions (CAI) as well as
Foshan Municipal Government, the second World Innovation and Invention Forum (WIIF)
opened in Foshan City, Guangdong Province on November 23, 2017. Lasting for three
days, WIIF convened the delegates of over 40 countries regions and international
organizations including World Intellectual Property Organization, International Federation
of Inventors’ Association, World Association of Inventors and Entrepreneurs, European
Invention Association and etc. Invention booths displayed 600 international inventions and
2500 national ones at WIIF Forum.
Awards:
1) Bronze medal from China Association of Inventions to Prof Narayana Rao & Team for
their innovation ` Cough and wheeze analyzer for respiratory digital health services'
2) Gold award from Manila Young Inventors Association to student innovators MANO
SATYA SAI and Team for their innovation `Semi automated prosthetic hand'.
