DATE: 27/04/2015
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & Vignana Jyothi Technology Business
Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the Month of April, 2015
1) Shortlisted names of TEP / ISB (2015-2017) students of VNRVJIET have been
received on 07-04-2015 and they were encouraged to participate actively in the
subsequent process of screening for final selection.
2) The following two faculty members of VNR Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering and
Technology (VNRVJIET) have participated in a Two Day National Seminar on “Make
in India (Opportunities & Challenges)” during 24th & 25th April, 2015 at Park Haytt,
Raod No 2, Banjarahills, Hyderabad being organized by The Federation of Telengana
and Andhra Pradesh Chambers of Commerce & Industry (FTAPCCI).
i)Dr. S.V.S.S. Srinivasa Raju
ii) Sri.A.N. Brahmeswara Rao
3) “Mobile Applications” is selected as a theme for My Big Idea Competition (MBI) for the
Months of April& May, 2015 and displayed the same in Institution Network.
ED Cell & MSME VJTBI Coordinators
Sri.K.Kodanda Ram & Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju
