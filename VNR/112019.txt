A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of November, 2019
I) ED Cell Activities:
i) Mr.Y.Ritheesh, Mr.G.Sanketh , Ms.Chidrupa and Tharun have won first prize in
INNOVENTRON-2019,organized by MLRIT, Hyderabad on 02-11-2019.
ii) Entrepreneurship Development Cell(ED Cell) of VNRVJIET in association
with Indian Innovators Association (IIA) has organized an expert Talk on
“Chinese Invention System” by Dr.Zengpei Xuan, Deputy Secretary General
of China Association of Inventions (CAI) and Vice President of China
Association of Productivity Promotion Centers (CAPPC) at Dr.A.P.J.Abdul
Kalam Auditorium in our Institute on 30-11-2019 from 2 PM to 4.30 PM.128
Faculty, B.Tech and Polytechnic students have participated in the Expert
Talk.

II) MSME VJTBI:
i) Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Manager, MSME Business incubator has visited the office of
MSME-DI, Balanagar, Hyderabad on 05-11-2019 and interacted with Sri K.C.Choudhary,
Assistant Director / Nodal Officer (BI) MSME-DI, Balanagar, Hyderabad regarding the
status of MSME proposal under new Scheme and requested them to include certain
favorable comments pertaining to the profile of the Institute. However, the application was
forwarded by the Implementing agency MSME-DI, Balanagar, Hyderabad to MSME, New
Delhi through online MYMSME portal on 06-11-2019 with favorable recommendations for
grant of Host Institution / Business Incubator status to VNRVJIET.
