DATE: 28/02/2015
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & Vignana Jyothi Technology Business
Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the Month of February, 2015
1) Sri.K.Kodanda Ram & Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju interacted with I Year M.Tech ECE
VLSI and Embedded System (2015-2017) students on 02-02-2015 from 1.30 PM to
3.30 PM at P.G Block Seminar Hall on ideation, in which 25 students have attended.
2) Dr.A.Subba Rao Garu & Sri.K.Kodanda Ram held a meeting with VJTBI Department
Incubation Coordinators on 06-02-2015 from 3.00 PM to 4.00 PM at P.G Block
Seminar Hall for requesting them to encourage their UG and PG students in submitting
Business Ideas to MSME sponsored VJTBI. Eight Faculty members have attended the
meeting.
3) Dr.A.Subba Rao Garu & Sri.K.Kodanda Ram reviewed the presentations of My Big
Idea Competition (MBIC) on 06-02-2015 from 4.00 PM to 5.00 PM at P.G Block
Seminar Hall with participants of the MBIC. In which 8 students have participated.
4) Sri.K.Kodanda Ram ,Associate Professor, AE & Sri A.Ramesh Kumar , Associate
Professor ,ECE have attended a two day Faculty Development Workshop on “Design
Challenges and Mentoring” organized by TEP/ISB Team at ISB ,Hyderabad during
07-02-2015 to 08-02-2015 from 9.00AM to 5.00PM.
5) Dr.Nanditha Sethi ,Director ,Center for Public Enterprises ,Hyderabad delivered an
Expert Talk on “Entrepreneurship and its role in economic progress: Role of Youth” at
VJIM auditorium between 3.00 PM to 5.00 PM on 13-02-2015 for the students of
VNRVJIET & VJIM organized by VNRVJIET ED Cell in association with VJIM. In which
25 students of VNRVJIET and 110 students of VJIM have participated.
6) Sri.K.Kodanda Ram addressed 3rd & 2nd Year Automobile students in Automobile CAD
Lab on MSME Incubation Support to students and introduced Vignana Jyothi
Entrepreneurship Training Model (VJETM) between 1.30 PM to 4.00PM on 16-02-2015
in which 5 Students of 2 nd Year and 10 students of 3rd Year have participated.
7) Sri.K.Kodanda Ram interacted with Dr .D.N .Rao Garu, General Secretary, Vignana
Jyothi regarding the progress of Incubation activities on 23-02-2015 at RCC Discussion
Room ,P.G Block between 12.30 PM to 1.30 PM.
ED Cell & MSME VJTBI Coordinators
Sri.K.Kodanda Ram & Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju
