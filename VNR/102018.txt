A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell, & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of October, 2018
I) ED Cell Activities:
i) As a part of Career Vision Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Coordinator ED Cell has given a
talk on “The Role of an Entrepreneur in the Economic Development of the Nation” to
I-Year B.Tech CSE-1 students of VNRVJIET during 12.40 PM to 1.4 PM on
04-10-2018, in which 53 students have attended. Ms.Priyanka Singh, Assistant
Professor, CSE organized the talk.
A talk on “The Role of an Entrepreneur in the Economic Development of the Nation” by

(ii) As a Part of career vision Sri K.Kodanda Ram , Chief Coordinator, ED Cell has

2.40 PM in Room No :A-013, B-Block. Mr.R.Kranthi Kumar, Assistant Professor, CSE
organized the talk. The topics covered are Entrepreneurship, Backside of
Entrepreneurship in which to 55 students have participated.

I-Year B.Tech, EIE-2 & 3 on 23-10-2018 during 2.40 PM to 3.40 PM in Room No :
C-201,Covering topics MBIC App, Ideation ,Incubation as a part of Career Vision
Programme in which to 24 students have Attended. Mr.K.Vijaya Chandra, Assistant
Professor, EIE has coordinated the activity.
