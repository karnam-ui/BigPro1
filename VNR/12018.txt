Date: 31/01/2018
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell, & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of January, 2018
1) Dr. A.S. Rao, Director (IIE&M) has addres sed 3rd year B.Tech Students of all branches
and sections who have opted Entrepreneurship as Open Elective and discussed various
aspects of Ideation and Vignana Jyothi Entrepreneurship Training Model at K.S.
Auditorium on 10-01-2018 during 10 AM to 12.30 PM. A total of 426 students and all
faculty who are teaching this open elective are present. The students have participated
enthusiastically by sharing their innovative ideas.
A.S.Rao, Director (IIE&M) interacting with the students after the session on 10/01/2018 in
K.S. Auditorium
2) A Proposal for New Generation Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre
(NewGen IEDC) was submitted to EDI, Ahmedabad on 10/01/2018 by Speed post. This
DST proposal is eligible for a grant in aid of Rs 2.875 Crores consecutively for 5 years
for 85 student projects, which includes recurring as well as non-recurring grant. The grant
in aid for each project is Rs 2.50 Lakhs for Prototype Development for an idea
which is innovative and commercially viable.
