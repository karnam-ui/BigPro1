Features/ Initiatives of Entrepreneurship Development Cell (ED Cell) & Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (VJTBI)
1) ED Cell established with funding from AICTE, New Delhi with Rs. 8 Lakhs.
1) MSME, New Delhi support with Rs 66.5 Lakhs for 10 Business Ideas & origination
of Vignana Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (VJTBI).
2) Technology Entrepreneurship Programme (TEP) initiated by Andhra Pradesh
Society for Knowledge Networks (APSFKNW) and Designed by Indian School of
Business (ISB), Hyderabad for the students of Second year leading to a certificate.
At present 39 students are undergoing training.
3) My Big Idea Competition (MBIC) with a new theme to encourage innovation among
students on a regular basis.
4) Campus Entrepreneur initiative to Promote selling skills among the students.
5) Organized DST/ NSTEDB sponsored Entrepreneurship Awareness Camps
(EAC’s) under the scheme of NIMAT.
6) Association with Pallesrujana and National innovation Foundation (NIF) for
promoting Rural Innovation.
7) Active Students Innovation Centre.
8) Claims 50 Alumni Entrepreneurs from this Institute in various areas.
9) Organizes Workshops Seminars, Webinar, Conferences, Faculty Development
Programmes (FDPs), ECFICIO(E-Week), Hackathons, Expert Lectures and Skill
Development Programmes.
10) Organizes Industrial units visit.
