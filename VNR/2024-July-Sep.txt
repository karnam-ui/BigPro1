EDITOR’S DESK
Greetings to Faculty, Students and Friends!
It gives me immense pleasure to bringing out this issue of our Departmental News Letter.
First of all, I would like to express gratitude to Lord for guiding and inspiring each and every
one of us do our best. I would like to thank each and every one in the department for their
contribution, either directly or indirectly. Due regards to Management, Principal, Director
and Head of the department for their kind co-operation and support in every activity of the
department. This News Letter covers the various departmental activities begin with FDP,
Student achievements, Faculty achievements, Guest Lectures, Industrial visits, Faculty
research contributions during July-September 2024. Looking forward to come up with a
greater number of activities in the next issue with the co-operation of everyone of you.
Wish you happy reading. You are welcome to give your suggestions for improvement.
Thanks & Warm regards
Dr. Sujith Bobba
Assistant Professor
Department of Mechanical Engineering, VNRVJIET 2
MESSAGE FROM HEAD OF THE DEPARTMENT
Dear Faculty and Student Friends,
I am happy to inform you that our department organized a one-day workshop on Hands on
session on 6- Axis Industrial Robot. Eminent personalities have delivered the lectures and
shared their knowledge and experience. Nearly more than hundred participants have
benefitted from this FDP. Our faculty members participated in faculty development
programs, NPTEL certification courses to enhance their learning skills to benefit the students.
Some of our faculty members actively participated in research activities like reviewing the
well reputed international SCI journals. I am happy to share that student have successfully
completed short term training programs on Machine Learning with Python and Introduction
to Digital Marketing. Guest lectures and industrial visits are also regularly organized to give
exposure to the present needs of the industry. Few of our faculty members published research
papers in reputed journals which are indexed in SCI and Scopus.
Let’s all strive a bit more and go beyond our comfort zone to achieve higher peaks.
With best wishes
Dr. B.V.R. Ravi Kumar
Head of Mechanical Engineering Department
Department of Mechanical Engineering, VNRVJIET 3
STUDENT EDITOR
Dear Faculty and Department Members,
eing part of the editorial team for our department's newsletter has been a truly enriching
experience. It’s been a pleasure to help shape and share the stories that reflect the energy,
achievements, and aspirations of our department. Through this role, I’ve had the chance to
see just how much effort and enthusiasm go into every initiative—whether it's cutting-edge
research, academic milestones, or vibrant student activities. It’s both humbling and inspiring
to witness the commitment and creativity that our faculty and students bring to the table.
Working on the newsletter has not only helped me grow as an editor and communicator but
has also deepened my appreciation for the teamwork and spirit that unite us as a department.
I’m grateful for the chance to contribute to a platform that brings our community together and
celebrates what makes us unique.
Thank you for the opportunity—it’s been an honor to be part of something that highlights the
heart and soul of our academic journey.

Departmental Achievements:
1. The ASME student chapter conducted “Mechanical Mania” for I, II, III & IV B.Tech.
Mechanical Engineering students on 25-09-2024. The main purpose of the event is to
encourage students to fun-based learning. A total of 58 students attended the event
2. The Department of Mechanical Engineering is has organized an ANSYS Certificate
Course for B.Tech III year ME-A and ME-B students (AY 2024-25) which was
conducted by industry experts, the course will be held on five consecutive Tuesdays,
starting from September 10, 2024.

on session on 6- Axis Industrial Robot for II year B. Tech. ME - I students. A total of 53
students attended

conducted by Dr. Achuta Ramaiah
