A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME
Vignana Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI)
Activities for the Month of November 2024
I) ED Cell Activities:
1. ED Cell organized the E- Hive event on 08.11.2024 from 02.00 PM to 04.00 PM
at APJ Abdul Kalam Auditorium, VNRVJIET. Dr. C. Kiran, Associate Professor,
EIE, VNRVJIET delivered a talk on Kick Start Your Entrepreneurial Journey. 103
Students from different programs attended the event.
Dr. C. Kiran, Associate Professor, EIE delivering a talk on Kick Start Your
Entrepreneurial Journey on 08.11.2024
2. M. Venkata Ramana, i/c ED Cell, VNRVJIET, delivered a talk on Uprise the
entrepreneur in you (Achieving problem-solution fit and product-market fit) as
part of Career Vision Approach (CVA) on 12.11.2024 from 2:40 PM to 3:40 PM at
PEB-14, VNR VJIET. 70 students of I B.Tech. CSE-A attended the talk.
M. Venkata Ramana, i/c ED Cell delivering a talk on Uprise the entrepreneur in
you in Career Vision Approach (CVA) Session to I B.Tech. CSE-A on 12.11.2024
3. Mr Krishna Prasad, Head IIE started new initiative ‘Talks & Take aways’ where
staff can share knowledge, networking in a useful way for VNRVJIET on
06.Nov.2024 so that this can be shared with students and help them in their
technical learnings & startups Link
4. Mr Krishna Prasad, Head IIE has formed a team to launch VJ Codelabs for
latest technologies in VNRVJIET Community page. Codelabs for IOT & Web
Development were launched on 14.Nov.2024.
Website : https://community.vnrzone.site/en/StartupZone/codelab
5. M. Venkata Ramana, i/c ED Cell, VNRVJIET, delivered a talk on entrepreneur in
you as part of Career Vision Approach(CVA) on 18.11.2024 from 2:40 PM to 3:40
PM at D-412, VNR VJIET. 38 students of I B.Tech. AE attended the talk.
M. Venkata Ramana, i/c ED Cell delivering a talk on entrepreneur in you in
Career Vision Approach (CVA) Session to I B.Tech. AE students on 18.11.2024
6. ED cell has submitted data related to NIRF 2025 on Innovation, Incubation,
Innovation Grant, FDPs on Innovation to PAAC on 18-19 November 2024.
7. ED Cell organized My Big Idea Competition (MBIC) on 21.11.2024 from 03.00
PM to 04.00 PM. ED cell has received 05 ideas against the circular. 02 ideas
were presented in front of Jury members. Mr. M. Krishna Prasad, Head, IIE and
M. Venkata Ramana, i/c ED Cell acted as Jury members and gave suggestions
to the students to take these ideas to the next level.
Student presenting idea in MBIC on 21.11.2024
8. Mr Krishna Prasad, Head - IIE, VNRVJIET organised a monthly Startup
Summit where nearly 10 student entrepreneurs shared their challenges,
failures & learnings with rest of students. It was followed by ideas
presentation from another 8 students on 26.11.2024.
Monthly Startup Connect on 26.11.2024
9. Informal Entrepreneurship challenge was given to few first 1st year
students to grow money with seed fund of 1000Rs in 10 days. Three first
year students completed the challenge and had great learnings.
Final Presentation of Entrepreneurship challenge on 18.11.2024
10.VNRVJIET Startup Bandiwala applied for incubation under VJ-Hub and
started its services as alpha testing in VNRVJIET campus.
Launch of Bandiwala Startup at VNRVJIET 18.11.2024
11. Mr Krishna Prasad, Head - IIE gave mentoring sessions to over 15 teams
as consulting hours to VNRVJIET & VJIM Students.
Mentoring sessions on existing startup ideas to teams from VNRVJIET & VJIM
12.VJ-VIBES was started by Krishna Prasad to promote culture of sharing
information & enrich tech & entrepreneurship awareness among students
on 13-Nov-2024
Students doing podcasting in Studio Room on Entrepreneurship & News
13. Mr Krishna Prasad started a 4-8pm training program for students on
Python, GPU Programming, Github, Startup projects for interested
students in D319 from 07-Nov-2024 to 21-Nov-2024.
14. ED-Cell members & students interested on entrepreneurship attended
the Finals of Red Bull Innovation challenge National Finals at T-Hub on
17-Nov-2024
15.M. Venkata Ramana, i/c ED Cell invited to panel discussion for Equinox
E-Summit 2024 on 29th November 2024 from 10.00 AM to 01.00 PM by
MLRIT, Hyderabad. This platform provided an opportunity to exchange
ideas, share experiences and explore collaborative opportunities. Dr.
Zahoorullah S MD, CIO, Atal Incubation Center, ALEAP, Hyderabad and
Mrs A. Vijay, Director, We Hub, Hyderabad also invited for panel
discussion.
16.Mr. K. Krishna Prasad, Head - IIE, VNRVJIET attended the 5th Edition
of EDU Summit with the theme ‘Skills for Tomorrow: Integrating Education,
Employment & Global Competence’ on 29 November 2024
(Friday) at Hotel The Park, Somajiguda, in Hyderabad, Organized by CII
Telangana. Experts from Industries, academics and administrators are
invited as keynote speakers.
Mr. K. Krishna Prasad, Head - IIE, VNRVJIET at EDU Summit, Organized
by CII,Hyderabad
