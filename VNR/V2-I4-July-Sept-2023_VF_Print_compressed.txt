After the movie was done, we ran into people we knew and weirdly, the number kept increasing!
In a few seconds, we went from 5 to 15!

asking other girls if they would like to join us. After recruiting a sufficient number, we all posed
for the camera.

had to leave. Since this day would never come again, we decided to be bold. On our way to the
entrance, we started greeting the pink girls 'Hi, Barbie!' and they greeted us back with the same
enthusiasm.
