A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell Activities for the Month of August, 2014
1) AIESEC workshop was held at convention centre Film City, Hyderabad in which Mozilla
Firefox technical sessions and E-talks were held on 10-08-2014.In his event 20 students of
VNRVJIET have participated.
2) NEN consultant Smt. Vishnu Priya interacted with principal and students regarding the
progress of ED Cell activities on 20-08-2014.
3) NEN Faculty Leaders meet was organized at Kasthurbha Gandhi Degree & P.G college for
Women, Hyderabad by NEN on 21-082014 in which 4 students of VNRVJIET ED Cell student
members.
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell Activities for the Month of September, 2014
1) Dr A.Subba Rao ,Director, VNRVJIET interacted with 30 students of TEP/ISB on 01-09-2014
2) A webnar by name WEBX by TEP/ISB was arranged on 09-09-2014 on Learning Management
Systems in which 30 students attended.
3) During Inaugural of Induction Programme a stall was setup by ED Cell faculty & students on
11-09-2014. About 37 students have shown inclination to be part of ED Cell activities.
4) My Big Idea Competition final presentation by the student participants before the selection
committee for selecting winners held on 27-09-2014 at P.G seminar hall.
