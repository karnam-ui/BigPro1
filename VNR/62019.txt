A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of June, 2019
I) MSME VJTBI Activities:
1) As per the E-Mail received dated 03-06-2019 from Sri K.C.Choudhary, Assistant
Director / Nodal Officer (BI) MSME-DI, Balanagar, Hyderabad regarding the registration
of our Institution as Host Institution(HI) / Business Incubator(BI) under the new scheme

VNRVJIET VJTBI has visited the office along with Dr S.Pranavananda Incubatee,
MSME VJTBI to clarify his doubts regarding the status of BI under old scheme attended
an “Awareness Programme on Support for Entrepreneurial and Managerial
Development of MSMEs through Incubator” on 11-06-2019 and met the officers
concerned for obtaining necessary inputs to complete the process of registration under
new scheme.
Awareness Programme was inaugurated by Sri.D.Chandra Sekhar,Additional Industrial
Adviser, MSME-DI Balanagar on 11-06-2019
