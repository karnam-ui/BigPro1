A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell, & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of May, 2018
I) ED Cell Activities:
ORL Makers garage-2K18 is organizing a workshop during 21/05/2018 to 06/06/2018 in
VNRVJIET campus. About 70 students from various engineering colleges are participating
in this workshop. Out of these 20 students are from VNRVJIET representing different
branches. The selected students will be showcasing their prototypes in Amravathi, AP during the
months of June and July,2018.
Coordinators of ORL Makers Garage -2K18
II) VNR VJIET TEP / ISB:
Curtain raiser event of the Technology Entrepreneurship Program (TEP) Version-2
designed by Indian School of Business (ISB), Hyderabad and sponsored by Telangana
Academy for Skill and Knowledge (TASK) was held on 14/05/2018 at ISB, Hyderabad.
Principals, Directors and Representatives of various engineering colleges have attended the
meeting. Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju and Sri K.Kodanda Ram have represented on behalf of
VNRVJIET. Two years programme of TEP / ISB has been condensed to one year keeping
other terms and conditions unaltered. The student enters into TEP certification Programme
in B.Tech second year second semester and completes by the end of third year first
semester.
