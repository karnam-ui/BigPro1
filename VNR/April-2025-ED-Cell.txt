3. Entrepreneurship Development Cell, VNRVJIET and Centre for Entrepreneurship
Development in Association with Consortium of Women Entrepreneurs Association
(UBUNTU), Hyderabad was organized One Day Digital Marketing Offline Workshop at
APJ Abdul Kalam Hall on 16.04.2025 from 10.00 AM to 04.00 PM. 05 Faculty members
from VNRVJIET, 95 members from Sri Sri Holistic, ALEAP, Rishi UBR Women’s
College, Hyderabad and BVRIT, Narsapur attended the workshop.
One Day Digital Marketing Offline Workshop on 16.04.2025
4. Entrepreneurship Development Cell, VNRVJIET and Intellectual Property Cell
organized World Intellectual Property Day - 2025 (Theme: IP and Music: Feel the
Beat of IP) on 26.04.2025 from 03.00 PM to 04.30 PM. Dr. B. Chennakesava Rao,
Director for Advancement, 38 Faculty members and 29 students from different
departments attended the event. Mr. M. Naveen Kumar, Assistant Director, DFO,
MSME, Balanagar, Hyderabad and Mr. K. Ravindranadh, Cognogen Private Limited,
Hyderabad invited as guests. Mr. M. Naveen Kumar delivered lecture on the various
MSME schemes for funding specially covered on Intellectual Property related schemes
and Mr. K. Ravindranadh enlightened the importance of Intellectual Property protection
in line with World IP day 2025 theme.
World Intellectual Property Day - 2025
