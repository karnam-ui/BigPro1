A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for
the Month of April 2022.
I) MSME & VJTBI Activities:
1) A Selection / Evaluation Committee with 5 members was constituted as per
the scheme guidelines and a Meeting of MSME, New Delhi Sponsored
Vignana Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (VJTBI) was held to shortlist
the Business Ideas received for MSME– Idea Hackathon 2022 under the
scheme of “Support for Entrepreneurial and Managerial Development of
SMEs through Incubators” to forward to the Development Commissioner -
MSME, New Delhi on 6th April, 2022 between 10 AM to 1 PM at Board
Room (B-107), VNR VJIET, Hyderabad.
