Under the chairmanship of Dr A S Rao garu, the committee met on 25/06/2015 and
decided to be available to all Faculty & Students in old T & P Room (P-002) between
3PM to 4PM on all working days, to discuss the issues related to Entrepreneurship
Development ,Industry & Institute Interaction, as well as guide them in promoting their
innovative ideas for possible incubation.

2) Members of Industry Institute Interaction and Entrepreneurship Development
Committee met with Dr.D.N.Rao Garu, Directors and Principal on 26-06-2015 at
Board Room to discuss about different ideas with respect to the Possibilities of their
Incubation. Dr.D.N.Rao Garu directed the committee to work towards centre for
excellence on Road Safety and to establish Smart Water Grid Technology Education
& Incubation Centre at our institution in consultation with the government of
Telangana state. In addition to above the following points also discussed in the
meeting
i) One team should visit to Eruvaka Technologies Pvt Ltd (IOT in Aquaculture),
Vijayawada (Website:www.eruvaka.com (Sreeram Raavi is the Founder,
Cell: 9247307318)
ii) Team should visit TS Water Grid and one working system has to be developed
in the institute within 3 months
iii) To develop a sensor based Water identification system to allow cars to enter on
to the rods and pits during rain.
iv) To develop a project in such a way that to provide pure drinking water facility at
all places in place of plastic water bottles in order to reduce the problem of
accumulation of plastic waste.
v) To make ample provision for trapping rain water in the form of soaking pits for
recharging ground water levels.
