A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of April, 2019
I) ED Cell Activities:
1) Sri.Nikhil Kumar, Sri M.Sai Kiran, Sri S.K Hasvik Sai, Sri.M.Karan Kumar and
Sri.A.Chandra Sai have participated in I Summit -19 awards, innovators challenge-
2019 held at Sri Satya Sai Auditorium, Hyderabad on 07-04-2019, conducted by
Literacy Helping Hands. The above team of students was placed in top 40 teams for
their project work on shield mask.
2) My Big Idea Competition (MBIC) March, 2019 presentations were held at P.G. Block
seminar hall on 09/04/2019 between 3 PM to 5 PM in which 6 teams have presented
their Innovative Ideas under open theme category. Dr.A.S.Rao , Sri.K.Kodanda Ram
and Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju have acted as judges in screening the ideas.
Participants and Judges of My Big Idea Competition held at P.G. Block Seminar
Hall on 09-04-2019
