A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for
the Month of January 2026
I. ED Cell Activities:
1. The ED Cell organized an Expert Talk on “Entrepreneurship Ecosystem” on
13.01.2025 from 2:00 PM to 3:30 PM at the B-Block Seminar Hall. Mr. Fakhruddin
Sheik, Deputy Director, Venture Development Centre, GITAM Deemed University,
Hyderabad Campus delivered the talk. Mr. Fakhruddin Sheik discussed market
strategies, business ideas, startups, pillars of venture development, and elements
of the entrepreneurship ecosystem. A total of 37 B.Tech. students and 8 faculty
members attended the event.
II. MSME & VJTBI Activities:
1. The development of the two ideas under MSME Hackathon 2.0 is in progress (Dr.
K. Ajay Kumar, MED, and Dr. D. Ramesh Reddy, ECE).
2. The development of the one Idea under MSME Hackathon 3.0 is in progress. (Mrs.
K. Bhagya Rekha, Assistant Professor, CSE, VNRVJIET).

Mr. T. Karthik Reddy, IV B.Tech ECE student, under the mentorship of Dr. D.
