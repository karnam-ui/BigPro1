HoD MESSAGE
Dear Students, Faculty, and Readers,
It is with great pride and enthusiasm that I welcome you
to the September-2025 edition of our newsletter “Digital
Dynamos” the official newsletter of the Department of
CSE-Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning &
Internet of Things (AIML & IoT). In this rapidly evolving
technological era, AIML & IoT are shaping the future,
transforming industries, and redefining the way we
interact with the world. Our department remains
committed to fostering innovation, research, and
excellence in these domains.
Through this newsletter, we aim to highlight the
remarkable achievements of our students and faculty,
ongoing research initiatives, industrial collaborations, and
recent advancements in the field. I encourage all students
and faculty members to actively participate, contribute,
and explore the endless possibilities that AIML & IoT
offer.
Let us continue to learn, innovate, and lead with passion
and commitment. Looking forward to an exciting journey
ahead!
Dr. Sagar Yeruva
Head of Department
Department of CSE-AIML & IoT

Department Highlights
The Department is pleased to announce the introduction of a new postgraduate program,
M.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (AIDS), with an intake capacity of 18 seats.
The department enthusiastically celebrated Durga Puja, bringing everyone together to honor
the tradition and deepen cultural connections.The celebration was held on September 27, 2025
with great pomp and joy.
The second-year students of the department warmly welcomed the first-year students by
organizing a Fresher’s Day celebration on September 26, 2025, at the KS Auditorium.
The Department of CSE–AIML & IoT organized a hands-on session on “Agentic AI and DevOps”
by Mr. Sairam Peddigari, CEO of CITTA Communities, on September 10, 2025. The event was
coordinated by faculty members Dr. A. Harshavardhan Sr. Assistant Professor, Ms. N. Haritha
Assistant Professor, Mr. D. Bupesh Assistant Professor, and Dr. Sayanti Chatterjee Assistant
Professor.
The department of CSE-AIML & IoT Heartily Congratulates Ms Sayeeda Khanum Pathan,
Assistant Professor, Dept of CSE(AIML & IoT) for successfully completing her Ph.D Viva-Voice
from KLU on 27.09.2025.
As part of its outreach activities, the department has donated a Samsung television to the
underprivileged pre-primary school children of Brahmanapally village, bringing joy and support
to the young learners.
