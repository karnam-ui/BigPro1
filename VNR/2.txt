A Report on Design Thinking Workshop (04-07-2014 to 06-07-2014)
A three Day workshop on “Design Thinking” as a part of Technology Entrepreneurship
Programme (TEP) of Indian School of Business (ISB), Hyderabad was held at VNRVJIET
Campus in association with Department of Mechanical and Automobile Engineering during
04-07-2014 to 06-07-2014 for the students of VNRVJIET, GRIET & NIT, Warangal as a nodal
centre.TEP is a two year certificate programme initiated by Andhra Pradesh Society For
Knowledge Networks”(APSFKNW) ,designed by ISB for II year B.Tech & II Sem students of
all branches of engineering. The students participated in the workshop actively with a great
enthusiasm. The students worked on Ideation to Prototype building activity using thermocole,
cardboard sheets & clay. Eminent faculty of ISB by names Deepankar Bhattacharyya, Ripul
Kumar, Kiran Nalluri & Honey Bajaj are part of this programme who motivated the students to
take up Technology Entrepreneurship as an alternative for employment.

A Report on Technology Entrepreneurship Programme (TEP)
Technology Entrepreneurship Programme (TEP) designed by Indian school of
Business (ISB), Hyderabad and is an Initiative of Andhra Pradesh Society For
Knowledge Networks (APSFKNW).It is a two years certificate course on
Technology Entrepreneurship starting from II Year II Semester of all branches of
Engineering(B.Tech) students.
VNRVJIET is one among top 20 Colleges in United Andhra Pradesh to offer this
programme to 50 students from each college every year as host institute. However
ISB has selected 39 students from VNRVJIET during(2014-2016),after completing
a screening process of 4 to 6 months. These selected students will be given
systematic training with a curriculum designed by ISB leading to a certificate at the
end of IV Year II Semester. Thus students will be getting an additional certificate on
Technology Entrepreneurship at the end of 4 years of B.Tech course.
1
This certificate will be useful to initiate Entrepreneurship, Technology Leaders,
Product Designers and even for Placements also. The fee structure is 8 Thousand
Rupees per student per year. Constant Instructions & Evaluation will be done by off
& on line modes by ISB. However for SC & ST students, there is no fee.
