A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for
the Month of February 2022.
I) ED Cell Activities:
1) Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju and Dr. M.Venkata Ramana have registered
and attended a Webinar (Virtual Session) through CISCO Webex Platform on
“Intellectual Property Rights and It's Protection" on 14-02-2022
between 11.45 AM to 1 PM. On the occasion of 75th anniversary of
independence, the Government of India has begun a mission to
spread awareness on IPR among faculty students under the scheme
– National Intellectual Property Awareness Mission (NIPAM). As a
part of this mission, This Webinar was organized by the Institute of
Innovation Council Narasaraopeta Engineering College, Andhra
Pradesh in association with the Office of the Controller General of
Patents, Trademarks and Designs (CGPDTM) (Chennai and New
Delhi). Mr.Vivek Raj, (NIPAM Officer, IPR Awareness
Program),Examiner of Patents & Designs, Intellectual Property Office,
Chennai, Ministry of Commerce & Industry, Govt. of India has acted
as the Resource person.
2) Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju and Dr. M.Venkata Ramana Coordinators of ED
Cell have attended a review meeting on how to improve Institutes Ranking
in ARIIA? along with Department Faculty ED Cell Coordinators and other
Cell In charges organized by Dr.C.D.Naidu, Principal and
Dr. B.Chennakesava Rao, Director for Advancement and Dean at Board
Room on 24-02-2022 between 3PM to 5PM monitored by PAAC Team.
(ED Cell & MSME VJTBI Coordinators)
Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Coordinator, ED Cell & Manager, MSME Business
Incubator
Dr.M.Venkata Ramana, Professor of Automobile Engineering & Coordinator, ED Cell.
