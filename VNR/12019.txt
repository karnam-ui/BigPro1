A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell, & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of January, 2019
I) ED Cell Activities:
1) A webinar was organized in association with TEP/ISB on the topic”Orientation to
TEP and its Learning Management System (LMS)”on 18-01-2019 at Room No:
P-003(PG Seminar Hall) in which 12 students of(2018-2020)Batch have
attended.
2) A 36 hour Hackathon was organized in association with J hub, JNTUH in
VNRVJIET campus.33 teams from different engineering colleges have
participated and 3 teams have come to next level of Hackathon from
VNRVJIET. Dr C. Kiran, Assistant Professor EIE associated with the activity.
3) An entrepreneur fest by name ECFICIO 2.0 (Release 2.0) was held for 2 days in
VNRVJIET during 22-01-2019 to 23-01-2019. In this connection several
competitions are conducted and different student teams have participated in this
fest. Dr C. Kiran, Assistant Professor EIE Coordinated the activity with the help
of student body of ED Cell, VNEVJIET. Sri. K.Kodanda Ram and
Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju Coordinators of ED Cell have extended their support
by promoting the fest.
