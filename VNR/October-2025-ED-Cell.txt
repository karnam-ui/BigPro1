A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for
the Month of October 2025
I. ED Cell Activities:
1. ED Cell in association with Hult Prize of VNRVJIET have organized Startup Shuffle
on 07 and 08 October 2025 from 02.00 PM to 04.30 PM in E - 437 and E - 438. The
participants pitched creative solutions to real-world challenges. The competition
featured engaging rounds like Jeopardy Game and Pitch Attack, designed to test
knowledge, strategy, quick thinking, and presentation skills. 45 students from
different programmes attended the event.
Participants in Startup Shuffle event
2. ED Cell in Association with Institute Industry Relations (IIR) of VNRVJIET have
organized a One Day Workshop on Awareness Programme on Entrepreneurship on
13.10.2025 from 01.40 PM to 04.00 PM at B- Block Seminar Hall. 133 students from
CE, ME and AE and 02 faculty members attended the event. Mr. P. Abdul Khader,
Deputy Manager (Technology) at National Small Industries Corporation, Technical
Services Centre, Hyderabad created awareness on Entrepreneurship, start-ups,
various business models, labour laws.
Speaker Mr. P. Abdul Khader with Participants in Startup Shuffle event
