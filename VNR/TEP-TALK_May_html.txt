“Navigator Shoe, helps the visually impaired people to efficiently detect the obstacles in the range of 1-2 meters and gives a feedback through a vibration once the obstacle is detected. It is a stand alone device and doesn’t need any internet or smart phone connectivity, the sensors, processor and the motor are all installed in the shoe.
This device intends to help the visually impaired people to have a safer and smoother navigation in public places.”
