A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities
for the Month of August 2022.
I) ED Cell Activities:
1) Dr. M.Venkata Ramana and Dr S.V .S.S.Srinivasa Raju Coordinators of
Entrepreneurship Development Cell(ED Cell) have taken initiative in
forming one team from our institution on 08-08-2022 to participate in
Entrepreneurship Development Drive (EDD) Sponsored by E-Cell, Indian
Institute of Technology, Madras ,which is going to be held in the month of
September 2022.
2) Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Coordinator ED Cell has attended a one day
Regional Meet of Institution’s Innovation Council (IIC) representing IP / Tech
Transfer Track along with another 3 Faculty Members Dr.C.Kiran, Associate
Professor, EIE, VNRVJIET , representing IIC Track Dr.S.Pranavanand,
Associate Professor, EIE, representing, NISP Track and Mr.P.Narendra,
Assistant Professor, ME representing, ARIIA Track nominated by the
Director, VNRVJIET held at Sreenidhi Institute of Science and
Technology(SNIST),Ghatkesar, Hyderabad on 12-08-2022.
Participants of IIC Regional Meet at the Venue, SNIST on 12-08-2022.
3) Entrepreneurship Development Cell (ED Cell) of VNRVJIET has organized
My Big Idea Competition (MBIC) for shortlisted ideas received on line. Ideas
are presented at PG-Block Seminar Hall on 16-08-2022. Dr.K.Ajay Kumar,
Associate Professor, ME and Dr.C.Kiran Associate Professor, EIE have
acted as judges in the process of selecting winners of the Competition.
Dr.M.Venkata Ramana, Incharge ED Cell and Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju,
Coordinator ED Cell have coordinated this activity.
My Big Idea (MBIC) Presentations at PG Block Seminar Hall on 16-08-2022
4) Dr. M.Venkata Ramana and Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju Coordinators of
Entrepreneurship Development Cell(ED Cell) have taken initiative in
forming one team from our institution on 20-08-2021 to participate in
National Entrepreneurship Challenge sponsored by E-Cell, Indian Institute
of Technology, Bombay, which is going to be held in the month of
September 2022.
II) MSME & VJTBI Activities:
1) Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Manage r, MSME Business Incubator, and
Dr.M.Venkata Ramana, Chief Coordinator of ED Cell have initiated the
process of opening Zero Balance Current Account with an authorization to
Principal and Director, Advancement of VNRVJIET to operate the Account
jointly at SBI,JNTUH branch, Hyderabad. The Account is in the name of
“Vallurupalli Nageswara Rao Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering
and Technology” as Host Institution (HI), approved by MSME, New Delhi
for receiving funds from GoI. Sri. M.Venkata Krishna Rao, Junior
Accountant of Accounts Department, VNRVJIET is Coordinating this
activity.
