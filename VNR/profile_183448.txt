Dr. Narra Dhanalakshmi is currently an Associate Professor in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at the VNRVJIET, Hyderabad, India. She has been with VNRVJIET since 2003. She is interested in the research areas of video content analysis, Artificial Intelligence, Embedded Systems and IoT.
