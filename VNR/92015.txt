DATE: 30/09/2015
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell ,IIPC & Vignana Jyothi
Technology Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of September, 2015
1) “Design Thinking Workshop” sponsore d by Telangana Academy of Skill
and Knowledge (TASK) and designed by ISB, Hyderabad was organized
at GRIET, Hyderabad for III Year B.Tech students of TEP / ISB
2015-2017 batch during 31st August and 1st September, 2015, in which 48
students of VNRVJIET and 19 Students of GRIET have participated.
2) Dr. Sandeep Chatterjee representing NGS Solutions COM Private
Limited, CEO of the company has visited VNR VJIET on 14-09-2015 and
entered an MoU for academic interface program with VNR VJIET. In this
connection a meeting was held with Dr. D.N. Rao, General Secretary,
Vignan Jyothi and Narne Prabhakar Rao, Member Vignan Jyothi,
Dr. S.S. Dasaka, CEO, Vignan Jyothi and Directors Dr A.Subba Rao and
Dr. A.Subananda Rao along with Deans, Principal and HOD’s of all the
Departments.
3) Dr. Sandeep Chatterjee,Ph.D gave a power point presentation to I Year
B.Tech students of CSE and IT on the topic “ Lessons from
Commercializing Ideas from Colleges Around the World” in K.S
Auditorium between 10.30 AM to 11.45 AM. In which 120 students have
participated.
4) Dr. Sandeep Chatterjee has also interacted with 20 TEP/ISB students of
VNRVJIET during 12.15PM to 1.30PM in P.G Block seminar Hall, on
commercialization of ideas and how to take forward these ideas.
5) VNRVJIET Entrepreneurship Development Cell(ED Cell) has organized
pre –Eureka one day workshop for budding entrepreneurs and students in
association with Students body of VNRVJIET ED Cell and E Cell, IIT,
Bombay, on 22-09-2015 at VJIM auditorium between 10 AM to 5 PM in
which 95 students of different branches of VNRVJIET and 10 students of
1
VJIM (Totaling 100 students) have participated. This workshop involved in
group activities, interactive sessions, case studies, samples,
presentations ,making business models and discussions. This series of
pre-Eureka workshop opens up excellent networking opportunities in India
and the Silicon Valley to the participants. A certificate and Start –up kit
given to all the participants.
6) Submitted the list of MSME short listed (9) Project Applications and
Minutes of the meeting to the office of DC-MSME, New Delhi on
22-09-2015 after final review by Dr. A. Subba Rao, Director,
Dr.C.Kiranmai, Principal, Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Incubation Manager,
VJTBI and Sri.S.V. Narayana Rao, Member, VJTBI
ED Cell, IIPC & MSME VJTBI Coordinators
