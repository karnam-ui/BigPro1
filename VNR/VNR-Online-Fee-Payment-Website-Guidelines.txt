All B.Tech & M.Tech Students ( Except-I Year) Must Pay Tuition Fee, Hostel
Fee, Transport Fee Through Website only.
Allowed Payment Types: 1) Internet Banking 2) Debit/Credit Card 3) UPI (UPI ID)
Note: Rupay Credit & Rupay Debit Cards are not allowed to pay the fee.
Steps to follow for Fee Payment through VNR VJIET Website:
1) Enter www.vnrvjiet.in
2) Select Fee Payment which is below of Spotlight
3) Enter Tuition Fee Payment:
4) Enter Roll Number and Click Validate ( Note: Not Required to Enter URN Number Field)
5) Fill All * Mark Fields Mandatorily
6) Check Box the Terms & Condition box and Select the Payment Method out of 3 Payment Methods.

6) Check Box the Terms & Condition box and Select the Payment Method out of 3 Payment Methods.
7) Example, If you select UPI, You have to enter the UPI ID, then you will get a pop to your UPI Account,
There after you have to make the payment from your G-Pay, Pay -pay by giving Pin No.
.
After Payment Successful Axis Bank’s Payment Slip (Receipt) will be downloaded automatically, Save
the same pdf receipt or take the print for proof of payment.
Show the receipt at Accounts Section after 3-5 working Days and collect the Original Fee Receipt.
