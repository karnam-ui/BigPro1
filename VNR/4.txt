A Report on Inspection of Business Incubator by
Mr Alak Kr .Mitra, Assistant Director, MSME, New Delhi
Date:17-11-2014
Mr Alak Kr .Mitra, Assistant Director, MSME, New Delhi has visited VNRVJIET for inspecting
Vignana Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (VJTBI) to review its progress on 17-11-2014
between 6 PM to 8 PM in the Board Room.
The following members have attended the meeting
i) Dr A.S.Rao, Director, Innovation Incubation Entrepreneurship and Globalization
ii) Dr.C.Kiranmai, Vice Principal & Dean Academic Affairs
iii) Dr. B.Ravindra Babu, Dean Administration & Finance
iv) Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju,Business Incubator Manager
v) Sri.K.Kodanda Ram, Chief Coordinator, ED Cell
vi) Dr.G.Ramesh Chandra, HOD,RCC
vii) Sri.T.Sunil Kumar, Associate Professor, CSE
He visited ED Cell, facilities provided at Incubation centre and RCC .He appreciated the
efforts made by the college in promoting the culture of Entrepreneurship across the campus.
He added that ongoing RCC projects can also be considered under this scheme. He
complemented the adequacy of the infrastructural facilities for incubating the Business Ideas
within the campus .He reviewed the progress made and suggested to send 2 or 3 Business
Ideas in one lot rather than 10 business Ideas at once, which are innovative in nature, cost
effective and having commercial viability. He emphasized on Business Ideas leading to
tangible products rather than IT products. He also suggested to encourage students to submit
their business ideas as per the prescribed format along with the Business Plan.
