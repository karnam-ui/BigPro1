INSTRUCTIONS AND INFORMATION TO CANDIDATES
1. VNR VJIET, located in serene atmosphere, just about 06 km from JNTU, Hyderabad is an Institute
of repute run by a philanthropic society formed by highly qualified and experienced entrepreneurs,
industrialists and academicians. It is on a high growth path and provides good opportunities for the
growth of employees.
2. VNR VJIET, invites desirable candidates, academically oriented and qualified candidates for the
post of Assistant Professor.
3. Qualifications and Salary as per AICTE norms. Institute offers perks such as EPF, Health
Insurance, Assistance to attend International/National Conference, Incentives for publications
and for good performance, research projects, etc. Application form can be downloaded from
our website: www.vnrvjiet.ac.in and send an advance copy of your application to
careers@vnrvjiet.in.
4. Retired persons with less than 65 years of age and of sound health can also apply. Higher start
for Experienced and well qualified person.
5. The qualified candidates should email their CV, Application and Annexure -I to
careers@vnrvjiet.in on or before 31-05-2025.
6. Candidates must be in sound health. He / She must be prepared to undergo such medical examination and
satisfy such medical authority as the Institute may prescribe.
7. Every successful candidate will be informed of the result of his/her application in due course
and any interim enquiries about the result will, therefore, not be answered.
1
8. (i) Candidates should satisfy themselves that they are eligible to apply before filling up the application
form. The conditions prescribed cannot be relaxed; and
(ii) Candidates who are already in service (whether permanent or temporary) should submit their
applications through the Heads of their Department/Organization OR produce No Objection Certificate.
9. A candidate who is found to have knowingly furnished any particulars which are false or to have
suppressed material information, will be disqualified and, if appointed, will be liable for dismissal
without notice.
10 Possession of minimum qualification does not automatically entitle a candidate to be called for
Interview.
11. DOCUMENTS TO ACCOMPANY THE APPLICATION FORM
a) Academic qualifications: - True copies of degrees, and Last Pay Certificate in case of those
employed, and other certificates of academic nature certified by Gazette Officers (other than
the applicant) should be attached to the application form. All certificates in original must be
produced only at the time of interview and shall not be attached to the Application Form, if
any of the originals are not produced at the time of interview when asked for, the candidates
are liable to be disqualified,
b) Character Certificate: - Candidate must submit a certificate in original together with a copy
thereof from a person in authority (e.g. Principal, Professor, Dean etc.) at an educational
institution which the candidate is attending or has attended for at least one year during the
three years immediately preceding the date of his/her application. The signatory of the
certificate should give his designation in full; and
12. If the applicant is employed and he/she feels that his/her application will not reach the Institute in time,
if forwarded through proper channel, he may fill in an advanced copy of the application on the prescribed
form and submit it to the Institute and get the original application forwarded through proper channel or
produce NOC from the present employer at the time of interview.
13. The date of the interview will be intimated by post under Certificate of Posting and also through E -
Mail. The Office will not be responsible for the late or non-delivery of the intimation posted to
candidates. Candidates are therefore, advised to give their E-mail address, so that the intimation can be
sent by E- Mail also.
14. The selected candidates will be required to join duty immediately from the date of issue of appointment
orders unless otherwise instructed in the order of appointment.
15. The Management reserves the right to fill up the vacancies or otherwise at its discretion.
