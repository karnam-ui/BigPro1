The Department of Mathematics and Management Sciences, with highly
                    qualified and experienced faculty, focuses on providing a strong
                    mathematical foundation tailored to engineering applications and
                    developing business acumen. Its curriculum and research activities are
                    specifically designed to support the analytical, problem-solving,
                    computational, business, and entrepreneurial skills engineers need
                    across disciplines. Our experienced faculty, with academic and
                    research expertise from prestigious institutions like NIT, IIT, and
                    various state universities, is central to this mission. The Department
                    offers Applied Mathematics in Artificial Intelligence, Statistical
                    Methods for Data Science, Mathematical Foundations of Artificial
                    Intelligence as open electives and Essential Mathematics for Machine
                    Learning as professional Elective. Also our department offers BBA
                    Business Analytics, BFSI, Innovation and Entrepreneurship minor
                    degrees and open electives like Personal Finance and Tax Planning,
                    HRM, Marketing Management. Overall, the Department of Mathematics and
                    Management Sciences is geared towards equipping students with
                    real-time, high-demand Mathematical and Management skills that are
                    directly applicable to engineering challenges.
The Department of Mathematics and Management Sciences, with highly
                            qualified and experienced faculty, focuses on providing a strong
                            mathematical foundation tailored to engineering applications and
                            developing business acumen. Its curriculum and research activities
                            are specifically designed to support the analytical,
                            problem-solving, computational, business, and entrepreneurial
                            skills engineers need across disciplines. Our experienced faculty,
                            with academic and research expertise from prestigious institutions
                            like NIT, IIT, and various state universities, is central to this
                            mission. The Department offers Applied Mathematics in Artificial
                            Intelligence, Statistical Methods for Data Science, Mathematical
                            Foundations of Artificial Intelligence as open electives and
                            Essential Mathematics for Machine Learning as professional
                            Elective. Also our department offers BBA Business Analytics, BFSI,
                            Innovation and Entrepreneurship minor degrees and open electives
                            like Personal Finance and Tax Planning, HRM, Marketing Management.
                            Overall, the Department of Mathematics and Management Sciences is
                            geared towards equipping students with real-time, high-demand
                            Mathematical and Management skills that are directly applicable to
                            engineering challenges.
To build a centre of excellence of an international repute in
                                    research, training, and education in the disciplines of
                                    Mathematics and Management and to inculcate higher order
                                    thinking skills to meet the needs of industry and society.
To attract students by providing a learning environment where
                                    they can develop and cultivate critical thinking, innovative,
                                    and leadership skills to solve real-world problems.
