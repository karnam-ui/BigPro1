5.5. Research Interests :
 Bhojpuri Folk Traditions and Oral Cultures: Exploring the performative, aesthetic, and
socio-cultural dimensions of Bhojpuri folksongs and Performing traditions
 Gender and Embodiment in Vernacular Contexts: Investigating how rural women's
embodied experiences and labour are articulated through oral and performative traditions.
 Ecofeminism and Environmental Humanities: Studying intersections of ecology, gender, and
rural knowledge systems in South Asian cultural texts.
 Cultural Memory and Resistance: Examining how oral traditions function as repositories of
memory and subtle resistance in patriarchal and caste-based societies.
 Interdisciplinary Humanities: Interested in the convergence of performance studies, folklore,
gender studies, and environmental thought within vernacular frameworks.
