According to the survey, about 93 % of the students expressed their satisfaction about Teaching
approach and the syllabus coverage. Concerning the fairness in evaluation, 96% of students
expressed good satisfaction levels. 83% of the students expressed their satisfaction with
teachers' approach for identifying their strengths or weaknesses and supporting the challenges
faced. 89% of students are happy with the institute taking active interest in promoting
internship, student exchange, field visit opportunities for students. The Mentoring process in
the institute facilitating cognitive, social and emotional growth, could gain satisfaction levels up
to EXCELLENT and Good from more than 88% of students. Regarding the efforts made by the
teachers and institute to inculcate soft skills, life skills and employability skills, 92% of students
expressed their satisfaction. About 87% of the students expressed their satisfaction about
training provided in the institute towards placement and 88% expressed their satisfaction with
encouragement for participation in cocurricular and extracurricular activities. 96% of the
students are satisfied with overall quality of teaching-learning process in the institute.
