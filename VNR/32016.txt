Date: 28/03/2016
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell , IIPC & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of March, 2016
1) Entrepreneurship Awareness Camps organize d as a part of E-Week during 29-02-2016
to 05-03-2016 at our Campus for the students of VNRVJIET.
2) All the rooms earmarked for Incubation Centre (P-405 to P409) and P002 (GD-1,2 & 3)
provided with all necessary Furniture and Name Boards for the facilities have been
provided in the first week of March,2016 to initiate incubation activities by incubatees.
3) Routhu Rajendra, Founder & CEO of Bluebirds Gaming Solutions Pvt Ltd has delivered
an expert talk on “Startups and the future of online games” on 02-03-2016 at P.G Block
Seminar hall. In which 65 students have participated.
4) Sri. Jayesh Ranjan ,Secretary, Information Technology, Electronics & Communications
Department , Government of Telangana, Commissioner of Industries and Managing
Director of the Telangana State Industrial Infrastructure Corporation (TSIIC) has visited
VJ-Hub on 04-03-2016 along with Vignana Jyothi Management Committee members,
who acted as a Chief Guest of Annual Day celebrations of VNRVJIET.
5) Second Industry Institute Interaction / Entrepreneurship Development Committee
Meeting was held on 22-03-2016 in Board Room from 3 PM to 5 PM to review the
progress made by Vignana Jyothi Incubation Centre. In which 8 presentations were
given by the students of VNRVJIET for seeking incubation space and suitable financial
assistance from Vignana Jyothi Management and MSMSE, New Delhi.
6) Agreement between MSME approved Incubatees(4) and the Host Institute(VJTBI) along
with the likely expenditure statements sent to MSME, New Delhi for release of approved
grant.
7) Received cheque fo r Rs 5.19 Lakhs (Rupees Five Lakh Nineteen Thousand only) from
Vignana Jyothi Management towards MSME approved Incubatees share and deposited
the same in the Andhra Bank account of VNRVJIET with a separate Ledger Account in
the name of Vignana Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (VJTBI).
8) Jarsh Advanced Safety Systems(Temperature Controlled Helmet):
A fully functional prototype has been fabricated and field testing has been started. A
temperature drop of 6 degrees Celsius has been observed. The prototype has been
tested on 10 individuals. Optimization of cooling parameters is to be done. Additional
facility is requested as thermal experiments cannot be performed accurately in rooms
that contain any running computers or laptops.
9) Eight students of TEP/ISB of VNRVJIET (2014-2016) & (2015-2017) Batches have
selected and attended for Stanford TEP B-Plan Boot Camp at ISB, Hyderabad during
24-03-2016 to 26-03-2016.
.
ED Cell, IIPC & MSME VJTBI Coordinators
Dr.A.Subba Rao , Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju & Sri.K.Kodanda Ram
