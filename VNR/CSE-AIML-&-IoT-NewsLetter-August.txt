HoD MESSAGE
Dear Students, Faculty, and Readers,
It is with great pride and enthusiasm that I welcome you
to the August-2025 edition of our newsletter “Digital
Dynamos” the official newsletter of the Department of
CSE-Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning &
Internet of Things (AIML & IoT). In this rapidly evolving
technological era, AIML & IoT are shaping the future,
transforming industries, and redefining the way we
interact with the world. Our department remains
committed to fostering innovation, research, and
excellence in these domains.
Through this newsletter, we aim to highlight the
remarkable achievements of our students and faculty,
ongoing research initiatives, industrial collaborations, and
recent advancements in the field. I encourage all students
and faculty members to actively participate, contribute,
and explore the endless possibilities that AIML & IoT
offer.
Let us continue to learn, innovate, and lead with passion
and commitment. Looking forward to an exciting journey
ahead!
Dr. Sagar Yeruva
Head of Department
Department of CSE-AIML & IoT

Department Highlights
The Department of CSE–AIML & IoT actively contributed to the “Fistful of Rice” initiative on
August 6, 2025.
On August 20, 2025, Dr. Y. Sagar (Associate Professor & Head, CSE–AIML & IoT), along with IV-
Year Coordinators and Mentors, engaged with final-year students to discuss classwork,
placements, and internship activities at VNRVJIET.
On August 21, 2025, Dr. Y. Sagar (Associate Professor & Head, CSE–AIML & IoT), along with III-
Year Coordinators and Mentors, interacted with third-year students to discuss classwork,
placements, and internship activities at VNRVJIET.
Mr. K. Kishan Babu (Assistant Professor) submitted a project proposal titled “Q-VISTA:
Quantum-Vision Integrated Surveillance Threat Analysis Platform” under the Quantum
Technology Cell (QTC), Quantum Algorithms scheme, with a proposed duration of six years.
A faculty team comprising Mr. V. Kishore, Mr. Ch. Vijay Kumar, Ms. R. Jyotsna, Ms. Akhila
Tejaswini, and Mr. N. Anjaneyulu (all Assistant Professors) conducted the outreach program
“Nation First – We Rise, We Act” at Brahmanapalli, Narsapur, Medak District (the department’s
adopted village) on August 6, 2025.
The Department of CSE–AIML & IoT, in collaboration with the ISOC Academic Hub, organized a
two-day Professional Development Program on “Building Inclusive AI & LLM for Social Good”
on August 11–12, 2025. Esteemed speakers included Mr. Bharath Nidikonda (Sr. Project Manager,
HCL Technologies), Dr. Praveen Gorla (Principal Researcher, VISWAM.AI), Mr. Ranjith Raj Vasam
(Executive Committee Member, Swecha), Mr. Biswajit Satapathy (Sr. AI Engineer, Ozonetel
Systems), Mr. Bharath Gogenini (AI Architect, Value Labs), and Ms. Nirmal Surange (Research
Scientist, VISWAM.AI, IIIT). The event engaged 150 participants, equipping them with
knowledge of inclusive AI and Large Language Models (LLMs) for social good. Sessions focused
on ethical AI practices, responsible innovation, and real-world applications, inspiring
participants to design solutions that drive equitable social impact.
On August 14, 2025, the Department of CSE–AIML & IoT hosted an expert talk titled
“Intelligence Unleashed: A New Era of AI.” The session was delivered by Dr. Bhasker Rao,
Founder & CEO of AI Labs Inc., Epic Semiconductor Inc., and Fortech Energy Inc. (USA).
Attended by 130 participants, the talk highlighted AI’s transformative role in driving
innovation, efficiency, and smarter decision-making across industries. It inspired attendees to
explore AI’s potential to revolutionize daily life and business, encouraging them to embrace AI-
driven solutions for future growth and innovation.
An Anti-Ragging Awareness Program was organized on August 12–13, 2025, with the
participation of 70 students. The sessions were led by Dr. Y. Sagar (Associate Professor & HoD),
along with faculty members Mr. V. Kishore, Mr. N. Anjaneyulu, Mr. U. Veeresh, Ms. Jyotsnarani
Tripathy, Ms. Manjusha Nambiar, Ms. T. Divya Bharathi (JCP), and Ms. H. Sulekha (Computer
Operator).

Research updates and Publications
Ms. N. Haritha (Assistant Professor), along with Dr. V. Lakshmi Chetana from the Department of
Computer Science and Engineering, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham (Amaravati Campus, A.P.),
presented a paper titled “Towards Generalizable and Explainable Early Sepsis Prediction: A
Hybrid Approach Using Multi-Center EHR Data and Advanced Feature Engineering” at the 6th
International Conference on Advances in Computer Engineering and Communication Systems
(ICACECS-2025). The paper was accepted, presented, and indexed in Scopus, and the
conference was held from August 6–8, 2025
Ms. Preety Singh (Assistant Professor), along with Mr. Soppari Bhanu Murthy, Ms. Shaziya Islam,
and Mr. Anandakumar Haldorai, presented a paper titled “Generative Adversarial Network-
Based Modeling of Stress and Strain in Hierarchical Composite Materials” at the International
Conference on Modern Sustainable Systems (CMSS-2025). The paper was presented and
indexed in Scopus, and the conference was held from August 12–14, 2025.
Ms. K. B. Anusha (Assistant Professor), along with Ms. Padmaja Kadiri and Mr. T. Chandra Sekhar
Rao, presented a paper titled “Novel and Efficient Classification of Cardiovascular
Abnormalities by Machine Learning” at the 9th International Conference on Information
Security and Cryptology (ICISC-2025). The paper was presented and indexed in IEEE, and the
conference was held on August 12–13, 2025.
Ms. P. Swetha (Assistant Professor), in collaboration with Dr. Abdul Bari, Dr. A. Rohini, Mr.
Madugula Yadaiah, Dr. N. Padmaja, and Ms. P. Rajyalakshmi, presented a paper titled “A
Federated Multi-Modal Framework for Privacy-Preserving Prediction of Post-Surgical
Complications” at the 3rd International Conference on Sustainable Computing and Smart
Systems (ICSCSS-2025), organized by Hindusthan College of Engineering and Technology,
Coimbatore, India. The paper was presented and indexed in IEEE, and the conference was held
from August 20–22, 2025.
Ms. K. Anitha (Assistant Professor), along with Ms. Vishwa Priya V, Ms. B. Padmini Devi,
Ms. Athira, and Ms. M. Vasavi, presented a paper titled “Real-Time Traffic Management and
Safety Enhancements in Internet of Vehicles Using Deep Reinforcement Learning” at the
International Conference on Networks and Cryptology (NETCRYPT-2025). The paper was
presented and indexed in IEEE, and the conference was held from May 29–31, 2025.
Ms. K. Anitha (Assistant Professor), along with Ms. K. Shailaja, Ms. Nikhila Kathirisetty, Mr. E. Ravi
Kumar, Dr. N. Sunanda, Ms. Manmath Nath Das, and Ms. Jyotsnarani Tripathy, published a paper
titled “Smart Irrigation System with Remote Plant Health Analysis” in the Journal of
Environmental Sciences (Vol. 11, No. 6, ISSN: 2229-7359). The paper is indexed in Scopus and
was published on August 15, 2025.
Mr. Naga Durga Saile K (Assistant Professor), along with Ms. Gaje Gouri Chandana, Mr. Gunjipalli
Chandra Sekhar, Mr. P. V. S. R. Krushik Sarma, and Ms. Rachuri Indhu Sree, published a paper
titled “Super-Resolution of Geospatial Images Using Enhanced GANs” in the International
Journal of Environmental Sciences (Vol. 11, No. 11s). The paper is indexed in Scopus and was
published in 2025.
