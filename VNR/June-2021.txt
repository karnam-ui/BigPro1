A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of June,2021.
I) ED Cell Activities:
1) Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju has Registered and attended a Virtual Session on T-Tribe
Talks "Tap into your Entrepreneurial Intuition" between 04.00 PM to 05.00 PM on 4th
June, 2021 by Mr Kashyap Kompella ,CEO, RPA2AI Research as a part of T-Tribe
Talks organized by T-Hub, IIIT Campus, Gachibowli, Hyderabad, Telangana.

“Felicitation Ceremony for the Ciena Spaces Program” with the Jury Members,
Sri. Prashant Kumar, Sri.Ramana and Sri.Joshua, representatives of NASSCOM
Foundation and representatives of Ciena Spaces on 25-06-2021 from 4PM to 5 PM
through online mode / Zoom Meeting where the top 5 teams identified out of 10 teams
shortlisted based on pitches made to the Jury, and these top 5 teams will be then
awarded a sum of ₹5 Lakh each to grow their startups further.

Telangana and Moderated by Sivaprasada Reddy Rachamallu, Managing Director,
