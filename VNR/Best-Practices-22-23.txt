Best Practice-1
1. Title of the Practice
Faculty Professional Growth Plan
2. Goal
• Faculty will identify and plan achievable targets in their profession for the next 2
terms.
• Focus and work to achieve each target.
3. The Context
Faculty Growth Plan for VNR VJIET will emphasize career development, research
support, and continuous learning, aligning with institutional goals and the evolving
landscape of higher education. This approach will capture Professional Development and
Continuous Learning, Research and Innovation Support, career Progression and Leadership
Development, Teaching Excellence and Pedagogical Innovation, Global Exposure and
Knowledge Sharing, etc.
4. The Practice
The Faculty Professional Growth Plan is designed with an intension of creating self-
guidance and awareness for improving their professional growth. The professional
growth plan includes the following categories:
1. Teaching and learning
2. Research and Consultancy
3. Developmental/Institutional development activities
4. Any other
Faculty will plan achievable targets for their professional Growth Plan and work to achieve the
targets. The targets are discussed and worked individually or through RIGs to reach the
targets. Faculty goals and Department goals are aligned to achieve quality indices.
Also, faculty connects with Industry/Academia/Research bodies to improve Research
and Consultancy potential. The progress of the FGP will be monitored twice in an
academic year.
5. Evidence of Success
The practice has resulted in qualitative and quantitative reflections. Enhanced research
output and quality, improved teaching quality and student outcomes, professional growth
and career advancement, national and international collaborations are the evidence of
success for this practice.
Best Practice-2
1. Title of the Practice
Industry-Linked Curriculum and Skill Development
2. Goal
To bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, ensuring that
students acquire the skills, knowledge, and practical experience necessary for successful
careers.
3. The Context
The industry-Linked Curriculum and Skill Development initiative is a response to the
growing need for alignment between higher education and the rapidly evolving demands of
industry. As technology and global market dynamics reshape the job landscape, institutions
must ensure that their graduates possess the skills, knowledge, and adaptability necessary
to thrive in competitive and often unpredictable environments.
4. The Practice
At VNRVJIET, the Industry-Linked Curriculum and Skill Development practice is deeply
embedded in the institution’s academic and professional training frameworks, aiming to
create graduates who are ready to meet the demands of modern industries. This practice
involves multiple strategic initiatives, collaborative programs, and experiential learning
opportunities that align academic learning with real-world industry requirements.
VNRVJIET collaborates closely with industry experts and corporate partners to co-develop
curriculum and incorporate up-to-date industry standards, emerging technologies, and
relevant case studies. Industry experts are part of the Academic Council, Board of Studies,
Technical event evaluations and Project evaluation. This ensures that students gain skills
and knowledge that are current, relevant, and directly applicable to their careers.
In-House Skill Development Workshops, Bootcamps, Value added courses, and training are
offered through industry experts.
5. Evidence of Success
The industry-Linked Curriculum and Skill Development practice at VNRVJIET has led to
significant achievements, evidenced by various measurable outcomes and qualitative
improvements in student performance, employability, and industry engagement. Good
number of Placement with high packages, industry recognitions and partnerships, Positive
Student and Employer Feedback, and Successful Alumni Achievements in the Industry are
few visible evidence of the success.
