Date: 31/03/2018
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell, & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of March, 2018
I) ED Cell Activities:
1) Sri. K. Kodanda Ram has delivered a lecture on ED Cell ,VJ Hub activities, Ideation and
My Big Idea Competition (MBIC) to I Year B.Tech students of CSE – II on 07-03-2018
in which 37 students have participated. Mrs Kriti, Assistant Professor, CSE and
Sri.T.Gnana Prakash , Assistant Professor, CSE have coordinated the activity.
2) An expert talk was delivered by Sri.Shashanka Vishnuvajahala of Fanatics of USA,
Senior Director of Engineering on topic “Anatomy of an e-commerce business in the
Cloud” on 07-03-2018 to 3rd year B.Tech students of all branches and sections who have
opted Entrepreneurship as Open Elective.
3) Association of Lady Entrepreneurs of AP (ALEAP) in association with VNRVJIET has
celebrated an International Women’s Day in K.S. Auditorium on 10-03-2018 between
10.30 AM to 4 PM in which Lady Entrepreneurs from ALEAP and 400 students from
different colleges across Hyderabad have participated. On this occasion, ALEAP has
organized Idea Competition, in which VNRVJIET has bagged First Prize of Rs 10,000/-
and Second Prizes of Rs 5,000/- and incubation support from ALEAP to these winners of
the competition.Sri K.Kodanda Ram, Chief Coordinator, ED Cell and
Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Coordinator, ED Cell have coordinated the activity.
