A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for
the Month of January 2024.
I) ED Cell Activities:
1. Mr. Gopichand Anumolu, Adithya Pramod Kotha, and Nikhil Swamy G (B.Tech. ME Class
of 2023 and ATLAST Motor Corp.) team won 1st prize at Entrepreneur Organisation Global
student entrepreneur awards at Navi Mumbai section held on 21.01.2024.
II) MSME & VJTBI Activities:
1. The First Instalment (70%) of GoI grant share for MSME Hackathon 2.0 projects are set
by CTTC Bhubaneswar (CNA) for nurturing & development of ideas in the PFMS portal on
02.01.2024 to utilize the amount as per scheme guidelines & GFR rules. The sanctioned fund
now available to the Principal Investigators (PIs) to utilize towards the idea development.
2. MSME – Idea Hackathon 2022 related documents (1). Fund Utilisation Document (2). Form
GFR 12A (3). Bank Statement were sent to Central Tool Room and Training Centre (CTTC),
Bhubaneswar on 17.01.2024 to release the II instalment of the fund.
