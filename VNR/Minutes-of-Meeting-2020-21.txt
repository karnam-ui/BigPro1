Principal requested to construct an Institute level committee to analyze and prepare the
action plan for NIRF 2021
The committee is constituted for studying the NIRF ranking framework. The committee
may study the NIRF application and the individual parameters, scores achieved by our
institute for the last 3 years, scores of top 100 institutes in the engineering category.
The committee must prepare a outcome oriented strategic action plan to improve score in
respective parameters thus impro,ving the rank, constructive inputs in filing the NIRF
application, develop long term strategy to continuously achieve rank in NIRF.
Individual parameter committee may study and discuss upon the enclosed material and
links. Committee may also refer and explore other related content. The action plan and
strategies may be submitted to the principal on or before 30- 10-2020. Team members
may contact PAAC in case of queries or for further information.

IQAC before 30- I 0-2020.
