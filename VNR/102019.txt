II) MSME VJTBI:

(MSME), New Delhi.
Dr.A.S.Rao, Director, IIE&M, Vignana Jyothi and Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Manager,

MSME-DI, Balanagar, Hyderabad and submitted a copy of all necessary documents
