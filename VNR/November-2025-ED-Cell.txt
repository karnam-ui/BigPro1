A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for
the Month of November 2025
I. ED Cell Activities:
1. M. Venkata Ramana, i/c ED Cell attended the Entrepreneurial Talk Series on
13.11.2025 from 11.30 AM to 12.30 Noon. Dr Amaresh Panda, Founder of RNA
Biotech & Scientist-E, BRIC-ILS shared his journey from academia to
entrepreneurship and covered problems with RNA isolation and also its kits.
2. Entrepreneurship Development Cell (ED Cell) in Association with Institute Industry
Relations (IIR) of VNRVJIET is organized an Expert Talk on Entrepreneurship as
a Career on 29.11.2025 from 01.30 PM to 03.00 PM at APJ Abdul Kalam Hall. Mr.
Jatil Sharma, Director, Tejaswi Green Energy Pvt. Ltd., Hyderabad delivered the talk
on the importance of Hackathons & Buildathons and discussed the startup cycle,
Entrepreneur’s Mindset & The Engineer’s Edge. 64 students from I year and 2 faculty
members attended the talk.
Mr. Jatil Sharma, Director, Tejaswi Green Energy Pvt. Ltd delivering the talk on
29.11.2025
