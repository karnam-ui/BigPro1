ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT
The Department of CSE- Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML) stands at the forefront of
innovation, shaping a future that transcends boundaries and revolutionizes the way to interact with
technology. The Department of CSE -AIML started in the year 2020. B. Tech-CSE (AIML) started with
an intake of 60 students and eventually increased to 180 in 2021. Currently we are having an
overall intake of 180 in AIML. The department also offers Minor program in AIML for the circuit and
non-circuit branches.

WHY B.TECH CSE (AIML)?
AIML technologies are driving transformative changes across diverse sectors, ranging from healthcare and
finance to autonomous systems and Cybersecurity.
The interdisciplinary nature of CSE- AIML provides students with a comprehensive skill set, encompassing not
only traditional computer science principles but also the intricacies of advanced machine learning
techniques.
Students have the opportunity to contribute to solutions that positively impact society, making a meaningful
difference through the application of cutting-edge technologies.
