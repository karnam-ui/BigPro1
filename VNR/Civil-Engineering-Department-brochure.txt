DEPARTMENT OF
CIVIL ENGINEERING
The Department of Civil Engineering was started in the year 2001. The department has well-
qualified, experienced, and dedicated faculty ably supported by highly skilled and competent
technical staff. The faculty are experts in various specializations of Civil Engineering, i.e,
Structural Engineering, Geo-technical Engineering, Transportation Engineering, Water and
Environmental engineering, Geo-spatial Technologies. The department has infrastructural
facilities required for imparting high-quality education to meet the present-day needs of the
industry. The department also has strong industry-institute interaction. Faculty members are
actively involved in research and consultancy activities.
