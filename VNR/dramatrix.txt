Dramatrix - The Drama Club of VNRVJIET
Dramatrix is a cultural club that comprises of actors, scriptwriters and creatives who have a keen interest in various forms of dramatics and more are eager enough to express themselves. It performs street plays, stage plays, mimes, etc on various events. The club believes that drama is not just about acting, it's much more than that. It's a way to express oneself, a form of expression.
