Department of Biotechnology, was established in 2025-26 academic year starting with B. Tech
                    Biotechnology program in VNR VJIET an Autonomous Institute under Jawaharlal Nehru Technological
                    University Hyderabad. Faculty members are qualified, extensive teaching and Research Experience
                    covering all domains of Biotechnology are a part of the team. The department goals are to establish
                    quality laboratory infrastructure for teaching and research, hand on experience individual projects
                    and good library, etc. Department offer students the ability to learn, advance and succeed in
                    exciting fields of Biotechnology and related domains with a variety of learning opportunities
                    designed to meet the needs of students.
Biotechnology is the transformative science of leveraging living systems - cells, organisms,
                            and biological processes to pioneer innovations that enhance human health, nourish our
                            planet, and power sustainable progress. Biotechnology continues to expand rapidly with new
                            discoveries at a breath taking pace. Our Program in B. Tech Biotechnology equips students
                            with the scientific and technological expertise to address pressing global challenges and
                            develop technological solutions to problems in health care, nutrition, environment,
                            agriculture, and energy. Our curriculum offers multidisciplinary approach where students
                            will learn engineering subjects such Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, Computer Science
                            and their application in addressing biological issues which are the emerging areas of
                            Biomechanics, Biosensors, Bioelectronics, Computer aided biology etc., to become
                            bioengineers apart from Life Sciences subjects. Other cutting edge areas students will be
                            exposed and specialize are in Medical Biotechnology & Bioinformatics, Plant
                            Biotechnology, Animal & Environmental Biotechnology, Bioprocess Engineering and
                            Nano-biotechnology and other emerging areas. Our pedagogy encourages imbibing practical
                            skills that can be honed through project-based learning and by studying real-world case
                            scenarios. Department nurture tomorrow's bioengineers and technologists who can design,
                            test, and manufacture biological systems and bio-based products for diverse applications.
                            This program, a merger of science, engineering and business, demands a multi-disciplinary
                            workforce skilled in basic-research, product development, regulatory affairs and
                            commercialization.
Nurturing Leadership:Cultivating critical thinkers, entrepreneurs, and
                            global problem-solvers who can
                            lead India’s as well global biotech revolution.
Laboratory Infrastructure:Department has established Cell Biology &
                            Microbiology Lab, Biochemistry
                            Lab, Molecular Biology & Genetic Engineering Lab, Bioinformatics, Fluid Mechanics and
                            Heat Transfer
                            Labs. The labs which will be established shortly, are Bioprocess Engineering Lab, Plant
                            Biotechnology lab,
                            Immunology Lab, Downstream Processing Lab, and Animal Biotechnology Lab etc. The established
                            labs
                            have good infrastructure (equipment’s) where students and faculty could conduct real time
                            projects and
                            carry R&D activities apart from curriculum bound hand on experience with experiments.
VNR VJIET has signed an MoU with Center for Cellular and Molecular Biology (CCMB),
                                    Habsiguda, Hyderabad for student related projects and R & D activities.
Apart from teaching, at VNR VJIET we counsel and guide the students based on their
                                    potential which
                                    domain of biotechnology they can succeed as a part of career counselling.
Biotechnology is one of the highly skilled field and Biotechnologists and
                                    Bioengineers could earn in par
                                    with any engineering professionals besides serving the nation.
To emerge as a premier Centre in Biotechnology for education and research, fostering
                                    innovation that
                                    bridges science and society. To nurture future-ready professionals who leverage
                                    cutting-edge biological
                                    issues integrated with engineering concepts to solve global challenges in health,
                                    agriculture,
                                    environment, energy, and industry driven, with ethical values for sustainable
                                    progress for India and
                                    globally.
1. Imparting knowledge of the fundamentals of Engineering, Science and Technology in
                                    students by
                                    providing good academic environment to pursue undergraduate, Post graduate and
                                    Doctoral programs
                                    in Biotechnology for a successful professional career
2. Developing liaison with Academia, R & D institutions and Biotechnology
                                    Industry for exposure in
                                    practical aspects in engineering and solution of the industry oriented and societal
                                    problems,
                                    entrepreneurial pursuit and project management
3. Inculcating interpersonal skills, team work, professional ethics, IPR and
                                    regulatory issues in students
                                    to improve their employability in changing global environment
4. Promoting strong research culture in students for life – long learning
