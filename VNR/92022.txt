A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for
the Month of September 2022.
I) ED Cell Activities:
1) ED Cell, VNR VJIET, Hyderabad has arranged a session on Young Leaders
Programme (YLP) offered by Indian School of Business (ISB) on 22nd
September 2022. Mr. Arun Prakash, Higher Education Marketing
Professional, Indian School of Business informed to the students of 3rd and
4th B.Tech of all branches of VNRVJIET about guidelines and procedure for
Admission into Post Graduate Programs in Management. 125 students were
attended and interacted with the resource person during this session.
Mr. Arun Prakash’s Presentation and Interaction with students at the venue
2) NBA Chairman visited ED Cell, VNRVJIET on 16/09/2022 as a part of
accreditation visit by NBA expert team to EEE, ME, ECE, CSE, EIE and IT
Departments.
II) MSME & VJTBI Activities:
1) Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Manage r, MSME Business Incubator and

“Vallurupalli Nageswara Rao Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering
