Report on Students Satisfaction Survey (SSS) for the A.Y. 2019-20
VNRVJIET is the institute which got embedded with the inherent closed loop system
by which it corrects itself through the feedbacks/surveys on continuous basis. Students
Satisfaction Survey (SSS) is one among such surveys. This survey includes majorly the
questionnaire recommended by NAAC. The organisation of survey, guidelines, questionnaire
and the results is presented as follows.

More than 80% of students expressed their satisfaction about teachers’ approach towards
teaching. Regarding the explanation of the content, more than 93% students stated that the
teachers mention the real time examples while teaching. Concerning to the fairness in
evaluation, 96% students expressed good satisfaction levels. More than 78% students
mentioned that the faculty are helpful in making SWOC analysis. Coming to the
implementation of Mezzanine Technologies (Fourth Generation Technologies) in the projects,
more than 88% students expressed happiness.
According to the opinion of 94% students, teachers communicate well in the classroom.
More than 88% students are happy with the course design and the material supplied during
online classes. The teaching and learning process in the institute with the implementation of
modern and distinctive pedagogical methods, could gain the satisfaction levels up to
EXCELLENT and Good from more than 71% students. Another 27% of students expressed
the appreciation with a remark of fair. Regarding the efforts made by the teachers and institute
to inculcate soft skills, life skills and employability skills, 94% students expressed their utmost
satisfaction.
In overall, more than 87% students are satisfied with the effectiveness of On-line classes.
