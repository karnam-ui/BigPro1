RULES AND REGULATIONS
1. The hostel resident should furnish the correct address, telephone, mobile number of the
parent/guardian. Any change of permanent address should be informed to the hostel
administration immediately.
2. Hostel Boarders should carry their identity cards, which must be produced whenever
required by the hostel administration and security.
3. All hostel Boarders should maintain their hostel rooms and hostel premises clean and
cooperate with the hostel staff.
4. Boarders are not permitted to make any loud noise and create disturbance.
5. All the Boarders should switch off lights and fans while leaving the room.
6. Posters should not be pasted on the walls/doors etc. and no captions shall be written on
the same.
7. No Boarder is allowed to stay in the rooms during college timings except on genuine
grounds with the permission of Warden.
8. Unauthorized exchange of rooms is not allowed.
9. Renewal of the hostel accommodation is based on the Boarder's performance in the
semester exam.
10. All the Boarders should pay the hostel fees before the commencement of the academic
year. The Management reserves the right for deciding the hostel fee structure.
11. Ragging is a criminal offence and is strictly prohibited.
12. Boarders will not possess, use/deal with any kind of intoxicating material including
alcohol, drugs of any kind, gutka, tobacco, cigarettes, chewing gums, or any other
sedative material.
13. Outside food is not allowed.
14. No Boarder should intentionally waste food. Boarders should adhere to mess timings.
15. Boarders are not permitted to entertain any guests in their rooms without prior permission
of the warden.
16. Parents and local guardians are allowed only on Sundays and public holidays between
4.00 PM to 6.00 PM.
17. Hostel authorities are not responsible for any loss of Boarder's personal items such as cell
phones, laptops or any other gadgets.
18. Boarders who wish to leave the station shall apply for leave and obtain permission in
writing two days in advance. They shall also intimate the address of their stay during this
period.
19. Boarders should not cause any damage to the hostel property. If any common property is
lost/damaged/disfigured, the loss will be recovered from the students, in equal share, who
are responsible for the damage or loss or in general use of that property. In case the
damage/loss cannot be pinpointed to the student(s), then a collective fine to recover the
loss will be levied by the Hostel Warden.
20. The Management has the full authority for the inspection of the room, bags, almirahs, or
any of personal belongings at any time.
21. Celebrating parties on any occasion including birthdays is strictly prohibited inside the
hostel and VJ premises.
22. Boarders are not allowed to play any outdoor games inside the hostel.
23. All the Boarders should hand over the room in person at the end of the academic year to
the warden/Supervisor.
24. Any objectionable incident comes to the notice of Management; the Boarder may be
suitably penalized or expelled from the hostel.
25. The decision of the Principal/Warden is final in all matters related to the hostel
administration.
26. Two and Four wheelers are not permitted into the Hostel.
27. The Management reserves the right to frame, amend, revoke or repeal the provisions
including hostel timings.
28. The institution shall not be responsible for any kind of accidents/events within/outside the
campus.
29. All types of charges and fees as charged by the institution are subject to revision from
time to time.
