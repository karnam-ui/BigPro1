ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT
The B.Tech in Robotics and Artificial Intelligence is a state-of-the-art undergraduate program aimed at providing students with a
comprehensive understanding and hands-on experience in the domains of robotics, intelligent systems, and artificial intelligence.
This program integrates fundamental engineering concepts with sophisticated computing and data-centric technologies, equipping
students to develop autonomous systems capable of perception, learning, and intelligent action.
ABOUT THE PROGRAMME
The fields of engineering and robotics, together with artificial intelligence, are experiencing rapid growth, equipping students to
fulfill the requirements of research, development, and industrial manufacturing. The demand for proficient graduates is
unprecedented, due to the rising adoption of robotics and AI for executing data-heavy and repetitive tasks. Our program aims to
nurture specialists in Robotics and Artificial Intelligence, created in partnership with industry leaders to guarantee alignment with
the latest trends and state-of-the-art technologies.
KEY FEATURES
Interdisciplinary Curriculum: Combines principles from multiple engineering fields such as Mechanical, Electronics, Computer
Science, and Electrical to foster a comprehensive understanding of intelligent systems.
CAREER
Robotics and AI offer thriving and future-proof career opportunities—especially as automation, smart systems, and intelligent
platforms redefine industries worldwide. In India, demand for AI professionals is projected to exceed 1 million by 2026, with over
2.3 million job openings expected by 2027—highlighting significant growth in sectors like healthcare, defense, logistics, and
manufacturing. Leading companies such as Databricks are investing aggressively—pledging more than $250 million into India’s
AI ecosystem, including building R&D centers and training thousands of engineers in Bengaluru. Meanwhile, HCLTech is
dedicating 15–20% of fresher intake to AI and robotics roles, offering substantially higher compensation for individuals with niche
skills. Graduates from VNRVJIET’s program are poised for roles like Robotics Engineer, AI/ML Specialist, and Ethical AI Architect—
in both national hubs and international markets—equipping them to become innovators and leaders in the digital transformation
era.

OPPORTUNITIES
Graduates can pursue professions such as Robotics Engineer, AI Developer, Data Scientist, Automation Engineer, Research
Scientist, or continue with higher studies in Robotics, AI, or linked subjects.
Research Scientist (AI/Robotics)
What you do: Work on cutting-edge problems in robotics or AI in academia or R&D.
Typical employers: ISRO, DRDO, CSIR, research labs, or top global tech firms.
Product Developer / System Integrator
What you do: Integrate mechanical, electrical, and software components into a final product (like a drone or robot).
Embedded Systems Engineer
What you do: Program and integrate hardware-software systems using microcontrollers like Arduino, Raspberry Pi, etc.
Used in: Robotics, IoT, consumer electronics.
Automation Engineer
What you do: Develop systems to automate tasks in industries like manufacturing, food processing, and logistics.
Tools: PLCs, SCADA, ROS, Python, etc.
Computer Vision Engineer
What you do: Work on image/video analysis, facial recognition, autonomous navigation.
Use cases: Drones, self-driving cars, surveillance systems.
Data Scientist / AI Analyst
What you do: Analyze large data sets and create predictive models using AI.
Tools: Python, R, TensorFlow, PyTorch.
