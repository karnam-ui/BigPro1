II) MSME VJTBI:
As per the oral instructions of Sri K.C.Choudhary, Assistant Director / Nodal Officer (BI)
MSME-DI, Balanagar, Hyderabad, Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Manager, MSME Business
incubator, VNRVJIET VJTBI has visited the office of MSME-DI, Balanagar, Hyderabad on
05-09-2019 and attended an “Awareness Programme on Support for Entrepreneurial and
Managerial Development of MSMEs through Incubator”. This awareness programme was
inaugurated by Sri.D.Chandra Sekhar, Additional Industrial Adviser,Sri B.Vijay Kumar,
Deputy Director and Sri K.C Choudhury, Assistant Director & Nodel officer
(BI Scheme),MSME-DI Balanagar. A detailed report pertaining to the Registration of
Vallurupalli Nageswara Rao Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering and Technology
(VNRVJIET) as a Host Institution (HI) for setting up of a Business Incubator (BI) was
prepared along with all necessary documents by Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju and submitted
the same in person to the Office of the Implementing Agency (Director, MSME-DI,
Balanagar) on 05-09-2019 for further action.
