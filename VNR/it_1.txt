The department of Information Technology was established in the year
          1997 with an annual intake of 60, increased to 180 in the year 2017.
          M.Tech(CNIS) Programme started in the year 2013 with an intake of 18.
          It has all infrastructural facilities required for imparting high
          quality education and the department is fully structured to meet the
          contemporary needs of the industry. Imparting high quality education
          is supported by well qualified and experienced faculty, further
          support is extended by technically skilled and competent programmers.
