Dr. B Chennakesava Rao
Director Advancement & Dean Admin
VNR VIGNANA JYOTHI INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING & TECHNOLOGY
Email ID: directoradvancement@vnrvjiet.in
Contact Info: 9866141821
Dr. B Chennakesava Rao graduated in B.E. from Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology in
Mechanical Engineering and Post Graduate M.E in Production Engineering from Osmania
University, Hyderabad.
He was awarded Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from Osmania University, Hyderabad. Dr.
Chennakesava Rao served as Faculty of MECH Dept., and as Principal for 11 years at CBIT,
Hyderabad. Under his leadership the institute got Sanction of Rs.4.00 crores project by MHRD
supported with the assistance of World Bank under Technical Education Quality Improvement
Programme – Phase –II Sub Component 1.1and CBIT has been recognized as well performed
Institute.
He is instrumental in setting an Incubation Centre - a Hyderabad-based technology start-up,
in the discipline of modern product development. This entails various activities such as
technological innovation, acquiring IP, defining products, software architecture, design and
coding, testing.
He is currently working as Director Advancement & Dean Admin in VNR Vignana Jyothi
Institute of Engineering & Technology and taking care of all the Research and Administrative
activities.
Dr. Chennakesava Rao has 38 years of Teaching/R & D experience, and has 20 technical
publications He has guided 10 PG & 60 UG Projects. He successfully organized more than 50
Workshops/Seminars/Conferences. He acted as expert committee member in various NAAC
accreditation visits.
He is a Life Member of ISTE (India), and Life Member of ISME.
