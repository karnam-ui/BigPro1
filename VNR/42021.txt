A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of April,2021.
I) ED Cell Activities:
1) Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Sri K.Kodanda Ram, Dr. M.Venkata Ramana and
Mr. H Vijaya Kumar have prepared and submitted a Questionnaire as desired by
NASSCOM Foundation / DXC Innovation Spaces in the requisite format for establishing
an incubator with a seed fund of 5 Lakhs each for short listed innovative ideas submitted
by the students of VNRVJIET sponsored by DXC Innovation Spaces supported by
NASSCOM Foundation on 24-04-2021.
2) Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju,Sri K.Kodanda Ram, Dr.C.Kiran, Dr. M.Venkata Ramana and
Mr. H Vijaya Kumar have attended “An Introduction and briefing about the DXC
Spaces innovation program” by NASSCOM Foundation / DXC Spaces briefing with
Universities on 26-04-2021 from 2PM to 3 PM through online mode.
3) VNRJIET ED Cell has organized a Webinar on the eve of World Intellectual Property
Day (World IP Day) with a theme of “Intellectual Property Rights & SMEs: Taking
your Ideas to Market” by Pocha Chakradhar Reddy, Associate, K&S Partners,
Intellectual Property Attorneys Hyderabad on 26-04-2019 from 03.30 PM to 05.00 PM
through Online / Virtual mode, in which 263 Faculty and Students of all branches of
VNRVJIET have Participated.
4) Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Sri K.Kodanda Ram and Dr. M.Venkata Ramana have
Registered and attended an online Session on “Operations Management” by Mr. K.G
Philipose, Director, Kirby India for the students and faculty of Mechanical, Automobile &
Civil Engineering branches on 28-04-2021 from 3 PM to 4 PM jointly organized by
VNRVJIET ED Cell and Confederation of Indian Industry(CII),Telangana, Hyderabad ,in
which 42 Faculty and Students have participated.
5) Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Sri K.Kodanda Ram,Dr.C.Kiran, Mr.Aziz Athani and
Dr. M.Venkata Ramana have Registered and attended a Webinar on “Creativity,
Innovation & Entrepreneurship” by Dr.Baishali Mitra, Dr.Pankaj Barthi and Dr.Lalith
Sharma for 3rd Year B.Tech Students of VNRVJIET, who have opted
“Entrepreneurship” subject as Open Elective on 29-04-2021 from 11 AM to 1 PM. This
Webinar was Jointly Organized by VNRVJIET ED Cell and Entrepreneurship
Development Institute of India (EDII), Ahmedabad in which 138 students have actively
participated in the Webinar.
(ED Cell & MSME VJTBI Coordinators)
