Date: 30/11/2017
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of November, 2017
1) Sri V.S.Praveen of YCI Technologies, T hrissur, Kerala interacted with Principal and
HOD’s in board room between 11AM to 12 Noon. He delivered a guest lecture for
students of I B.Tech on topic “You can Invent” in Dr.A.P.J Abdul Kalam Auditorium on
03-11-2017 in between 2.30 PM to 4 PM , in which 25 students have participated.
2) Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Manager, MSME Business Incubator and Sri.K.Kodanda
Ram, Chief Coordinator, ED Cell have given a talk on “The role of entrepreneur in the
economic development of the nation” to I Year B.Tech CSE-3 students of VNRVJIET on
10-11-2017 , in which 42 students have attended. Smt.Kriti Ohri , Assistant Professor,
CSE organized the talk.
