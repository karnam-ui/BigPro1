A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of February, 2020
I) ED Cell Activities:
i) An Expert Talk by Sri.Ganesh Rayala, Senior Manager Marketing Communications at
T-Hub was arranged by ED Cell on 10-02-2020 at K.S.Auditorium between 11 AM to 1 PM
for 3rd Year B.Tech II Semester students who have opted Entrepreneurship subject as
open elective. The talk was attended by 158 students. Dr.C.Kiran ,Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa
Raju and Sri.K.Kodanda Ram have arranged the Talk.
An interaction with students by Sri.Ganesh Rayala on 10-02-2020 at K.S.Auditorium
ii) Sri Charan Lakka Raju, CEO, stuMagz d eliverd a guest lecture on Business Model
Canvas & Business Modelling at K.S.Auditorium on 12-02-2020 between 01.40 PM to
03.40 PM for 3rd Year B.Tech II Semester students who have opted Entrepreneurship
subject as open elective. The guest lecture was attended by 126 students.
Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Sri.K.Kodanda Ram, Dr.C.Kiran and Sri S.R.Somayajulu have
attended the Lecture.
A guest Lecture by Sri Charan Lakka Raju, CEO, stuMagz on 12-02-2020
iii) An Expert Talk on “Digital Marketing” by Sri Shreyas Devershetty, Manager, Digital
Marketing of “Thick Shake Factory” was arranged by ED Cell on 17-02-2020 at B-Block
Seminar hall between 11.00 AM to 01.00PM for 135 Students of 3rd Year B.Tech II
Semester, who have opted Entrepreneurship subject as open elective.
An Expert Talk on “Digital Marketing” by Sri Shreyas Devershetty
iv) An Entrepreneurship Fest Called “ECFICIO 3.0” was organized by ED Cell & VJ Hub
during 26th & 27th of February, 2020 at VNRVJIET.As a part of which several
Entrepreneurship related events like, Panel Discussions, Entrepreneur Talks,
Workshops, My Big Idea Competition (MBIC), Patent Idea, Startup Idea, Play & Learn
Competitions were organized and the winners of these competitions were distributed with
cash prizes and Certificates. “ECFICIO 3.0” was initiated by Dr C.Kiran and supported by
Sri.K.Kodanda Ram and Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju.
Inaugural Function of ‘ECFICIO-3.0” at K.S.Auditorium on 26-02-2020
II) MSME VJTBI:
i) MSME Webinar on “Ideas for New India Challenge 2020” was arranged by NMIU
Secretariat, O/o DC MSME, Ministry of MSME for the coordinators of approved Business
Incubators. Ms Mandeep Kaur, IAS, Joint Development Commissioner acted as a speaker
of the Webinar and interacted with the coordinators of Host Institutions(HI) / Business
Incubators(BI) across the country on 13-02-2020 between 12 Noon to 1.30 PM.
Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Manager, MSME Business incubator has attended this
Webinar through online at English Communications Skills Lab, VNRVJIET and obtained
valuable inputs pertaining to the guidelines for submitting the Ideas to “Ideas for New India
Challenge 2020”.
ii) Uploading of “Ideas for New India Challenge 2020” by the students, others, startups and
entrepreneurs were completed between 16-02-2020 to 20-02-2020 through My
Government Portal under the guidance of Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Manager,
VNRVJIET MSME Business incubator. A total of 12 Ideas were uploaded, out of which 5
Ideas from students and 7 Ideas from others, startups and entrepreneurs.
iii) As per the guidelines of MSME 5 Ideas from students and 5 Ideas from others, startups
and entrepreneurs (Total 10 Ideas) were shortlisted by duly constituting an internal panel
experts Committee consisting of Principal, Directors and Manager, VNRVJIET MSME
Business Incubator and forwarded the same for final screening to the MSME-DC, New
Delhi through My Government online Portal on 22-02-2020.
III) TEP / ISB
i) At the end of 3rd Phase of online and practicum component, 14 students of (2019 -2020)
Batch VNRVJIET TEP / ISB have got short listed and obtained eligibility for getting Course
Completion Certificates.
