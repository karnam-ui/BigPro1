A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of December, 2019
I) ED Cell Activities:
i) A visit was arranged to the students of VNRVJIET by ED Cell to IIA International
Innovation Fair (IIIF-2019) sponsored by Indian Innovators Association at NSIC,
Kushaiguda, Hyderabad during 01-12-2019 to 03-12-2019.In this connection an exhibition
of innovative products and technologies were showcased from various countries across
the World. The exhibition was attended by 186 students of all branches along with faculty.
Dr.A.S.Rao, President, IIA and Sri.V.Ramnteja played a key role in arranging IIIF-2019 at
Hyderabad. Dr.Y.Padma Sai, Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Sri.K.Kodanda Ram and
Dr.C.Kiran have motivated the students to attend the International Innovation Fair-2019.
IIA International Innovation Fair (IIIF-2019) at NSIC, Kushaiguda, Hyderabad
during 1-12-019 to 03-12-2019
VNRVJIET students and faculty visit to IIIF-2019, NSIC, Hyderabad.
ii) TiE Premium League was organized by Sri Charan Lakk Raju, CEO of stuMagz for the
students of VNRVJIET in K.S. Auditorium between 10 AM to 11.30 AM on 11-12-2019.
“Graduate Premier League” (GPL) was conducted from Dec. 11 to Dec. 28. 247 students
of IV year B.Tech. (CSE, ECE, IT, EIT, and EEE) have participated in the PerspectAI
app-based tests conducted through stuMagz for TiE.
A lecture by Sri Charan Lakk Raju,CEO of Stumagz “Graduate Premier League” on
11-12-2019 at K.S.Auditorium, VNRVJIET
iii) Prof. Greg Collier & Mr. Krish Nangegadda from Northeastern University (NU), Centre for
Entrepreneurial Education, USA have visited the campus on 18-12-2019 along with their
coordinators in India. The basic idea of this visit would be to understand and guide the
campuses & the students on entrepreneurship and the start-up ecosystem. They
coordinate the NU-IDEA Program which creates an ecosystem of mentors, coaches, and
an environment for students to start building new ventures and acts as a support system
for the needy students in the process of incubation. The program offers online courses,
local design and implementation support, as well as ongoing assistance and ecosystem
assessment to track improvement. In this connection representatives from Northeastern
University, Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Coordinator ED Cell, Dr G.Ramesh Chandra, HOD,
RCC, Sri S.V.Narayana Rao, Manager,VJ-Hub,Dr C.Kiran, Global Relations Officer
Coordinator, VNRVJIET gave presentations in the Board Room between 11.30 AM to 2.15
PM. Dr.C.D.Naidu,Principal, Dr.B.Chennakesava Rao, Director, Dr.A.S.Rao, Director,
IIE&M, HoD’s of all departments,Dr.S.Pranavananda, Convener, IIC have attended the
meeting. The ED Cell and the Global Relationships Office have together coordinated this
meeting with Northeastern University’s team of delegates for a discussion on the NU-IDEA
accelerator programme.
Interaction with Northeastern University IDEA program delegates by the
representatives of VNRVJIET at Board Room on 18-12-2019.
iv) An expert talk on “The need of Entrepreneurship” by Prof. Greg Collier & Mr. Krish
Nangegadda of Northeastern University (NU), USA to III Year B.Tech II Semester
students of all branches who have opted Entrepreneurship as Open Elective subject on
18-12-2019 at K.S Auditorium between 2.30 PM to 4.30 PM. Two twenty students have
attended the expert talk.
