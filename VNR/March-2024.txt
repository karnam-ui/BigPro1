A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME
Vignana Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI)
Activities for the Month of March 2024.
I) ED Cell Activities:
1. Six students from different programmes attended the E - Summit organized by IIT Madras
from 07.03.2024 to 10.03.2024. Mr. M. Krishna Prasad, Head - Innovation Incubation &
Entrepreneurship, VNRVJIET also attended the event. The E-Summit at IIT Madras is an annual
event that brings together students, entrepreneurs and industry leaders to explore and celebrate
innovation, entrepreneurship, and technology. Students attended various sessions, workshops
and competitions. They got various networking opportunities. The following students attended the
event.

2. Meeting is conducted with ED cell student members on 19.03.2024. 10 students attended the
meeting. Mr. M. Krishna Prasad, Head - Innovation Incubation & Entrepreneurship and Dr. M.
Venkata Ramana, Coordinator - ED Cell, VNRVJIET organized the meeting. Start -up culture,
events to be conducted in the VNRVJIET are discussed in the meeting.
3. Meeting is conducted with ED cell Department coordinators on 21.03.2024. It was decided to
have interaction with I and II Year students on entrepreneurship and startup culture in the Institute.
In this connection, all ED cell department coordinators are informed to arrange interaction with I
and II year students Department wise on a mutually convenient day.
4. World water day celebrations jointly organized by CII Hyderabad Chapter and Dr. Reddy's
Laboratories held on 22.03.2024 at Dr. Reddy's Laboratories, Bachupally, Hyderabad. 30
students from the Civil Engineering Department and Dr. K. Ravi Kumar, Associate Professor,
CED attended the event.
