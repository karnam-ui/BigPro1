A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell, & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of March, 2019
I) ED Cell Activities:

presented their Innovative Ideas under open theme category. Dr.A.S.Rao ,

and declaring the 3 ideas. Mr.P.Dinesh Kumar and Ch. Akarsh of I-Year B.Tech,
CSE-1 have organized the Competition. The Prize winners of the Competition are
honored with Cash Prize and Merit Certificates.

II) TEP / ISB:
1) A WebEx session (Speaker Series-1) was conducted for TEP / ISB (2019-2020) Batch
on 01/03/2019 during 3 PM to 4 PM in P.G. Block seminar hall in which 7 TEP / ISB
students have participated. Sri K.Kodanda Ram, Chief Coordinator of ED Cell and
Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju Coordinator of ED Cell coordinated the activity. The topic of
the session was “How to Start Startup”. Mr.P.Dinesh Kumar and Ch. Akarsh of I-Year
B.Tech, CSE-1 have actively participated in organizing the programme.
2) A WebEx session (Speaker Series-2) was conducted for TEP / ISB (2019-2020) Batch
on 28/03/2019 during 2 PM to 3 PM in P.G. Block seminar hall in which 2 TEP / ISB
students have participated. Sri K.Kodanda Ram, Chief Coordinator of ED Cell and
Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju Coordinator of ED Cell coordinated the activity. The topic of
the session was "Finding the right co-founder for a technology entrepreneur".
Mr.P.Nikhil Kumar, Student Ambassador of 2019-2020 Bach actively participated in the
programme.
