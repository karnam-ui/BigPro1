a). Learning from NPTEL video materials.
b). Learning from on-line courses from NPTEL.

the concepts in the minds of students.

topics.
e). Using Black Board, Projector for showing any relevant topic video, PPTs, etc for teaching.
f). Demonstrating Turbines and Pumps with models.
g). Conducting POGIL activity on concept based topics in Disaster Management, FM and H&HM.
h). Class room discussions to help the students to deepen the understanding of the topics.
i. Carrying students to nearby industry, place of interest based on the topic covered in the class.
j). Tutorial classes to make the students to practice more model kind of problems to become ready
to solve any civil engineering problems.
k). Personal meetings with students to get feedback on the topic covered.
l). Maintaining good document of WIT and WIL for the topics covered which is cross check of
teaching and learning point of view.
m). Doing NPTEL Online Courses to update my knowledge in various fields.
n). Conducting On-line classes using Graphic Tablet, other interacting modes.
