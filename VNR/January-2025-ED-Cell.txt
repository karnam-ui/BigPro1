A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for
the Month of January 2025
I) ED Cell Activities:
1. ED Cell took part in the Republic Day parade held at VNRVJIET on 26.01.2025 from
09.00 AM to 12.00 Noon.
2. A seminar on “Entrepreneurship Awareness Program” was organized by ED Cell and
Institute Industry Relations in association with MSME Technology Centre, Hyderabad,
Ministry of MSME-Government of India, Central Institute of Tool Design, Hyderabad
on 30.01.2025. 73 B.Tech. students from all the programmes and 02 M.Tech. students
attended the event. Mr. Sanath Kumar, Dy. Director, MSME and Dr. Alapati Sri
Nagesh, Consultant Psychologist and International Corporate Trainer created
awareness and importance of Entrepreneurship to the students. The various funding
schemes to the students for start-ups are also covered from 10.00 AM to 01.00 PM.
II) MSME & VJTBI Activities:
1. Development of the Two Ideas under MSME Hackathon 2.0 are under progress. (Dr.
K. Ajay Kumar/ MED and Dr. D. Ramesh Reddy/ECE).
2. Development of the One Idea under MSME Hackathon 3.0 is under progress. (Ms. K.
Bhagya Rekha, Assistant Professor, CSE, VNRVJIET).
Dr. M. Venkata Ramana
Professor of Automobile Engineering &
Coordinator, ED Cell.
