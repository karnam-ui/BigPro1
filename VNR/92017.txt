Date: 30/09/2017
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell, IIPC & Vigna na Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of September, 2017
1) Sri K.Kodanda Ram Chief Coordinator , ED Cell and Mr.G.Goutham Babu of
IV th B.Tech,Mechanical have attended an expert talk delivered by Smt.Mohini Kalkar of
Grind Master Machines Pvt Ltd, Aurangabad at ISB, Hyderabad on 01-09-2017.
2) As a part of Career Vision Programme an expert talk was arranged by Sri Vamsi
Krishna, an Entrepreneur of FIXU Services Pvt. Ltd, Hyderabad to I Year B.Tech EEE
students on14-09-2017, in which 52 students have participated.
3) Indian Innovators Association has organized a 3 day Exhibition during 08-09-2017 to
10-09-2017 at Visakhapatnam led by Dr.A.S.Rao, President, Indian Innovators
Association. Students and faculty of VNRVJIET have won gold and silver medals for
exhibiting their innovative ideas in the International Innovation Fair-2017.
Dr.A.S.Rao, President, Indian Innovators Association was honored by
Sri N.Chandra Babu Naidu, Chief Minister, A.P
1
Dr D.N.Rao and Dr.A.S.Rao have participated in a discussion about the International
Fair in TV-5 satellite TV Channel

Minister of Andhra Pradesh Sri. Nara Chandra Babu Naidu for building an innovation portal in
IIA FAIR 2017, Vishakapatnam.

Hyderabad has given a talk on Entrepreneur Journey to B.Tech I-Year EEE students,
In which 57 students have participated on 19-09-2017.
An expert talks on Entrepreneurship Journey by Ajay Akhnoor on 19-092017

Entrepreneurs have participated and received mementos from Dr.D.N.Rao, President,

Dr.C.D.Naidu,Principal,VNRVJIET. On this occasion a website of an Alumni
