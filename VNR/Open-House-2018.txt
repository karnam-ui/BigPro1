Open House 2018 Report
"Open House-2018" is held from 12-03-2018 to 13-03-2018 at VNR VJIET in the premises
of PG Block. The Open House is inaugurated by Dr. D. N. Rao, President on 12th , March
2018.

scrutiny of the posters was done by Dr. A.S. Rao, Dr. B. Chenna Kesava Rao and Dr. G.
Ramesh Chandra.
