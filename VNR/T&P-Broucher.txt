Introduction
Integrated Mentoring, Training & Placements is an innovative and
novel process introduced by the Management of VNR VJIET. The aim is,
initiate the students to the academic environment, guide, motivate
and train all students from the very beginning to enhance their growth
and help them achieve their goals. This program enables each student
to become pro-active in defining their own goals and bring out
inherent talent.
VNR VJIET also makes every effort explicitly to provide employment
opportunities to the students in Core, High Tech, Indian & Global
companies. Our Experts facilitate the students to explore on career
options and impart knowledge on key competencies required to fulfill
individual aspirations. With this objective a policy on ‘Training &
Placement’ is evolved.
VNR VJIET adopts the policy of inclusiveness of all categories of
students and the students of different performance levels in providing
the opportunities keeping intact the unbiased social justice to all the
students.

TRAINING & PLACEMENTS
T&P Organization
The Training & Placement Cell is headed by experienced fulltime
expert / Professor assisted by officers (Corporate Relations) and
Training Officer.
A placement committee comprises of representatives from faculty
and students, functions with a responsibility to organize Company
visits, student database, prepare placement brochure, communicate
the companies, support placement activities and participate in
campus placements etc.
Student representatives along with faculty members coordinate the
activities of ‘High Intensity Training program’ and other training
programs that are structured throughout 4 yrs.
TERM: The term usually is one year since the student representatives
are from final year. In case a member not showing interest or not
complying with the activities, a new member of the same branch is
nominated with the concurrence from the Department Head.
Periodicity of the Meeting: The training & placement committee
will meet twice in semester – On emergency whenever a placement
activity is scheduled, the committee will meet with either particular
branch or with all the branch coordinators as per placement
requirements. Minutes of the meeting will be shared either through
mail or through hard copy.

TRAINING & PLACEMENTS
Norms for students to attend the
Campus Placement drives
Student to be eligible for placement must have attended at least
75% of the Training classes.
Student has to register him/herself at the beginning of the third
year second semester for placement in the given format and by
paying a nominal fees as fixed by the institute, indicating the dream
company choice.
Submit the latest CV online with a recent digital photograph and
also submit five pass port size photographs ( Hard copies).
Submit a copy each of the relevant certificates as given in the
Registration format both soft and hard copies.
Students are advised to follow the norms displayed from time to
time to maintain the dress code etc. while appearing for placement
selection.
General
Training Cell provides equal opportunity of training to all the
aspirant students.
Each student will be given a maximum of five attempts in campus
placement drives.
Student, once selected will not be allowed further in any placement
drive until and unless all the students of that class got placement.
However, he/she will be allowed for his/her Dream Company.
As soon as the student gets the dream job, he/she would not be
considered for any other company.
Student who fails to attend the placement interview, whether on or
off Campus , subsequent to his/her consent for attending the same
will be removed from the eligible placement list.

Thrust Areas of the Finishing School
- Career Vision Approach
VNR VJIET Finishing School is a unique endeavor with the vision of
enhancing employment amongst fresh engineering graduates.
Imparting business communication, technical, and behavioral skills is
essential for sustained performance and career success, meeting the
ever-growing global industry demand for suitable talent at the entry
level.
In order to address the gap between campus to corporate, Finishing
School offers a range of student development programs that are
aimed at enhancing the employability skills of students and young
graduates, thus preparing them to face the challenges and demands
of the real world.
*A dedicated Training & Placement Cell Provides Expert Services for all Engineering
Students through Career Vision Approach
The concept and design of the Finishing School is to support the student
journey from Campus to the Corporate with Career Vision Approach.

Hands-on session on Agile Project
Delivery by DBS India
Venue: A113 Computer Science Lab
Date: 20/8/2022
Speakers: SITARAM TADEPALLI, SHIKHA SRIVASTAVA, ROHINI
JANAPALA
Attendees: 43 IV year students across CSE, IT
We were very fortunate to host the DBS team at VNR VJIET, for the
campus connect program. They delivered a preplacement session on
“Agile Product Delivery” for the few selected fourth year students as a
part of the campus connect program.
The session started with an introduction to the company. Thereafter
insights were given as to why agile software development is required in
today’s world. A comparison was given of how agile software
development differs from the traditional software development
process.
TRAINING & PLACEMENTS
The team highlighted that coding is just one part but how it is done is
important. Hence, agile methodology is adopted to develop software
that gives the principled way of developing the software’s to avoid
wastages such as inventory, defects, motion, transportation, over
processing, defects, and waiting.
The key takeaways of the session: AGILE principles, simulation of AGILE,
concepts in AGILE, how to create GIT repository and how to create
sprints. The team conducted a group activity and best teams were
given prizes.
