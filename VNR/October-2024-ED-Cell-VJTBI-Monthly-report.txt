A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME
Vignana Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI)
Activities for the Month of October 2024
I) ED Cell Activities:
1. M. Venkata Ramana, i/c ED Cell and Professor, AED attended webinar on
16.10.2024 from 02.30 to 05.00 PM. Dr. K.R. Suresh Nair, Founder and
Managing Director, Amar Raja Design Alpha delivered a talk on Product life
cycle. Dr. BernardLim, Vice president, Director and Co - Founder of Appscard
Group delivered a talk on understanding the current Market trends.
2. 13 students from different programs of B.Tech. attended the E-Merge 2.0 event
at IIT Hyderabad on 20.10.2024. This event provided a good opportunity to our
students to interact with students from other colleges across the country.
3. M. Venkata Ramana, i/c ED Cell and Professor, AED attended webinar on
21.10.2024 from 02.30 to 04.00 PM. Dr. J. Lakhmi, CEO, IIITB, Innovation
Center, Executive Vice President, COMET Foundation delivered a talk on
Translational Research.
4. M. Venkata Ramana, i/c ED Cell and Professor, AED, Dr. Shaik Amzad,
HoD/AED, Dr. Shaik Ansari, Head, IIR, Adjunct Professor, AED and Dr. E. V.
Ramana, Professor, MED attended a Seminar on Convergence of Academia and
Industry 4.0 on 23.10.2024 organized by Marconi Technologies, at The Westin,
Hyderabad. The resource persons Mr. M. Kannan, Director, Technical, Marconi
Technologie, Mr. Rakesh, Technical Solution Consultant, Marconi Technologies,
Mr. Promod Butty, National Technical Manager, Marconi Technologies delivered
talks on Industry 4.0, Product life Cycle, and Simulations of EVs.
II) MSME & VJTBI Activities:
1. Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSME), New Delhi called for Idea (Project)
Proposals under MSME Idea Hackathon 4.0. The same was communicated
through circular and Institute email to all faculty and students. A total of 73 Idea
proposals were received. The screening and evaluation committees are formed
as per scheme guidelines to select the best ideas for uploading into the MSME
portal for further processing.
2. Development of the Two Ideas under MSME Hackathon 2.0 are under progress.
(Dr. K. Ajay Kumar/ MED and Dr. D. Ramesh Reddy/ECE).
