1) An expert talk was arranged on IP for Resea rchers at Dr.A.PJ.Abdul Kalam Auditorium on
10-08-2017 from 3 PM to 5 PM. In which 63 faculty members and 12 students have
participated .The speakers of the session are Sri.Arjun Bala, Managing Partner, Patent
Attorney,CPVA of Metayage IP Strategy Consulting LLP,Bangalore and Sri. Kameshwar
Eranki,President & CEO,VajraSoft Inc.,Dublin,USA, who shared their patent experiences
as case studies through a power point presentation.
