ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT
The Department of EIE was established in the year 1999 with 60 students intake in the UG
program in Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering (EIE). Due to strong demand, the intake
was enhanced to 120 during the year 2011. A Post Graduate program in Electronic and
Instrumentation Engineering was started during the year 2010. Minor degree Program in AI
and ML, Data Science, Cyber Security and Banking, Financial Services & Insurance (BFSI) are
offered to the UG-EIE students. The Department is accredited by National Board of
Accreditation (NBA) from 2008 onwards. More than 50 percentage of the faculty members of
the Department are PhD degree holders from IITs, NITs and other State universities of repute.
The faculty members and the students have published their research work in journals and
conferences of repute that are indexed in Web of Science, Science Citation Index, Scopus and
other reputed databases. The Department funds the students to take up innovative projects and
apply for patent. A few have been granted with students as inventers. The faculty members of
the Department have secured external funding for research from DRDO, IIMR, AICTE and other
private agencies.
ABOUT THE PROGRAMME
The program blends Software Programming, Electronics, Control systems, and Instrumentation,
preparing students for software, automation, healthcare, and robotics industries. The program
provides practical experience through labs, workshops, and industry collaborations with cutting-
edge tools like PLCs, IoT devices, and embedded systems. With a Minor Degree in Artificial
Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI&ML), Data Science, IoT, or BFSI, the career prospects of the
EIE graduates stand on par with any other contemporary programs.
ABOUT FACULTY
The Department has well-qualified faculty with expertise in diverse areas, including Process
Control Instrumentation, Automation, Signal and Image Processing, Bio-Medical Instrumentation,
Embedded Systems, Internet of Things, Robotics, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence. A
good number of faculty members are trained at IITs on contemporary technologies like IoT,
Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science.
CAREER
The career opportunities in this field span from Research, Design, Development, Manufacturing,
Process, and Control in industries of Electronics, Electrical, Chemical, Pharmaceutical, Food,
Agriculture, Biomedical, Automotive, to Power generation. Graduates can serve top-notch
Industries and IT companies in India and abroad. 96 percent and 100 percent of the graduates
of the 2025 batch with a minor in AI&ML and Data Science have been placed successfully with
high packages, respectively.
STUDENT INTERNSHIPS
The students secure internships in reputed organizations like DRDO, NRSC, NTPC, BHEL, ZF TCI India, Analog
Devices, JPMC, Deloitte, Amazon, Accenture, TCS, and so on. The internships in the private sector are mostly
converted to full-time employment after the internship period.
