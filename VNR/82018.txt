I) ED Cell Activities:
(i)Sri K.Kodanda Ram ,Chief Coordinator, ED Cell interacted with the students of I-Year
B.Tech, IT-1 on 09-08-2018 during 2.40 PM to 3.40 PM in Room No :A-002, B-Block. Sri
A.Srinivasa Rao, Smt .B.Lavanya Assistant Professor, IT have coordinated the activity. The
session was engaged with topics like ED Cell Activities, Creativity, Innovation, MBIC App and
Ideation to 54 students.
(ii) Dr.A.S.Rao interacted with of I-Year B.Tech students of AE in D-402 (Yogaiah Naidu Bhavan)
during 1.40 PM to 2.40 PM on 10-08-2018. Sri K.Kodanda Ram, Sri Balappa Hadagali
coordinated the activity. The topics covered were Industrial revolution Future Technologies
and Ideation exercise on future cars .In which 57 students have participated.
(iii) Sri K.Kodanda Ram ,Chief Coordinator, ED Cell interacted with the students of I-Year B.Tech
,AE on 10-08-2018 during 2.40 PM to 3.40 PM in D-402. Sri Balappa Hadagali coordinated
the activity. The session was attended by 57 student’s covering topics like ED Cell Activities,
VJ –Hub activities, MBIC App and Ideation.
(iv) Sri K.Kodanda Ram ,Chief Coordinator, ED Cell interacted with the students of I-Year B.Tech
,AE on 13-08-2018 during 11.00 AM to 12.30 PM in D-402. Sri Balappa Hadagali coordinated
the activity. The session covered some topics from Entrepreneur Magazine, Economic Times
Paper. In which 53 student have attended.
(v) A session was organized by Sri K.Kodanda Ram, Chief Coordinator, ED Cell on 20-08-2018
covering the topics like Creativity & Innovation, Biomimicing using a power point presentation
in D-402 during 11.00AM to 12.30 PM to the students of I-B.Tech, AE. Sri Balappa Hadagali
coordinated the activity. In which 52 Students have attended.
(vi) A session was conducted by Sri K.Kodanda Ram to 68 students of I –B.Tech ECE-II & III on
20-08-2018 during 1.40 PM to 4.00 PM in B-213 covering topics like ED Cell, VJTBI, VJ-Hub,
TEP/ ISB, Incubation, Ideation, Creativity and Innovation. Smt G.Lakshmi, Sri.G.Ramesh
Kumar coordinated the activity.
(vii) Dr.A.S.Rao has acted as resource person to I-Year B.Tech, AE students on 27-08-2018
during 11.00 AM to 12.30 PM in D-402 covering topics like Imagineering, Differences
between Science &Engineering and Ideation Exercises on smart cars. The session was
attended by 48 Students.
