1. PREAMBLE
Knowledge is widely recognized as the key driver for economic growth and the
development of the global economy, especially in the midst of the information and
communication revolution. In this regard, technical education plays a vital role in
creating skilled engineers, enhancing industry productivity, and improving the overall
quality of social life. The impact of technology has revolutionized the traditional
classroom teaching and learning methods, offering novel approaches to address
intellectual, social, and practical problems that often require interdisciplinary solutions.
In order to stay at the forefront of both academia and research, it is imperative to
empower our teachers and ensure they are well-informed about current and future
trends. By aligning the curriculum with both academic and research orientations, we
can empower our students to think outside the box and explore new horizons, thereby
nurturing their ability to tackle complex challenges.
Vallurupalli Nageswara Rao Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering and Technology
(VNR VJIET) is an exceptional institution that has consistently pursued academic
excellence through unique pathways since its establishment. Over the years, it has
emerged as one of the most distinguished and premier higher education institutions in
the State of Telangana. Its complexity, diversity, and comprehensiveness serve as a
wellspring of creativity and innovation.
As a modern world-class academic institute, VNR VJIET is strongly committed to
sustainable development through research and the expansion of innovative
technologies. In an era driven by global progress and technology, research conducted
within academic institutions not only fosters economic growth but also contributes to
achieving self-reliance in technology and innovation in line with the national mission
initiatives such as Atmanirbhar Bharat, Make in India and Digital India. VNR VJIET
focuses on fundamental, long-term research and innovation in cutting-edge
technologies, encompassing a diverse range of activities, including:
 Producing high-quality engineers equipped with the necessary skills and
knowledge at various levels, from undergraduate to postgraduate.
 Exploring new horizons through fundamental research.
 Continuously enhancing the knowledge repository and domain expertise.
 Encouraging new ideas and proposals through research awards and rewards.
 Becoming a source of innovation, addressing societal needs, and developing new
products that contribute to revenue generation.
Institutions that combine state-of-the-art research and academics make substantial
contributions to national prosperity and security. The strategic plan of VNR VJIET is
designed with this context in mind, providing a clear sense of direction and outlining
measurable goals. The Strategy Document emphasizes the importance of achieving
objectives while adhering to a high-quality policy and core values. By following this
2
strategic plan, VNR VJIET aims to strengthen its position as a leading institution in the
field of higher education and research, and to continue its journey towards academic
excellence and innovation.
2. VJ PHILOSOPHY
It is the philosophy of Vignana Jyothi that education is a process of "presencing" that
leads, both individually and collectively, to one's deepest capacity to sense and shape
the future. Based on a synthesis of direct experience, leading-edge thinking, and ancient
wisdom, it taps into “serendipity” and deeper levels of “learning” to discover new
possibilities.
3. INSTITUTE VISION
To be a world class University providing value-based education, conducting
interdisciplinary research in cutting edge technologies leading to sustainable socio-
economic development of the nation.
4. INSTITUTE MISSION
 To produce technically competent and socially responsible engineers, managers
and entrepreneurs, who will be future ready.
 To involve students and faculty in innovative research projects linked with
industry, academic and research institutions in India and abroad.
 To use modern pedagogy for improving the teaching-learning process.
5. QUALITY POLICY
 Impart up-to-date knowledge in the students’ chosen fields to make them quality
engineers.
 Make the students experience the applications on quality equipment and tools.
 Provide quality environment and services to all stakeholders.
 Provide systems, resources and opportunities for continuous improvement.
 Maintain global standards in education, training and Services.
6. CORE VALUES
VNRVJIET upholds a set of core values that define its principles and aspirations. These
values permeate all activities and endeavors of the institution:
 Values-Based Education:
We foster a robust learning environment that instills strong values and ethics,
enhancing academic excellence and nurturing students' social and interpersonal
skills to shape them into responsible citizens.
3
 Equity and Inclusiveness:
We equip students with knowledge and skills to actively participate, collaborate,
compete, and engage with society, ensuring that personal or socio-economic
circumstances do not become barriers to their success.
 Sustainability:
We envision a future that encompasses environmental and socio-economic
requirements, cultivating the knowledge and skills necessary to achieve
sustainable development.
 Compliance with Requirements:
We ensure that every activity and procedure adheres to the established
guidelines and specifications set by governing authorities and statutory bodies.
 Righteousness:
We uphold integrity and transparency in our deliverables through well-

7. 1. Teaching-Learning:
Achieve academic excellence by designing, implementing, and continuously
updating the curriculum focusing on advanced frontiers of knowledge and
facilitates experiential learning through ICT.
4
Values-based education creates a robust learning environment that enhances
academic achievement and fosters the development of students' social and
relationship skills, which endure throughout their lives. This is accomplished
through a blend of teaching and learning methods that integrate ethical values and
cutting-edge interdisciplinary research. As a result, students experience holistic
personality development and gain social competence, equipping them with the
necessary skills, intelligence, and attitude to thrive in all aspects of their lives. The
pursuit of academic excellence at VNR VJIET ensures a value-added experience for
students.
At VNR VJIET, we foster an environment that nurtures and celebrates academic
excellence, individual initiative, and personal responsibility as students work
towards their educational goals. We are committed to maintaining high standards of
academic performance and have established consequences for unacceptable
academic results.
VNRVJIET aims to deliver "Excellence" achieved through the implementation of
effective educational practices as well as "Prestige," which is the provision of high-
quality education to empower students to become successful professionals in their
respective fields. We aim to nurture careers characterized by excellence and
leadership in the domains of science and technology through the following
measures:
 Cultivate excellence in the educational system in order to meet the
knowledge challenges of the 21st century and enhance our competitive
advantage in various fields of knowledge.
 Develop a curriculum that aligns with the latest scientific and technological
advancements, while also incorporating anticipated future developments.
 Foster interdisciplinary interactions among faculty, students, the industry,
and the external community, focusing on emerging technologies and the
evolving needs of society.
Strategies:
Structured Induction Procedure:
At VNR VJIET, excellence in engineering education is viewed from the students'
perspective, where they learn not only in classrooms but also through active
engagement in listening, visualizing, experimenting, experiencing, and practical
training. Our philosophy of education adopts an "end in mind" approach, and thus, the
teaching-learning process is structured as the 'Career Vision Approach.' This approach
encourages students to envision their career opportunities and outlines the path for
successful transition towards their goals. To facilitate this, we organize TED videos,
inspirational talks by successful entrepreneurs, and lecture series on career
5
opportunities during the freshman days. Moreover, our structured induction program
includes interactions with senior students, exposing first-year students to projects and
practices within the institute based on their individual interests.
Regular Curriculum Revision and Updates (Multidisciplinary Education):
The curricula of all programs at VNRVJIET are designed to encompass multiple
dimensions. In addition to providing engineering knowledge, the curriculum focuses on
instilling desirable attributes, professional ethics, relevant industry skills, and
addressing societal aspirations. To promote higher-order thinking, the course outcomes
are aligned with the revised Bloom's Taxonomy, encouraging analysis and evaluation of
concepts, processes, procedures, and principles. The courses are developed to meet the
Program Educational Objectives (PEOs), Program Outcomes (POs), and Program
Specific Outcomes (PSOs). The implementation of the Choice Based Credit System
enables students to explore cross-domain subjects and technologies, as well as engage
in interdisciplinary project work. The curriculum is regularly updated in alignment with
NEP 2020 to incorporate industry needs and subjects that enhance students' self-
learning and employability skills.
Pedagogy and Delivery Methods:
We adopt a modern pedagogical approach, striking a balance between traditional
methodologies and innovative practices in day-to-day classroom delivery at VNR VJIET.
Our classrooms are equipped with the necessary infrastructure to facilitate new
methods of teaching and learning. VNRVJIET has 18 differentiators in teaching learning
practices which enables the students to enhance their learning skills like WIT and WIL,
Story Board, VNR Lab Protocol, course project based learning etc. Our faculty members
are trained in ICT methodologies and continuously updated through faculty
development programs, workshops, and seminars. To support faculty teaching, student
learning, and communication, we have established a dedicated teaching/learning center
and a writing support center. We also encourage e-learning and online learning as
supplementary approaches to traditional classroom teaching. Smart classrooms with
state-of-the-art facilities to facilitate hybrid (online/ offline) teaching, and easy &
effective interaction with students
By implementing these effective pedagogical strategies and providing the necessary
support systems, we strive to create an engaging and enriching learning environment at
VNR VJIET.
Some significant aspects of a revitalized approach to Teaching-Learning:
 An attempt would be made to reduce the number of contact hours for students
by appropriately reducing the credits to the range as per HEI guidelines.
 More open and interactive spaces for faculty and students would be created
6
 The credit component in the curriculum for Laboratory based learning (keeping
total number of credits intact) would be increased towards a practical
experiential learning approach
 Guided Self-Learning component would also be incorporated and implemented
 Project-based learning would be strengthened
 Students would also be enabled to enhance their soft skills, problem-solving
approaches, and competence to create business plans
 Learning would be considered a collaborative and fun endeavor.
 Industry and Alumni interaction would be further strengthened.
 An Entrepreneurial eco-system would be nurtured and sustained.
 Curriculum could be contemporary and relevant to the industry.
 Inclusion of Interdisciplinary/multidisciplinary courses in line with NEP 2020.
 Digital Humanities courses would be introduced.
 Design Thinking courses could be offered in all the 4 years of study.

7. 2. Research and Innovation:
Promote knowledge exploration through innovation and research, catering
to the diverse needs of society.
In the realm of academia, which now encompasses multiple disciplines within a single
subject, it is crucial to grasp the intricacies of multidisciplinary research in order to
actively engage with scholarly pursuits. The advent of globalization and liberalization
has given rise to a plethora of issues and challenges spanning various fields.
Multidisciplinary research has become indispensable in addressing complex problems,
serving as a vital link between fundamental and applied research to the transformative
research which is the need of the hour to encourage National Startup India Mission.
Our Institute places a strong emphasis on fostering students' imagination, initiative, and
practical skills, equipping them with the ability to innovate and confidently navigate
through challenges. In addition to academic endeavors, we have introduced an
innovative concept of establishing Virtual Industries within our laboratories, providing
invaluable exposure, experience, and opportunities for both students and faculty. Our
exclusive Research and Consultancy Center (RCC) further promotes multidimensional
research in cutting-edge technologies by cultivating a vibrant research culture and
effectively harnessing available competencies. We actively undertake problem-specific
and inventive research to address regional and national needs. Therefore, our Institute's
commitment to maintaining its status as a leading research institution inherently
involves a dedication to high-quality teaching and learning integrated with research,
achieved through:
 Fostering synergies between research and educational activities
 Implementing research-based learning at various levels, encompassing both
processes and products
 Enhancing collaborative research efforts with academic institutes, industry
partners, and research and development organizations
 Facilitating the cultivation of entrepreneurship through reengineering
approaches.
Strategies:
Cross Domain Knowledge Centers:
VNR VJIET operates cross-domain knowledge centers that expose students to
authentic work environments. These centers possess the flexibility to adapt to
evolving technological platforms, mirroring real industries and providing
8
experiential learning through virtual industry laboratories. The primary goal of
these labs is to enhance the practical skills, imagination, initiative, inquisitiveness,
and teamwork of undergraduate and postgraduate students, enabling them to apply
their knowledge to real-life situations. The Institute offers a unique opportunity for
all students to conduct their full-time project work in these state-of-the-art
laboratories.
Research Centers and Special Interest Groups:
The Institute has established Research Centers in various domains such as Disaster
Mitigation, Renewable Energy Systems, Machine Vision, Data Science, Networks and
Cyber Security, Robotics, Micro Cars, Joining Technologies, Education Technology,
and Nano Technology. These centers are strategically distributed across
departments to foster focused advanced research and foster partnerships between
the Institute and industry. They also facilitate industry consultancy and testing.
Faculty and student involvement in these centers ensures they stay updated with
developments in their respective fields, preparing them to tackle real-world
problems. Additionally, special interest groups are formed to concentrate on specific
areas of development. Scheduled meetings of these groups facilitate discussions on
technological advancements and explore project proposals for funding or in-house
execution, involving students. These discussions often result in high-quality
publications.
Innovation and Entrepreneurship:
The Institute endeavors to create an ecosystem for deep collaboration with the
industry through consultancy, sponsored research projects, technology transfer, and
continuing education. Dedicated facilities called the Weekend Projects Lab and
Twinning Lab have been established to transform classroom learning into a project-
based experience. Innovation is fostered through initiatives like the Big Idea
Competition, where the best ideas are rewarded. Infrastructure and seed funding
are provided through the VJ HUB, an incubation facility for stakeholders to develop
and implement their ideas. VNRVJIET offers young technocrats the opportunity to
unleash their full potential by establishing their own ventures, thus becoming job
generators rather than job seekers. Start-ups are supported in developing their
ideas into commercially viable products in line with the National Mission of Start-
Up India.
The Institute provides advanced training and mentoring to students through the
Entrepreneurship Development Cell, enabling them to translate their ideas into
practical applications or products. This is facilitated through awareness camps,
guest lectures, seminars, workshops, business plans, and skill development
programs. The Industry Institute Interaction Cell actively builds partnerships with
industries in areas aligned with the Institute's strengths. Collaborations are fostered
through structured student internships and the appointment of industry
9
professionals as Adjunct Faculty and Professors of Practice. A significant amount of
research originates from the faculty's engagement with the industry, addressing
identified problems.
Some key aspects of our planned approach to Research and Innovation:
 Efforts would be made to enable the faculty to invest more time in research; PG
students and Ph.D. scholars would be encouraged to take up teaching
assistantships for better learning and experience as well as provide for more
research time to the faculty
 FSR to be maintained at 1:15 over the complete academic year. (To handle the
floating of faculty we aim to maintain 1:14 based on student strength in the
campus)
 Create better awareness about availability of Seed Money for research projects
 While faculty submit research proposals, 10% of the budget could come from the
institution/ industry
 Leveraging Alumni network for MOUs with industries
 Conduct high-end conferences for establishing a research ecosystem
 Increase intake of Ph.D. scholars (The target could be about 100 full-time
scholars sponsored by the institute and industry)
 Run Executive Development Programs in emerging technologies
 Building an innovation ecosystem & Pipelining for startups
 Revenue generation through research projects and consultancy
 Collaboration with premium institutes like IITs, NITs & IIITs for research
projects
 Establishing high-end research infrastructure (Research centres) and more
spaces for research activities
 Research budget component in the annual budget may be enhanced
 Research incentives for publications, projects and patents
 Networking with the State and National innovation and entrepreneurial eco
system

7.3. Holistic Development of Students:
Enable students to cultivate a well-rounded personality with a global vision
and a strong sense of social responsibility.
11
The holistic approach to education at VNRVJIET embodies the necessary elements of
creativity, interdisciplinary learning, complexity, and adaptability to meet global needs
effectively. Our values-based education creates a robust learning environment that not
only enhances academic achievement but also nurtures students' social and relationship
skills, which endure throughout their lives. We achieve positive outcomes by integrating
ethical values into our teaching-learning methods and conducting cross-domain
research in cutting-edge technologies. This approach facilitates the comprehensive
development of our students' personalities, equipping them with the social capacity,
skills, intelligences, and attitudes required for success in all aspects of life.
Active participation in co-curricular activities (CCA) and extra-curricular activities
(ECA) plays a pivotal role in enhancing students' well-rounded personalities, preparing
them to navigate the challenges of the future confidently. Through these activities,
students gain valuable experiences and appreciation that greatly assist them during
internships. Moreover, these activities allow students to hone their talents and develop
specialized skills. Remarkably, academic performance also improves as students learn
to balance their involvement in CCA/ECA with their academic pursuits, while
simultaneously gaining a deeper understanding of effective time management.
Our strategic plan focuses on the following key aspects:
 Build self-confidence and empower students to excel in their academic pursuits.
 Provide comprehensive training for skill development and encourage active
participation in internships.
 Foster inclusiveness and cultivate a strong sense of social responsibility among
students.
Strategies:
Career Guidance, Competence Development Cell (CDC), Mentoring, Training, and
Placement(MTP):
VNRVJIET implements a unique initiative of Mentoring and Training that plays a pivotal
role in identifying students' strengths and weaknesses and providing targeted training
to enhance their skill set in their chosen domains. Our structured mentoring program
focuses on social-emotional learning and cognitive development, shaping students into
well-rounded, successful, and competitive individuals. Through SWOC analysis, we
assess students' strengths and weaknesses, enabling us to offer career counseling and
focused training through CDC and MTP.
Remedial classes are arranged to enhance the academic skills of students who require
additional support. During freshman year induction, students are familiarized with the
campus and available facilities, setting the stage for their dreams to come true.
Interaction with senior students provides insights into the academic and personal
growth opportunities within the institute. This interaction allows newly joined students
to learn from the experiences of seniors, including exposure to academic projects,
12
research involvement, participation in professional society activities, and engagement
in other student clubs. We also establish networking opportunities with alumni, offering
career guidance, role models, and professional opportunities. The Career Vision
Approach enables students to visualize the advancements in their chosen careers and
develop strategies to continuously improve and stay updated.
Student Support and Progression:
At VNRVJIET, we encourage and support students in setting their own targets. Our
institute strives to expand opportunities throughout and beyond the student lifecycle.
We provide exposure to cutting-edge technologies and advancements through on-
campus certification courses. We facilitate flexible learning through MOOCs, SWAYAM
NPTEL, Coursera, edX, Udemy etc. On campus training and skill development courses,
along with MOUs with reputed organizations and industries, engage students in
internships and open doors to future employment. Peer-led learning is promoted to
help students build expectations and confidence.
Extra-Curricular and Co-Curricular Activities:
VNRVJIET integrates co-curricular and extra-curricular activities into student life to
foster the development of well-rounded personalities. We have institutionalized a
robust ECA/CCA system, establishing various cultural committees and student chapters.
Our exclusive Coliseum (Student Activity Center) is dedicated to these activities. We
provide a well-equipped sports complex with ample play areas, along with a National
Service Scheme (NSS) Unit on campus. Students are encouraged to participate in
intercollegiate, interuniversity, state-level, and national-level competitions. Each year,
we organize a national-level cultural fest, sponsoring students to participate in cultural
and sports events organized off-campus.

7.4. Quality Assurance
Continuously strive for institutional effectiveness and relentlessly pursue
excellence by implementing robust quality assurance systems.
14
VNRVJIET continuously assesses and aligns its culture, people, structure, work
processes, and technologies with its Vision, Mission, Objectives, and Quality Policies. To
support the strategic areas of academics, research, innovation, and globalization,
maximizing the utilization and effectiveness of human and financial resources and
facilities is essential. Continuous monitoring of key success indicators and sharing these
results with constituent committees enables the review and improvement of support
functions, processes, and resource allocation strategies across the institute.
Quality in Education is defined as the development of intellectual skills and knowledge
that equips graduates to contribute to society through productive and fulfilling
engineering careers, as innovators, decision-makers, and leaders in the global economy.
To achieve the esteemed goal of producing well-qualified and trained technocrats, our
institution works efficiently and effectively. We strive to impart technical competence to
students by creating a conducive environment for their personality development and
enabling them to achieve higher grades in their respective fields. This is achieved
through:
 Transparency and Leadership
 Quality assurance and Accreditations
 Outflow management and growth plans
Strategies:
1. Good Governance:
Participatory governance at VNRVJIET ensures a sound, ethical, and sustainable
environment that meets the needs of the institution and key stakeholders. Policies and
procedures are implemented and progress is monitored through regular updates. The
overlapping interests of stakeholders are addressed to effectively deliver education and
meet national needs. Scheduled interactions among key implementers in academics,
research, innovation, and globalization enable discussions on progress and potential
enhancements. Close monitoring of capital, revenue, income, and expenditures ensures
timely fulfillment of requirements.
2. Resources and Infrastructure:
Continuous efforts are made to identify and retain critical faculty talent, enhancing
outcomes in a learner-centric environment. Opportunities for professional
development, collaboration, and networking are provided to faculty for upgrading their
competence. Faculty members stay updated with the frontier of science and technology
through qualifications enhancement, workshops, seminars, conferences, and other
quality improvement programs. Industry-oriented certificate courses and practical
training in industries are integrated into the curriculum to bridge the gap between
education and societal needs. IT infrastructure, library resources, and learning spaces
15
are upgraded to align with curriculum updates and research demands. Expansion plans
are regularly reviewed, and budgeting is done based on expense monitoring and
revenue projections. Adequate provisions are made for research and development
activities, including access to scientific journals and recruitment of qualified and
motivated research-oriented faculty.
3. Quality Assurance Systems:
VNRVJIET prioritizes quality assurance systems to maintain a growth-oriented
approach and a strong reputation. These systems benchmark against reputable
organizations and provide metrics for continuous monitoring and implementation of
standard procedures aligned with the institution's Vision and Mission. Internal audits,
ISO Audit, Academic Audit and Administrative Audit led by the quality policy steering
committee, are conducted to identify any areas for improvement, and remedial
measures are suggested and implemented.

 Faculty Appraisals, Awards and Incentives:

7.5. Human Resources:
Strengthen and optimize our human resources strategies to nurture a
culture of professional growth and excellence among faculty, staff, and
administrators.
To foster a culture of professional growth and excellence among faculty, staff, and
administrators at VNRVJIET, we will focus on strengthening and optimizing our human
resources strategies. Our aim is to create an environment that supports continuous

8. Alignment with the National Education Policy:
VNRVJIET is committed to providing quality education and nurturing well-rounded
individuals. As an institution, we recognize the National Education Policy (NEP) as a
transformative roadmap for reforming the education system in India and strive to align our
academic practices with the principles and objectives outlined in it. The institution’s strategic
plan also reflects how VNRVJIET has embraced the NEP and outlines how we will further
strengthen this alignment over the next five years. We have undertaken several initiatives to
ensure alignment with the key tenets of the policy. Given below is a brief overview of the
same.
 Firstly, we have emphasized multidisciplinary learning by encouraging students to
explore diverse academic disciplines through flexible course offerings. By promoting
cross-disciplinary interactions and collaborations, we foster holistic development and
enable students to develop a broad-based skill set.
 Secondly, VNRVJIET has adopted a competency-based education approach, which
focuses on the application of knowledge rather than rote memorization. Our faculty
members employ innovative teaching methodologies, including project-based
learning, case studies, and practical assignments, to develop critical thinking,
problem-solving, and communication skills among students. This pedagogical shift
empowers learners to adapt to real-world challenges and enhances their
employability prospects.
 Thirdly, we have embraced technology-enabled learning to enhance accessibility
and flexibility in education. By leveraging online platforms, we offer blended
learning models, incorporating both in-person and virtual components. This approach
allows us to reach a wider audience and cater to diverse learning needs, making
education more inclusive and learner-centric.
