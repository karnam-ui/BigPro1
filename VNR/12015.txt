DATE: 31/01/2015
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & Vignana Jyothi Technology Business
Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the Month of January, 2015
1) Dr.A.Subba Rao Garu, Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju & Sri.K.Kodanda Ram interacted
with TEP ISB (2014-2016) students on 03-01-2015 from 3.30 PM to 4.30 PM at P.G
Block Seminar Hall and advised the students to participate in My Big Idea Competition
by taking leverage of TEP/ISB training. They are asked to convert their mini projects as
proposals for MSME incubation support.
2) A workshop was held by Akshay Jalan ,Academic Associate and Kritika Mathur
Prasad,Academic Associate (TEP/ISB Faculty) on 07-01-2015 at B-Block Seminar
Hall,VNRVJIET between 10 AM to 3PM with a break of one Hour between 1 PM to 2
PM.During this occasion Dr.A.Subba Rao Garu, Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju &
Sri.K.Kodanda Ram addressed the students regarding the progress of the programme.
3) An Info session on "Taking a step ahead from Engineering with Technology
Entrepreneurship Programme(TEP)”was held by Akshay Jalan ,Academic Associate
and Kritika Mathur Prasad, Academic Associate (TEP/ISB Faculty) on 07-01-2015 at
K.S. Auditorium,VNRVJIET between 3PM to 4PM for B.Tech II Year II Sem students of
all branches of VNRVJIET regarding admission process for admission into
TEP(Sponsored by the Governments of Andhra and Telangana States & Organized by
ISB for the course period of 2015-2017). About 115 students have enthusiastically
attended the session and have shown interest to join the programme. This programme
was organized by coordinators of ED Cell.
4) Dr.A.Subba Rao Garu, Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju & Sri.K.Kodanda Ram had interacted
with M.Tech I Year I Semester Power Systems students on 17-01-2015 at P.G Block
Seminar Hall from 3.30 PM to 4.30PM in which 12 students have attended.
5) Presentations of My Big Idea Competition on the Theme of “Swachh Bharat” was held
at P.G Block Seminar Hall on 21-01-2015 from 4.30 PM to 5.30 PM in which 8
students of VNRVJIET & VJIM have presented their ideas. Dr.A.Subba Rao Garu
K.Kodanda Ram & Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju acted as judges to select the best ideas.
6) A live webex / Webinar session from RADY School of Management, USA a session by
Prof. Sanjiv Erat called Introduction to Platforms. This session is directly related to the
module Live Design which is open for the students of TEP on the platform was
organized by TEP/ISB Faculty of 2014-2016 batch on 22-01-2015 at P.G Block
Seminar Hall between 10 AM to 11 AM in which 11 Students of TEP have attended.
This programme was monitored by Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju & Narne Raghuvardhan,
TEP student.
7) Dr.C.D.Naidu ,Chairman ,ED Cell & VJTBI has nominated the following Faculty
members from each department with the recommendations of their respective HOD’s
to act as faculty in charge to identify projects for MSME VJTBI Business incubator on
20-01-2015. These faculties will be identifying the business ideas that arise among the
Faculty & Students with respect to their departments and forward the same from time
to time to the coordinators of VJTBI.
