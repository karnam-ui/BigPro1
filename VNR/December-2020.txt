A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell&MSMEVignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of December,2020.
I) ED Cell Activities:
1) A n expert talk was arranged through online by Sri .K.Kodanda Ram, Coordinator , ED
Cell to I Year B.Tech I-Sem CSE Students belonging to IoT and Cyber Security
Branches on “Activities of ED Cell in VNRVJIE” on 22-12-2020, in which 110 students
have participated. Mr.R.Kranthi Kumar Assistant Professor ,CSE has coordinated the
activity.
2) An expert talk was arranged through online by Sri .K.Kodanda Ram, Coordinator ,ED
Cell to I Year B.Tech I-Sem CSE-3,CSBS and CSE-DS Students on “Activities of ED
Cell in VNRVJIE” on 26-12-2020, in which 150 students have participated.
Mr.K.Bheemalingappa ,Assistant Professor ,CSE has coordinated the activity.
3) Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Professor of Mechanical Engineering and Coordinator ,ED
cell has given presentation on “Review on progress of ED Cell activities during the
period from January,2019 to December,2020” in Board Room in the Presence of
Principal and Director for Advancement and Dean (Administration) on 29-12-2020
between 3 PM to 5 PM.
