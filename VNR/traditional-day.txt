Traditional Day continues to strengthen the bonds within our college, creating an environment where individuals from various backgrounds can thrive and learn from one another. It is a day of celebration, learning, and unity that embodies the values and spirit of VNRVJIET.
Traditional Day is a special occasion at VNR VJIET where students, faculty, and staff come together to celebrate and showcase the rich cultural diversity that exists within our college community. This annual event provides a unique platform for individuals to express their cultural identities, learn about different traditions, and strengthen the bonds of unity.
