Date: 30/06/2016
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell , IIPC & Vign ana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of June, 2016
1) Submitted Bond for utilization of funds , Pre -Receipt Bill and Undertaking to MSME,
New Delhi on 10-06-2016 for release of First installment grant-in-aid for four project
Ideas of Rs. 7,43,160/-{Rs. 6.978 Lakh for support services(30% of sanctioned
Amount) + Rs. 45,360/- for infrastructure and training of 4 ideas}.
2) Gogineni Srinivasa ,Chairman, GBI India Ltd gave presentation about the profile of his
company and possible interaction with Vignana Jyothi Terchnology Business Incubator
(VJTBI) in board room on 15-06-2016 and which was not fruitful.
3) Sri N.V.S.Krishna Rao, Chief Manager, Andhra Bank, JNTUH gave consent to be a
member of Local Management Committee for MSME, New Delhi sponsored Business
Incubator.
4) Submitted Bank Mandate Form to MSME, New Delhi through on line on 21-06-2016 for
release of First installment grant-in-aid for four approved project Ideas.
5) Jarsh Advanced Safety Systems(Temperature Controlled Helmet):
The team has visited a helmet manufacturing plant for 2 days at Vega Auto
Accessories, Udyambag, Belagavi, Karnataka and have attained valuable inputs on
design parameters involved. A detailed study of plant was done and design of helmet as
per inputs is being contemplated and done simultaneously.
VJ Hub (ED Cell, IIPC, RCC & MSME VJTBI) Coordinators
