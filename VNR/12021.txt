A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of January,2021.
I) ED Cell Activities:
1) A s a part of Career Vision Programme an expert talk was arranged through online by
Sri .K.Kodanda Ram, Coordinator ,ED Cell to I Year B.Tech I-Sem CSE-2 Students on
the topic “Activities of ED Cell in VNRVJIE” on 08-01-2021, in which 60 students have
participated.
2) A Meeting was arranged with Patent Attorney of K.S Partners using MS Teams by
Sri.K.Kodanda Ram, Chief Coordinator of ED Cell and Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju,
Coordinator of ED Cell to 4 Mechanical Engineering Students belonging to II Year
B.Tech I-Sem pertaining to Patenting of an Innovative Idea on 11-01-2021 between
4PM to 5PM and obtained cost estimation for filing patent application.
3) A session on Machine Learning and Data Science was arranged by CII, Telangana in
association with ED Cell VNRVJIET to I Year B.Tech I-Sem students of
CSE-1 to 4,CSBS,CS- IoT, CS- DS,CS- AIML and CS- CyS on 22-01-2021 between
1.30 PM to 3 PM, in which 540 students have participated.
4) An Orientation Session on Young Leaders Programme (YLP) was arranged to the
students of 3rd and 4th Year B.Tech students of all branches of VNRVJIET offered by
Indian School of Business(ISB) ) on 28-01-2021 between 5 PM to 6 PM through Online
mode. The event was attended by 36 students. The event was organized by the
students coordinators ofstudents body of ED Cell, Sri.K.Kodanda Ram and Dr
S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju.
(ED Cell & MSME VJTBI Coordinators)
