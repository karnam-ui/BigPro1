DATE: 24/12/2015
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell , IIPC & Vignana Jyothi
Technology Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of December, 2015
1) An approval received on 02-12-2015 f rom MSME, New Delhi for VJTBI
incubation support to the tune of Rs 31.4 Lakhs to incubate 5 Ideas.
2) An approval has been received from EDI, Ahmadabad under the scheme
of DST/NIMA Project to conduct one Entrepreneurship Awareness Camp
(EAC) in our institution.
3) Sri. K.Kodanda Ram, Chief Coordinator, ED Cell conducted a meeting
with Department ED Cell Coordinators and Project Coordinators on
5-12-2015 between 3 PM to 4 PM to stream line My Big Idea Competition
and other Entrepreneurship related activities Sri B.D.V Chandra Mohan
from Civil Engineering Department & Sri G.Prabhakar from EEE
Department have attended.
4) Sri. K.Kodanda Ram, Chief Coordinator, ED Cell interacted with students
and presented ppt on idea generation ,opportunity identification and
startups on 11-12-02015 between 2.30 PM to 3.30 PM in which 18
M.Tech(I-Year) students have participated.
5) Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Business Incubator Manager, VJTBI
and Sri. K.Kodanda Ram, Chief Coordinator, ED Cell have attended
award night of TEP / ISB programme at ISB during 3 PM to 7 PM in which
10 TEP/ISB Students of 2014-2016 batch received their certificates.
6) Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Business Incubator Manager, VJTBI has
attended Research Advisory Board meeting on 21-12-2015 at Board
Room and discussed about MSME approved ideas.
ED Cell, IIPC & MSME VJTBI Coordinators
Dr.A.Subba Rao , Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju & Sri.K.Kodanda Ram
