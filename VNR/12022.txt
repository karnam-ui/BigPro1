A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for
the Month of January,2022.
I) ED Cell Activities:
1) As a part of Career Vision Approach, an expert talk was arranged through
offline by Dr. M.Venkata Ramana, Coordinator of ED Cell to I Year B.Tech I-
Sem CSEML Students on “Entrepreneur in you” on 03-01-2022 at Dr. A.P.J.
Abdul Kalam Auditorium from 1.40 PM to 2.40 PM. In which 54 students and
One Faculty have participated. Dr. Harshavardhan Awari, Senior Assistant
Professor, CSE (AML and IoT) has coordinated the activity.
An interaction with CSEML students by Dr.M.Venkata Ramana on 03-01-2022
2) A review on preparation of ppt for presentation to be given to DST on approval
of sanction of NIDHI TBI to the institution through Google Meet on 18-01-2022
from 2 PM to 3.30 PM with Dr.Chenna Kesava Rao, Director , Administration ,
Dr.A.Subhananda Rao, Director, Research & Consultancy , Dr.Y.Padmasai,
Principal Investigator, Dr.C.Kiran, Co - Principal Investigator, Dr.G.Ramesh
Chandra , HOD, RCC and Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Coordinator, ED Cell.
3) Dr.M.Venkata Ramana and Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Coordinators of ED
Cell have prepared & submitted data related to ED Cell activities for
preparation of Annual Quality Assurance Report (AQAR) - (2020-2021) to
be submitted to NAAC through the Institution portal by IQAC and ACC of our
Institute in the form of Excel Sheet on 07-01-2022.
4) Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju has registered and attended a virtual session
through CISCO Webex platform on “OMICRON & COVID-19 in 2022” between
4 PM to 5.30 PM on 20/01/2022. Opening remarks by Ms. Swathi K, Session
Chair and Convenor, CSR Panel, CII Telangana & Head- NATCO Trust by
NATCO Pharma Ltd. Dr Sai Praveen Haranath, Senior Pulmonologist, Apollo
Hospitals and Dr Vishal Kumar Chitkeshi, Pulmonologist, KIMS Hospitals have
addressed the session as keynote speakers. This session was organized by
CII Telangana in collaboration Department of Collegiate & Technical
Education, Government of Telangana.
5) As a part of Career Vision Approach (CVA) an expert talk was arranged
through online MS Teams Plat Form by Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju,
Coordinator of ED Cell to I Year B.Tech I-Sem CSE Students of Sections A &
D on “The Role of Entrepreneurs in the Economic Development of any Nation
and Entrepreneurship Initiatives of VNRVJIET ED Cell” on 22-01-2022 from
1.40 PM to 2.40 PM, in which 95 students and One Faculty have participated.
Mr.Shaik Abdul Hameed, Assistant Professor ,CSE has coordinated the
activity.
An Expert Talk by Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju through MS Teams on 22-01-2022.

present in 61st FPDC meeting on 31-01-2022.
