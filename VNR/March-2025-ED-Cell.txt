A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for
the Month of March 2025
I) ED Cell Activities:

Workshop on Process of Establishment (Startup) on 01.03.2025. Mr. Abdul Khadhar,
Dy. Manager, NSIC, Hyderabad and Mr. B. Vihar, CA, Prayagh Consumer Care Pvt.
Ltd., Hyderabad delivered a talk on Guide to Business Incorporation, Start up funding
schemes, Registration of Startups. 35 students from different programs have attended
the program.
Workshop on Process of Establishment (Startup) on 01.03.2025
