ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT
In 2021, we broadened our horizons by introducing the B.Tech programme in Al & DS. This
addition brings a new dimension to our academic offerings, with an intake of 60 students. The
department is meticulously designed to delve deep into the captivating realms of Artificial
Intelligence and Data Science. This programme fosters a holistic learning environment, equipping
students for impactful contributions in the dynamic field of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science.
VISION
To produce engineers with in-depth technical knowledge and ethical values.
To incorporate ingeniousness and self-driven capabilities in the students in the fields of
mezzanine technologies.
To nurture the ecosystem of Innovation, Research and Development through an integrated
learning environment for faculty and students.
MISSION
Offer a diverse curriculum in line with industry, professional, and research bodies.
Provide a project-based learning environment for developing diverse practical capabilities.
Offer advanced trainings to enable process skills and competencies.

OUTREACH ACTIVITIES
The Department has successfully conducted multiple outreach activities in the adopted village, Peddachinthakunta. These
included formalities for village adoption, initial discussions with the villagers, a thorough village survey, and awareness
programs for school students. A dental camp was organized, benefiting 159 individuals, including students and staff.
Additionally, a visit to a nearby old age home was a heart-touching experience, fostering meaningful interactions and
bringing joy to the elderly residents. These initiatives aimed to enhance health, education, and social well-being within the
community.
