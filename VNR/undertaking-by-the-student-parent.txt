VIGNANA JYOTHI HOSTEL
UNDERTAKING BY THE STUDENT & PARENT/GUARDIAN
1. I have read and will follow the VJ Hostel Rules and Regulations.
2. I agree to never damage the allotment room and the hostel.
3. I will not harm the hostel's furnishings, electrical systems, or fixtures.
4. I will not engage in ragging or other indiscipline-related or unlawful actions.
5. I will not bring, keep, or consume any forbidden substances or goods.
6. I shall lock my almarah and turn off lights and fans when leaving.
7. Taking mess food outside the dining hall is prohibited and may result in disciplinary
action.
8. I will not take away any crockery, utensils, or any item from the mess, kitchen, or office
space.
9. I will not keep costly items (e.g. jewelry) in the hostel room, and the VJ Authority is not
liable for their loss.
10. Men are not allowed to visit female hostel residents, and women are not allowed to visit
male hostel residents.
11. Unauthorized individuals, including parents, siblings, friends, guests, and previous
students not on the college's roll, are not permitted to stay in my hostel room under any
circumstances.
12. I will not obstruct the hostel's entrance or hallways, and will provide barrier-free access
for those with disabilities. I will not entertain with my personal or friends' vehicles. VJ
authorities are not liable for any injuries/ accidents.
13. I shall only utilize hostel transport and adhere to hostel hours (before 5.30 PM).
14. I will not keep or feed any animals (dogs, cats, etc.) inside the hostel.
15. I shall not inconvenience my roommate.
16. I agree not to use any forbidden equipment, gadgets, or appliances in the VJ hostel.
17. I agree to pay fees on a regular basis by the deadline. Failure to do so may result in
penalties.
18. I am responsible for maintaining the cleanliness of my room and hostel.
19. I will vacate and officially hand over the allotted hostel room/Rack keys during the
vacation unless authorized to stay back.
20. Upon completion of the approved stay, I shall hand over my room keys and sign the
vacation register. Furthermore, if I fail to register for a semester by the deadline, I shall
vacate the hostel. Hostel Fee is not refundable.
21. I shall carry my VJ Hostel identity card/semester registration card and show it to any VJ
Hostel official upon request.
Signature of the Student with date
Signature of the Parent/Guardian with date
Verified &Signed by Hostel In-charge
Read all the points carefully. Ink-sign the undertaking and submit the original in the Office of
the Warden at the time of joining the allotted hostel.
