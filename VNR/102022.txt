A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for
the Month of October, 2022.
I) ED Cell Activities:
1) Dr. S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Co-Ordinator o f ED Cell has Registered and successfully
completed 8 Weeks NPTEL-AICTE Faculty Development Programme
(FDP) and a Certificate Course on “Entrepreneurship and IP Strategy”
during July-September, 2022 with a consolidated Score of 86% and stood
topper of the course, organized by IIT, Madras. Received Certificates on
12-10-2022.
2) Dr.M.Venkata Ramana, In charge ED Cell has attended a webinar through
online platform on “Early Stage of Entrepreneurship and Innovation" on
15th October, 2022 from 11:00 AM organized by Department of Computer
Science and Engineering, GAPCRUD Private Limited in association with IIC,
JIS University.
3) Internal Quality Assurance & Academic Audit Cell expert team of two
members have visited ED Cell office on 22-10-2022, verified all the files and
expressed their satisfaction during the exit meeting on the same day.
4) ED Cell Department Faculty Coordinators and Student body ED Cell
coordinators meeting was arranged by Dr.M.Venkata Ramana and
Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Co-Ordinator’s of ED Cell on 25-10-2022 at P.G.
Block Seminar Hall with an agenda of organizing My Big Idea
Competition(MBIC), Students Induction Programme (SIP) and launching of
promotional activities to participate in MSME Idea Hackathon-2(2022) in the
next month.
ED Cell Faculty and Students Coordinators Meet on 25-10-2022 at PG Block Seminar Hall
II) MSME & VJTBI Activities:
1) Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Manger, MSME Business Incubator and
Dr. M.Venkata Ramana Coordinator of Entrepreneurship Development Cell
