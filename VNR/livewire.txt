Livewire
Livewire crew is an all style collegiative dance crew from VNR VJIET that seeks to promote all the hip hop and western dance styles.They perform on various genres of music and represent the vintage as well as the modern culture representing our Telugu states .They have been very dedicated and loyal to dance since the year of establishment. They have been constantly setting the bars higher year by year. Livewire has brought VNR not only a level of dignity in the field of dance but the respect that is nothing less than any other University . Livewire is the most active club that never steps back from bringing a change in the society with their creative and thoughtful performances .
