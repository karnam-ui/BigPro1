ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT
The Department of Automobile Engineering started its undergraduate program in 2010,
accredited by the National Board of Accreditation. With a dedicated and experienced faculty,
the department keeps about with the latest developments in the field. It provides best-in-class
facilities, modern laboratories, a well-furnished seminar hall, and a library with a diverse
collection. The department fosters strong ties with industries through faculty research and
collaborative projects. Specializing in industry training and addressing relevant problems, the
program stands out. Faculty members consistently publish research findings in journals,
conferences, and patents. The department trains students to excel in the competitive field of
automobile engineering, addressing technological challenges and societal needs.
VISION
To become and be one of the elite technical institutes acclaimed by the peers and industry with a
world-class technical education, a contemporary teaching facility, and state-of-the-art
laboratories to suit global standards.
ABOUT THE PROGRAMME
Automobile Engineering / Automotive Engineering / Vehicle Engineering is one of the most
challenging careers in the field of engineering. This discipline offers a wide scope, blending the
principles of various engineering fields to design and manufacture automobiles. It integrates
aspects of mechanical, electrical, electronic, software, and safety engineering to create vehicles
that are not only functional and efficient but also safe and innovative.
ABOUT FACULTY
The department's faculty, a key strength, is committed to high-quality teaching and research with
practical values. With 20 members, including 9 doctorates, possessing diverse knowledge, the
faculty consistently upgrades academic standards and research expertise to align with the latest
technological developments. Encouraging lateral thinking, they promote collaborative projects
with industry. The faculty is easily accessible, fostering close student-teacher relationships.
CAREER
The career opportunities in this field span automobile manufacturing, production plants, service
stations, state road transport corporations, private transport companies, insurance companies,
and motor vehicle departments. Automobile engineers, equipped with computer knowledge in
CAD, CAM, ERP, and programming, can find employment in computer-aided industries or
research and development wings of companies. The growth of the automobile industry globally
has created abundant opportunities for engineers in India and abroad. Graduates can serve
top-notch automobile and IT companies.
