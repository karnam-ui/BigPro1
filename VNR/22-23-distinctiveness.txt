"Building Balanced Leaders: VNR VJIET’s Commitment to Emotional,
Professional, and Social Growth"
In an ever-evolving academic landscape, VNR VJIET stands out with its
professional society chapters and student clubs, making a beacon of holistic
development. Clubs like Art of Living ( AoL), IUCEE ( Indo Universal Collaboration for
Engineering Education) , and Wall of Kindness are introduced in 2022-23 along with
existing active other clubs.
The "Art of Living" club promotes happiness, mental well-being and stress
management through various events, viz., workshops and meditation sessions,
integrating a sense of calm and balance within the student community.
Meanwhile, the IUCEE (Indo Universal Collaboration for Engineering Education)
student chapter with an objective to empower and enrich students with the necessary
tools to develop and grow as professionals and leaders. It enables programs that
enhance the holistic development and experiences. It plugs the students into the IUCEE
Foundation eco-system to learn and share with association and activities.
The Wall of Kindness is a heartwarming initiative that underscores the values of
empathy and social responsibility. This unique club activity encourages students to
donate items for those in need, fostering a culture of generosity and community support.
These clubs are not just extracurriculars—they are integral to VNR VJIET’s identity,
empowering students with skills that transcend academic knowledge. These experiences
shape individuals who are not only career-ready but also emotionally intelligent and
socially conscious.
