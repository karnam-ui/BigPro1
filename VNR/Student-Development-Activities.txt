Professional Societies/ Student Clubs activities
1. Stentorian: The English Literary Club of VNRVJIET
Stentorian, the English Literary Club of VNRVJIET has a focus on creativity, good linguistic ability, and a liking
for literature among students. It was started in the year 2012, to give the students a way to pursue their love for
language and literature, while training them in the areas of communication as well as critical thinking abilities.
It has been named Stentorian to stand for a loud and powerful voice. It symbolizes that the club aims to empower
students with confidence. Since the club's establishment, Stentorian has become a hub for all literary enthusiasts
to gather and share ideas. It has also served as a prominent forum for students to express their skills in spoken
arts, showcase their flair for the English language, and stand for impressive communication skills that move
beyond the general spoken manner.
The events and activities conducted by Stentorian are meticulously planned and organised, selected from the
various interests and active involvement of club members. Notable events organised by Stentorian include:
1. Stentorian’s Debate Society: A platform for interactive debates on controversial topics, which provides an
opportunity for spoken word expression, thus paving a way for everyone on campus to ponder over topics of
interest to the student body at VNRVJIET.
2. Open Mic Shack: A recurring event that is conducted once a month to provide the students with an open-to-all
mic shack, where they get a chance to express themselves in the presence of an audience, without restrictions to
the medium they choose to communicate in, be it their own composed works such as poetry, stories, stand-up
comedies, and musical presentations or more.
3. Strident: Stentorian’s very own literature magazine, showcasing the creative prowess of students at VNRVJIET.
It features a rich collection of poetry, stories, essays, and artwork, reflecting the vibrant literary spirit of the club.
4. VJMUNSoc: As a wing under the club, VJ Model United Nations Society has been dedicated to fostering
diplomatic skills and global awareness among students. It organizes simulations of UN proceedings, encouraging
critical thinking, public speaking, and collaborative problem-solving.
5. Literary Quizzes: A fun and unique experience that fosters the competitive spirit of students, quizzes on a wide
array of topics cover knowledge about various literary books, grammar, and vocabulary to equip students with
information on a bulk of different things.
6. Themed Events: Special events like book reading, collaborative writing, and reading challenges are regularly
hosted to help exchange literary ideas among members.
Stentorian has been an active contributor to the growth of intellectual engagement and artistic exploration on the
campus. The club activities are designed to offer experiences that will foster the intellectual and professional
development of the students so that they can be the pillars of literary life at VNRVJIET.
