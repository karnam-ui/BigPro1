DATE: 31/03/2015
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & Vignana Jyothi Technology Business
Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the Month of March, 2015
1) E-Week celebrated during 2nd & 3rd of March, 2015 as a part of Convergence 2K15 at
VNRVJIET. During this programme several activities like Stock Pulse, B-Quiz, Street Play
and B-Simulation were conducted in which 150 students of VNRVJIET have participated. The
Prizes for these programmes were given by Dr.A.Subba Rao Garu, Director, VNRVJIET on
19-03-2015 at Amphi theatre.
2) A workshop on “Design Challenge” for TEP/ISB students of VNRVJIET and
BVRIT,Narsapur was held by Akshay Jalan ,Academic Associate and Kritika Mathur
Prasad, Academic Associate (TEP/ISB Faculty) on 23-03-2015 at Yogaiah Naidu
Bhavan Seminar Hall (D-304) in association with Department of Mechanical
Engineering, VNRVJIET between 10 AM to 4.00PM with a break of one Hour between
1 PM to 2 PM. In which 36 students of VNRVJIET and 17 Students of BVRIT (Total-53
Students) have participated.
3) Six TEP / ISB Groups have been identified on 27-03-2015 and allotted with Project
Titles to proceed with “Design Challenge”.
4) Received 8 applications for MSME Business Incubation support for initial screening.
5) Open house projects are exhibited to the students of VNRVJIET during 3rd & 4th of
March, 2015.
ED Cell & MSME VJTBI Coordinators
Sri.K.Kodanda Ram & Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju
