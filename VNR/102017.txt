Date: 31/10/2017
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell, IIPC & Vigna na Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of October, 2017
1) ED Cell and Department of CSE have jointly organized a two day Workshop cum
National Level Championship in association with IIT- Kharagpur entitled “UTTKRAANTI “
during 12 th and 13th October,2017 on Big Data and Cloud Computing at Dr. A.P.J
Abdul Kalam Auditorium, VNRVJIET. In which 63 students of CSE and ECE have
participated.
A group Photograph of the Participants of “UTTKRAANTI” on 13-10-2017
2) Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Manager, MSME Business Incubator and Sri.K.Kodanda
Ram, Chief Coordinator, ED Cell have participated in a one day conference organized by
Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) in partnership with Government of Telangana, the
13th edition of its flagship Conference, Man’Exe-2017 with the theme “Make in
Telangana : Creating a Sustainable & Globally Competitive Ecosystem” on
13 October 2017 at Hotel Taj Krishna, Hyderabad.
3) As a part of Career Vision programme Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Coordinator, ED Cell
has addressed I- B.Tech CSE-3 students about "Entrepreneur in You" on 23-10-2017
from 3 PM to 4PM at Room No:B-008, in which 53 students have attended.
