Chairman welcomed all the members to the 14th meeting of Academic Council held
online through Google Meet platform on July 31st, 2021 at VNRVJIET.
Chairman introduced the newly appointed University nominees – , Dr. M. Manzoor
Hussain, Registrar, JNTUH, Dr. D. Srinivasa Rao, Director, Academic Audit Cell, JNTUH
and Dr. Manjula Vani K. Professor of Civil Engineering, JNTUH to the members of the
Council. Other members of the council introduced themselves to the University
nominees.
Thereafter, formal agenda items were taken up and the following matters were
considered, deliberated upon and decisions taken are as under:

14.4 Revised paper pattern and evaluation criteria (One-time) for the Semester End
Examinations of B.Tech. and M.Tech. programmes under R18 and R19 academic
regulations in the academic year 2020-2021 due to COVID-19 pandemic.
➢ With reference to the JNTUH issued guidelines, revised question paper pattern and
evaluation criteria (One-time) for the Semester End Examinations of B.Tech. and
M.Tech. programmes under R18 and R19 academic regulations in the academic
year 2020-2021 due to COVID-19 pandemic as indicated in Annexure-IV was
presented before the committee.
➢ Council approved the revised paper pattern and evaluation criteria (One-time)
for the Semester End Examinations of B.Tech. and M.Tech. programmes under R18
and R19 academic regulations in the academic year 2020-2021.
14.5 Approval of promotion guidelines of B.Tech. I, II, III & IV year and M.Tech. I & II
year students in the academic year 2020-2021 due to COVID-19 pandemic.
➢ In line with the JNTUH issued notifications dated 15.02.2021 for odd semester and
24.05.2021 for even semester, the following promotion guidelines were proposed
for approval before the Council:
• No attendance based detentions for odd semesters.
• No attendance based detentions for even semesters. However, if the
college desires to detain any students (due to non-attending of online
classes and not appeared for mid-exams) the hall-tickets of such students
need to be uploaded using the corresponding registration portals.
➢ Council approved the promotion guidelines of B.Tech. I, II, III & IV year and M.Tech.
I & II year students in the academic year 2020-2021 due to COVID-19 pandemic.

14.10 Any other matter.
14.10.1 Approval of the academic calendars of B.Tech. I, II, III and IV year
programmes and M.Tech. I and II year programmes in the academic year 2020-2021:
➢ Academic calendars of B.Tech. I, II, III and IV year programmes and M.Tech. I and
II year programmes as well as revised academic calendars due to COVID-19
pandemic in the academic year 2020-2021 (Annexure-VII) were presented before
the Council for approval.
➢ Council approved the academic calendars to be followed for B.Tech. I, II, III and
IV year programmes and M.Tech. I and II year programmes in the academic year
2020-2021.
14.10.2 Examination and Evaluation: Approval of
a) Moderation Norms
b) Revaluation Norms
c) Permission to conduct special mid exam:
➢ Moderation Norms:
• Moderation Norms (Annexure-VIII) as stipulated by the JNT University,
Hyderabad, vide ref No U.O. No. JNTUH/95/2012 dated 15-05-2012 for
moderation of external examination marks before the declaration of the results
were presented before the Council for approval.
• Council approved the moderation norms to be followed at VNRVJIET.
➢ Revaluation Norms:
• Revaluation Norms (Annexure-VIII) as recommended by JNT University
Hyderabad dated 23-07-2012 were presented before the Council for approval.
• Council approved the revaluation norms to be followed at VNRVJIET. Council
further resolved that the same norms of revaluation shall be applicable to
challenge valuation also.
➢ Permission to conduct special mid exam:
• Multiple requests were received from B.Tech. students of academic year 2019-
2020 who were promoted to the next semester in the academic year 2020-2021
in spite of not attending classes and mid exams due to COVID-19 pandemic.
