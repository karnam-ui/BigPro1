A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for
the Month of September,2021.
I) ED Cell Activities:
1) Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju has registered and attended an online CII IGBC
Web seminar through Cisco Webex Events on “Shaping the Hospitality
Industry towards Health, Well-being, Air circulation against Covid-19”
on 08/09/2021 between 03.30 PM to 05.30 PM organized by CII Godrej
GBC.
2) Dr.M.Venkata Ramana and Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju Coordinators of
Entrepreneurship Development Cell(ED Cell) have attended an online “CII
TS-PACKCON 2021” Web conference through Cisco Webex Events on
“Sustainable Packaging Solutions for Future Demands” with a theme of
“Global Trends on Innovation and Marketing in packaging Industry-
Asthetics Vis a Vis Functionality” and “Beyond Covid-19: The new
normal for packaging Industry: Challenges and opportunities” by Mr
Gaurav Maheswari,MD, Signod India, Group President, Signode Asia
Pacific, Mr Sanjay Singh, Past Chairman, CII Telangana & Group Head-
paper & Packaging ITC Limited and Inaugural address by Adhar Sinha,
IAS Special Chief Secretary to Government and Director General,
Environment Protection Training & Research Institute (EPTRI),
Government of Telangana on 29/09/2021 between 10.00 AM to 01.30 PM
organized by CII Telangana, Hyderabad.
3) Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju and Dr.M.Venkata Ramana have attended an
on line “Jarsh Safety International Virtual Signing Ceremony of
strategic tie-up with NIA Limited for GCC and Africa” on 30-09-2021
between 12 Noon to 1.30 PM. Mr. Jayesh Ranjan (IAS), Principal
Secretary, TS Government is the Keynote Speaker and Chief Guest at the
event. Dr.Agit Rangnekar, Director general of RICH & former Dean of ISB,
Hyderabad and Dr.D.N.Rao, President Vignana Jyothi Educational Society
& Chief Promoter of Jarsh Innovations Pvt.Ltd as distinguished speakers
of this Virtual International Collaboration Signing event.
