DEPARTMENT OF CSE - INTERNET OF THINGS
ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT
The Department of CSE- Internet of Things (IoT) stands at the forefront of innovation, shaping a
future that transcends boundaries and revolutionizes the way to interact with technology. The
Department of IoT started in the year 2020, offers one UG course namely B. Tech-CSE (IoT) with
an intake of 60 students.The department also offers Minor programme in IoT for the circuit and
non - circuit branches.
VISION
To emerge as an epicenter having a transformative impact in the education and research of
Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning and Internet of Things with an ecosystem that
contributes to society through innovativeness and creativity
MISSION
To impart innovative teaching and learning methodologies to generate knowledge through
the state-of-the-art concepts and technologies in AI-ML and IoT with personal and professional
responsibilities and commitment to lifelong learning with societal aspirations.
To produce successful Computer Science and Engineering graduates with a specialization in
AI-ML and IoT.
To establish centers of excellence in leading areas of AI - ML and IoT in collaboration with
industry to uplift innovative research and development.

STUDENT PROFESSIONAL CHAPTERS:
Internet Society:
Student Professional Chapter with the Internet Society was officially inaugurated on 1st February
2025, marking the beginning of an exciting journey towards innovation, connectivity, and digital
empowerment. The Internet Society supports and promotes the development of the Internet as a global
technical infrastructure, a resource to enrich people’s lives, and a force for good in society. The work
aligns with the goals for the Internet to be open, globally connected, secure, and trustworthy.
Vision:
The Internet Is for Everyone.
Mission:
Building and supporting the communities that make the Internet work.
Advancing the development and application of Internet infrastructure, technologies, and open standards.
Advocating for policy that is consistent with our view of the Internet.
IEEE CIS:
The IEEE Computational Intelligence Society (CIS) Chapter at Vallurupalli Nageswara Rao Vignana
Jyothi Institute of Engineering and Technology (VNRVJIET) was inaugurated on 8th February 2025,
with the objective of fostering collaboration, facilitating learning, and encouraging professional
growth in the field of Computational Intelligence for both students and faculty.
Vision:
To cultivate a culture of excellence in computational intelligence through continuous learning, research, and
collaboration, empowering students to become global leaders in the field.
Mission:
To facilitate the dissemination of knowledge in computational intelligence through workshops, seminars, and
technical talks.
To provide a platform for students to engage in hands-on projects, research initiatives, and collaborative
learning.
To encourage participation in IEEE-sponsored events and competitions at regional, national, and international
levels.
To connect students with industry professionals and academicians through networking opportunities.
