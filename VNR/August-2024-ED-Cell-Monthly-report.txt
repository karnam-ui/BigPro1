A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME
Vignana Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI)
Activities for the Month of August 2024
I) ED Cell Activities:
1. A meeting was conducted with Head, IIE and Department ED Cell Department
faculty coordinators and student members on 08.08.2024 with an agenda of ED
Cell activities to be organized from August 2024 to December 2024.
2. Entrepreneurship Development Cell (ED Cell) in Association with SAE Collegiate
Club of VNRVJIET organized a One Day Workshop on Process for Establishment
of Industry (Micro, Small and Medium) on 16.08.2024 for B.Tech. final year
students from 10.00 AM to 04.30 PM. 123 students from different B.Tech.
programs attended the workshop. Mr. Abdul Khader P, Dy. Manager (Tech),
National Small Industries Corporation (A Government of India Enterprise),
Hyderabad delivered a talk on the importance of entrepreneurship, demonstration
on Udyam registration. Mr. G Vasu, Motivational Speaker enlightens the students
with the journey of successful entrepreneurs.
3. Entrepreneurship Development Cell (ED Cell) in Association with IIC, VJ-
Community and ASME, VNRVJIET organized World Entrepreneurship Day on
21.08.2024 for all B.Tech and M.Tech students from 02.00 PM to 04.40 PM at
KS Auditorium. 402 students from different B.Tech. programs attended the event.
Mr. M. Krishna Prasad, Head, IIE created the awareness and importance of
Entrepreneurship on the national economy. As part of the Startup Problem Hunt,
Small problems were given to students to identify the solutions.
4. Entrepreneurship Development Cell (ED Cell) in Association with VJ
Community has given orientation on ED Cell and its activities to I B.Tech. students
on 30 - 31 August 2024 from 01.00 PM to 02.00 PM at KS Auditorium and D 402.
246 B.Tech. students from AE, ME, CE, IT and ECE attended the session.
II) MSME & VJTBI Activities:
1. Development of the Two Ideas under MSME Hackathon 2.0 is under progress.
