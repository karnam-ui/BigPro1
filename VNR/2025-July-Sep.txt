EDITOR’S DESK
Greetings to Faculty, Students and Friends!
I am very happy to provide this edition of our Departmental News Letter. First and foremost,
I want to thank the Lord for leading and motivating each and every one of us to strive for
excellence. I want to express my gratitude to everyone in the department for their direct or
indirect contributions. Sincere gratitude to the department head, director, principal, and
management for their helpful assistance and cooperation in all departmental activities. This
News Letter covers the various departmental activities begin with FDP, Student
achievements, Faculty achievements, Guest Lectures, Industrial visits, Faculty research
contributions during July- September 2025. I am honoured to acknowledge the contributions
made towards the completion of this issue by my students, illustrious alumni, and my
academic colleagues. I anticipate continuing to receive the same level of support. We want to
set up the new editorial board in the following semester. Students, alumni, and faculty
members who are interested may contribute. See you in the next Edition.
Thanks & Warm regards
Dr. Sujith Bobba
Assistant Professor

MESSAGE FROM HEAD OF THE DEPARTMENT
Dear Faculty and Student Friends,
I am happy to inform you that our department has organised one week faculty development
program on “Hands-on Experience in AI, ML, and Python for Core Engineering
Applications. The department routinely hosts a variety of professional development events
and provides a personality development program to help students grow. Students compete in
a variety of intra- and inter-collegiate sports and co-curricular activities at the institute level.
Our students have their own clubs such as ASME, ISTE, ISIE and have received numerous
awards for their participation in different events. The department consistently strives to foster
students' overall development and instill the traits and attributes that society demands and
accepts. Students and faculty take personal initiative for social causes.
The department's main goal is to transform students into capable and decent citizens.
With best wishes
Dr. B.V.R. Ravi Kumar
Head of Mechanical Engineering Department

Departmental Achievements:
1. The Department of Mechanical Engineering organized a One week faculty development
program on “Hands-on Experience in AI, ML, and Python for Core Engineering
Applications,” organized by the Department of Mechanical Engineering in association
with the Departments of CSE (CyS, DS) and AI&amp;DS from 7th – 11th July 2025.
2. The Department of Mechanical Engineering organized a guest lecture on 12th September
2025 for II B.Tech. students on the topic “Role of Biomimetics in Society for Sustainable
Development,” delivered by Dr. Jitendra Kumar Katiyar, Associate Professor,
Department of Mechanical & Industrial Engineering, Manipal Institute of Technology,
Manipal, India.

Drones Academy, successfully conducted a DGCA-certified ‘Workshop on Drone
Technology – Learn to Fly Drones’ on 25th September 2025 at with 51 students actively
participating. “Resource Person was Sumit Krishnan, DGCA-certified RPA instructor at
India Drone Academy,
4. 18th BOS meeting for ME was held on 9th September 2025 at 10:00 AM in the department
through Hybrid mode (Online and Offline mode).

accompanied by 4 Faculty members – Dr. S. Bahnu Murthy (Assistant Professor, ME) ,
Dr. G. V. Subhash (Assistant Professor, ME), Dr. S. V. Satya Prasad (Assistant Professor,
ME) & Smt. Ch. Rajeswari (Assistant Professor, ME) – visited the KGN Industries,
Sultanpur, Hyderabad.
