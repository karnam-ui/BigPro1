A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of May, 2019
I) ED Cell Activities:
1) Dr. Chenna Kesava Rao, Director for Advancement, had represented on behalf the
Management of VNRVJIET the launch event of TiE GRAD 2019-20 on 04-05-2019 at
Club Botanica ,Gachibowli ,Hyderabad.
2) The following students have been shortlisted by The Indus Entrepreneurs (TiE) to
attend a 4-day residential programme “TiE Grad BizHack Bootcamp” to be held at
Vardhaman College of Engineering, Shamshabad, Hyderabad, during May
30-05-2019 to 02-06-2019.The students will be solving real- world problems with case
studies, and the programme also deals with Business Strategy, Financial Planning,
Logistics, Supply Chain Management and related knowledge. Over 30 students have
applied to the programme through online and 5 students per college has been
shortlisted by TiE. This programme was monitored by Dr.C.Kiran,Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa
Raju and Sri.K.Kodanda Rarm.
