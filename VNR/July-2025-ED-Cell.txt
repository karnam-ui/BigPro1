A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for
the Month of July 2025
I. ED Cell Activities:
1. M. Venkata Ramana, i/c ED Cell attended an online Session on Mobilising IICs &
Leveraging MIC Schemes on 02.07.2025 from 03.00 PM to 04.00 PM. Dr. Abhay
Jere, VCM, AICTE & CIO, MIC informed the various schemes under MIC.
2. Meeting is conducted with all the Department ED Cell faculty Coordinators on
11.07.2025 from 09.30 AM to 10.15 AM at Department Library (D-400). The ED
Cell activities for A.Y. 2025-26, Proposal for MSME Hackathon 5.0 and Motivation
of students to participate in ED Cell events were discussed.
3. Entrepreneurship Development Cell (ED Cell) has organized a Sensitization
Program on MSME Hackathon 5.0 on 17.07.2025 from 10.00 AM to 12.00 Noon
Mr. K.C. Choudhury, IEDA (R), Ex-Assistant Director Grade-1, Ministry of MSME,
Hyderabad has been invited as a guest. Mr. Choudhury provided the inputs to
prepare the idea proposal for MSME Hackathon 5.0 and interacted with faculty and
students. 169 students and 09 faculty from different programs attended the event.

II. MSME & VJTBI Activities:
1. Development of the Two Ideas under MSME Hackathon 2.0 are under progress.
(Dr. K. Ajay Kumar/ MED and Dr. D. Ramesh Reddy/ECE).
2. Development of the One Idea under MSME Hackathon 3.0 is under progress. (Mrs.
