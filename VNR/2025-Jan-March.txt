EDITOR’S DESK
Greetings to Faculty, Students and Friends!
It gives me immense pleasure to bringing out this issue of our Departmental News Letter.
First of all, I would like to express gratitude to Lord for guiding and inspiring each and every
one of us do our best. I would like to thank each and every one in the department for their
contribution, either directly or indirectly. Due regards to Management, Principal, Director
and Head of the department for their kind co-operation and support in every activity of the
department. This News Letter covers the various departmental activities begin with FDP,
Student achievements, Faculty achievements, Guest Lectures, Industrial visits, Faculty
research contributions during Jan- March 2025. I am honoured to acknowledge the
contributions made towards the completion of this issue by my students, illustrious alumni,
and my academic colleagues. I anticipate continuing to receive the same level of support. We
want to set up the new editorial board in the following semester. Students, alumni, and
faculty members who are interested may contribute. See you in the next Edition.
Thanks & Warm regards
Dr. Sujith Bobba
Assistant Professor

Departmental Achievements:
1. Centre for Composite Materials, Department of Mechanical Engineering has organised
workshop on “ABAQUS” (Finite Element software) in association with Institute of
Engineers (Hyderabad Section) for faculty skill enhancement from 6th to 10th January
2025.
2. Mr. B. Kalyan Ram from Indxo AI Pvt. Ltd., Mr. Arindam Lahiri from ASDC, and Mr.
Aniruddha Kadkol from PwC, India, visited the Mechanical Engineering Labs on 9th
January 2025."
3. The Department of Mechanical Engineering organized a Guest Lecture on "Waste to
Energy" for III B.Tech. students by Mr M.V Chary, President-Operations and Projects,
Jindal Urban Waste Management Limited, along with his Team on 25.01.2025
4. The Department of Mechanical Engineering is organized a Guest Lecture on " Additive
Manufacturing Applications & Case studies in Dentistry" on 5th February 2025 by Dr.
Revath Vyas Devulapalli, Dentist, Oral Medicine and Radiology, DSR Multispeciality
Dental Hospital, Opposite N7 Hotel, On Karmanghat to Champapet road, Beside Krishna
Sai Hospital, Hyderabad
5. The Department of Mechanical Engineering organized a Guest Lecture on “Basics of
reliability testing” by Mr. Kenji Suzuki, Chief Representative Officer, Espec Engineering
(Thailand) Co. Ltd., Thailand, on 17th February 2025 at Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam
Auditorium, with a total of 96 II B.Tech. students attended the guest lecture.
6. Dr. G. Naga Srinivasulu, Professor, Mechanical Engineering, NITW, Warangal, visited
the Hydrogen Fuel Cell Electric Bike startup, provided valuable inputs, and engaged in
discussions.
7. Bluestar company employees were trained at the VNR VJIET on February 12, 2025. The
employees practiced technical skills in hands-on experience, where they could apply
theoretical knowledge in real-world situations. The VNR VJIET could provide the
necessary equipment and expertise for this training.
8. The Department of Mechanical Engineering organized a Faculty Development Program
Empowering Educators with SolidWorks&quot; during 4 th to 8 th March 2025 at PDP
Lab (D-313), with atotal of 40 faculty ( ME:34, AE:4, CE:2) attended the Faculty
Development Program.
9. The Department of Mechanical Engineering organized a Guest Lecture on “overview of
aircraft and crash dynamics” by Mr. Arjun Koustubhan K.S., Senior Principal Engineer &
Mr. Sambhasiva Kodati, Principal Engineer, Collins Aerospace, HITEC City Phase-II,
Hyderabad. on 24th March 2025 at Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Auditorium, with a total of 86
II B.Tech. students and 10 students attended the guest lecture.
10. Dr. Pankaj Srivastava, Scientist F, IRDE, Dehradun, visited the Mechanical Engineering
labs on 27-03-2025 at 10:00 AM, exploring Additive Manufacturing, Composite
Fabrication, Automation and Robotics and CAM labs.
