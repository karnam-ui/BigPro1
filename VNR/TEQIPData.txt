Need Analysis (TNA):
 Faculty Development Programmes:
o Organized several workshops, Training Programmes in House as well
as deputing the faculty to outside at the National and International
Level Programmes.
o Support for Foreign Travel: 2 Faculty Members were sponsored for
participation in International Seminars / Conferences with financial
support towards Registration, Travel and Other expenses.
 Staff Development Programmes:
o Organised the several Staff Development Programmes in – House as
well as deputed them to reputed professional training organizations in
the respective domain areas.
o Support for Qualifications improvement and Skills Up gradation for
Teaching and Supporting Staff.
 Enhanced Interaction with Industry:
o Establishment of Institute Industry Interaction (I – I – I) Cell and
organization of several programs in collaboration with industry.
o Deputed Senior Professor to participate in Academia Industry
Interaction organized by CII at Chennai.
o The Institute is being made Member of the CII.
 Institutional Management Capacity Enhancement:
o Several Senior Officers in the Institute viz Principal, Coordinator and
Nodal Officers.
 Implementation of Institutional Academic Reforms:
o Establishment of Academic Council at the Institute level and Boards of
studies at the department levels for introducing academic reforms.
o Establishment of Research Advisory Board with eminent
Academicians, R&D experts and industry personal to undertake
several collaborative Research projects through Research and
Consultancy Cell of this institute.
o Achieved UGC Autonomous status letter no.
o NAAC Accreditation with A Grade and
o NBA Accreditation for all eligible courses.
