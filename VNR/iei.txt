Student chapter IE(I)-Institution of Engineer,(India)
The Institution of Engineers,(India),IE(I) is the national organization of engineers in India. It is the world's largest multi-disciplinary engineering professional society in the engineering and technology world. This chapter helps the students to implement the technical knowledge which they get from guest lectures, workshops and project exhibitions. It enhances the thinking ability and build a leadership quality to pursuit the budding engineers career    growth. Team IEI conducts various activities and events for the students and the biggest of all is the  annual technical fest "EmeRgE ",  which is an alloy of fun and learning. The vision of IEI is to develop into the premier technical and scientific institution, producing distinguished individuals with original thinking, excellent practical skills, and creative minds, and to envision a future where technology enhances humankind and mission of establishing it in our campus is to impart state of the art  knowledge to individuals in various technological disciplines and to inculcate in them a high degree of social consciousness and human values.
