A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell&MSMEVignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of May, 2020
I) ED Cell Activities:
1) Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju has Attended NPTEL : Week 5 on line Lecture series on
“Innovative Idea Generation Using Design Thinking” on 16-05-2020 from 6 PM to 8 PM by
Mr. Dilip Purohit, Director, Capgemini
2) Attended CII TELANGANA DIGITAL SUMMIT Virtual Conference on “Digital Revolution in
Post Covid Era: Touching Lives & Enriching Businesses” on 22 May, 2020 from 10 AM to
5 PM.
3) Attended UML Global Entrepreneurship Webinar on 27-05-2020 from 6.30 PM to 7.30 PM
monitored by Krishna Vedula.
4) Attended an Online Webinar on “Accelerating Engineering Skills to Challenge the COVID-
19 Crises Times” by Dr.B.V.R Mohan Reddy ,Founder and Executive Chairman of Cyient
Ltd, Hyderabad ,organized by Anurag University & Vidya Jyothi Institute of Technology on
30-05-2020 from 11 AM to 12.30 PM.
5) An MoU draft is prepared for working in association with Gram bazaar of Pallesrujana
with VNRVJIET and shared with Dr A. Subhananda Rao, Director-R&D for further
approval. The final draft will be submitted to Dr. D.N.Rao garu for approval duly
incorporating all policy matters after thorough scrutiny of the Draft by the Principal &
Director , VNRVJIET. This includes a proposal for setting up of Rural Incubation Center at
VJ Hub, VNRVJIET.
6) MVGR College of Engineering ,Vizianagaram is one of the designated MSME Business
Incubators would like to associate with VNRVJIET.MVGR College of Engineering seek
to bring together a small group of likeminded innovative institutions for a pilot project with
a possibility of collaborative start up opportunity from AP and Telangana .The interested
students who are planning to work in these pilot projects which are innovative in nature
can work in groups from VNRVJIET,MVGR and teams in the USA with an entrepreneur
coaching program in the USA using small technology development projects. A dialogue is
initiated with MVG R College of Engineering , Vizianagaram in these lines. Sri K.Kodanda
Ram, Chief Cooordinator and Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Coordinator of ED Cell
monitoring the activity.
