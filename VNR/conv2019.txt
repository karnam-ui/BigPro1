CONVERGENCE 2K19
Report
ABOUT:
The National Level Technical Symposium “CONVERGENCE” since its inception in 1999 has been
instrumental in shifting the paradigm of usual coursework and has evolved as an ultimate platform for
the techno-maniacs. This is the 21st year of the legacy of Convergence and new contemporary
themes were added which includes, Hackathons, Designathon, Projects with Societal Impact, Coding
contest, Startup Idea competitions, Patent Idea competitions and Technical Paper presentation.
These events were in addition to Workshops, Poster Presentations, Seminars, Guest lectures and
other technical competitive events.
COMMITTEE:
A committee was constituted for undertaking all required work regarding organizing the technical fest.
The core team (final years) consisted of 72 students and volunteers from third year.
The student chapters involved in preparing for CONVERGENCE-2K19 were ISTE, IEEE, ISOI, CSI,
SAE, VGLUG, VSI and IETE.

Hardware Hackathon (Makeathon):
A hardware hackathon is a design sprint-like event in which electronic designers, coders,
domain experts, programmers, project managers, and others involve in development of a
project. The goal of a hackathon is to create usable hardware with the goal of creating a
functioning product by the end of the event. The project must be completed within 24 hours.
The participants were issued certificates and winners were awarded cash prize and
merit certificates.
Software Hackathon:
A software hackathon is a design sprint-like event in which computer programmers and others
involved in software development, including graphic designers, interface designers, project
managers, and others, often including domain experts, collaborate intensively
on software projects. The project must be completed within 24 hours.
The participants were issued certificates and winners were awarded cash prize and
merit certificates.
Societal Impact / Rural Innovation Projects (Designathon):
The project has to be developed with in 24 hours. The theme may be selected by the
participants. The product can be either software/ hardware or a pragmatic solution to the
problem defined.
The participants were issued certificates and winners were awarded cash prize and
merit certificates.
Coding Contest:
The students can form a team and work on the task given to them within the stipulated time. The
scores of each team will be displayed online. The team that shall complete all the task in shortest time
shall be declared as winner of the competition.
The participants were issued certificates and winners were awarded cash prize and
merit certificates.
Patent Idea Competition:
This event is for the students of VNR VJIET only. The students shall present their ideas in the format
prescribed to the jury which includes attorney and other external & internal experts.
The best four patentable ideas are selected and awarded. Participation certificates were also
issued.
Startup Idea Competition:
The students from all the institutes shall present their idea and business plan for starting their own
enterprise. The expert committee shall scrutinize and award the suitable idea.
The best four Startup ideas were selected and awarded. Participation certificates were also
issued.
.
Technical Paper Presentations:
The students shall present their project in a prescribed format of presentation. Also, the students can
select any latest technological topic and present the same. However, preference shall be given to the
papers based in project, either simulation or prototype model.
The topics were divided into four major domain. Two prizes were awarded under each domain.
Poster Display & Presentations:
The students shall take any topic of their interest. They must prepare a poster explaining the same. It
can be problem or solution to the problem.
Techno Expo:
The students shall exhibit the project developed by them and prepare a poster to showcase
the internal diagram/ block diagram / circuit diagram etc.
Events:
A total of 15 technical competition in addition to the above-mentioned event was conducted. The
students will be given task that has to be accomplished with in stipulated time. The winners of each
event are adjudged and awarded.
Workshops:
The workshop were conducted on niche technological areas. Eight workshops were
organized. The students shall attend the workshop for two days. At the end of the workshop,
certificates signed by conducting industry shall be issued to the participants.

SAARADHI EVENTS:
As a part of Saaradhi team NSS, TEDx VNRVJIET, VNRSF, VJSV(TLC) came the together to set up
various stalls for raising awareness about the various programs being undertaken by various societies
of VNR VJIET. NSS
Here are the events on the three days of SAARADHI theme in CONVERGENCE, 2018 where TEAM
NSS brings out a platform for engineers to be a part of it. We have four events along with stalls.
EVENTS:
1) SOCH: Let’s engineer the tomorrow at “SOCH”. Valuable ideas i.e., technical solutions in the form
of presentations, models, projects that are related to social issues can be presented. The first best
idea would be given a prize of Rs.1500, second best Rs.1000 and third best idea of Rs.500.
STALLS:
NSS GALLERY: We have a VISION and a MISSION for every event which we do. This gallery helps
the audience in knowing the vision and mission of the events which we do and throwing a light on the
solutions for the problems which we are trying to solve.
VJSV(TLC): The VJSV(TLC) has set up a stall under Saaradhi. The stall was named as UNIKI (‘ ’)
which aimed to emphasize the importance of Telugu and Telugu speaking people to the current day
generation.
VNRSF: VNRSF the student force of VNRVJIET took an active part in conducting various events like
social assembly, jagruthi, Praja, Mulam and WOW (Wealth Out of Waste).
JAGRUTHI: Jagruthi, the name depicting - “enlightenment” is an awareness stall. The stall dealtwith
how a nation can develop by developing the individual in various aspects which comesunder a
broader umbrella called Human Development Index (HDI)”. HDI is the measurementof development
of people in the country considering the factors such as Life expectancy, Education and Opportunities
and the GDP and National per capita income. So, here comes therole of an individual in developing
himself, community or the people around him and his state and country individually and collectively.
In the stall the students, faculty and visitors come to know and understand how they can contribute in
raising the factors for HDI. Factors such as Life expectancy can be increased through good health
and protecting and conserving the environment. Good education can be received through the addition
of morals to the intellectuality and abolishing the in equalities among the people based on color,
creed, region, religion and gender. National income canbe achieved through various roles we take as
a responsible citizen and being a good taxpayer. This stall helped the visitors understand how they
can contribute for the development of thecountry and in ranking good in HDI through the examples of
Scandinavian countries whichstands top in Education, Health, Peace, Happiness indices.
WOW: WOW is a platform giving awareness on how waste can be converted into beautiful items and
creates wealth out of it.
Not only showcasing the items made of the waste which are made by the team but also explaining the
importance of the waste management, the WOW stall got a huge round of applause from the faculty
and the students. On the last day of the convergence, the items were put on auction and the money
will be converted as the fund to the orphanages.
