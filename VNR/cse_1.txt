Connect with us
The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), established
            in the year 1995, evolved towards enhancing Computing and its
            applications to build the intellectual capital of the society. The
            department is witnessing a period of exciting growth and opportunity
            propelled by the growth of technology and its recognition through
            excellence.
The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) started in
                1995 is a renowned department for its capacity in R&D aside from
                its regular academics . B.Tech CSE is NBA Accredited and is
                recognized as a Research Center by JNTUH. The department offers 2
                UG programmes: B.Tech CSE and B.Tech CSBS (Computer Science and
                Business Systems). It also offers 2 PG programmes: MTech- Software
                Engineering (SE) and M.Tech-Computer Science and Engineering
                (CSE).
CSE department has committed and competent faculty who keep
                abreast with the upcoming technologies, through continuous
                participation in seminars and workshops, registering for advanced
                courses, and acquiring merit certifications. Dedicated labs are
                set up to foster Research in current domains such as Data
                Sciences, Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality and Gaming. The
                Computer Science & Engineering department has bagged various
                prestigious awards such as Largest CSI Student Branch, Young
                Researcher, and Best innovator. The department has MOUs with
                prestigious companies and institutes such as Cybersecurity Centre
                of excellence, TCS, Talent Sprint, Thalassemia and Sickle Cell
                Society, and many more. The girl students of the department enroll
                for “Women Empowerment Program” offered by Google, and “Amazon
                Girl Internship program” offered by Amazon and win best accolades.
                Every year TCS enrolls students from the department to train and
                certify them in the latest technologies and prepare them industry
                ready. We believe internships are complimentary to formal
                coursework, our students undergo paid national and international
                internships with companies as diverse as Google, Morgan Stanely,
                Amazon, JPMC, CHUBB, TCS, Cognizant and Zebra technologies, etc.
                Highest stipend of Rs. 110 K/month is offered by Google. The
                students from the department are recruited by leading IT companies
                as diverse as Amazon, Microsoft, Capgemini, TCS, JP Morgan,
                Deloitte, ServiceNow, etc. The highest pay package of 49 LPA has
                been offered by Google and an international pay package of 97 LPA
                is offered by Juniper Networks. The average pay package for our
                students is as high as 11 LPA. The first batch of CSBS students
                graduated this year (2023) are placed in Oracle, JPMC and TCS
                digital with pay packages of 18.5 LPA, 17.75 LPA and 7 LPA. Coming
                to higher education, the students have opted for masters in
                prestigious word class universities such as University of Texas,
                University of Florida, University of Illinois tec.
For the overall development of students, they are engaged in
                various activities umbrellaed under Professional societies and
                associations such as CSI, ISTE, IEEE, CSE Association, and Google
                developer student chapter. To keep pace with the continuously
                changing technology various guest lectures, technical symposiums,
                certification courses, workshops, and training programs are
                conducted at scheduled intervals. The TURING club in the
                department encourages students participate in coding contests and
                hackathons, such as Smart India Hackathon, Code vita, Code for
                Good, UBER challenge nationwide and they bagged awards and prizes.
                We have whirlpool of activities for students such as Career
                Vision, Mentoring, Remedial Classes, Bridge Course, Workshops,
                Placement Training, Motivational Lectures, Sports, Cultural and
                Technical Fest, Gama club for research.
Research is an integral part of academics, CSE faculty are
                actively involved in raising funding proposals with reputed
                organizations like KUDA (HRIDAY), AICTE, DST, UGC, ISSR, MHRD,
                ITRA, DRDO, TEQIP etc and are successful. We have bagged research
                projects worth Rs. 1.35126 crores and consultancy projects worth
                11 lakhs. The department has 31 applied patents, 4 granted, 26
                published and 1 filed patent in various active research areas.
                “Center of Excellence in Data Sciences”, in the department,
                fosters research in various mezzanine technologies such as machine
                learning, deep learning, data analytics, and big data. The
                department has successfully organized international e-Conference
                “International Conference on Advances in Computer Engineering and
                Communication Systems” consecutively for 3 years which was a huge
                success and we continue to progress in this direction. The strong
                CSE Alumni network remains connected and support department in the
                activities like career counseling, placement guidance and mock
                interviews, and lectures on changing needs of the industry. They
                actively take part in the design of the curriculum and their input
                keeps the faculty and students up to date with the changing
                technology scenarios.
To achieve academic and research excellence in essential
                    technologies of Computer Science and Engineering by promoting
                    a creative environment for learning and innovation.
To provide dynamic, innovative and flexible curriculum which
                    equip the students with the necessary, problem driven skills
                    to strengthen their career prospects and potential to pursue
                    higher studies.
To foster inquisitive-driven research among students and staff
                    so as to reinforce the domain knowledge and address
                    contemporary societal issues.
To inculcate ethical values, leadership qualities and
                    professional behavior skills for improving the living
                    standards of people
The graduates of the program will become proficient in the
                    principles and practices of computer science, mathematics and
                    science, enabling them to solve a wide range of computing
                    related problems.
A graduate of the Computer Science and Business Systems
                      program will be able to:
An ability to independently carry out research / investigation
                    and development work to solve practical problems.
Develop technologically competent computer professionals in
                    today’s IT centric scenario by training them in the
                    contemporary software engineering principles and paradigms.
