Our department seamlessly blends the foundational principles of
          chemistry with state-of-the-art technology to address modern
          challenges. From developing green technologies to innovating smart
          materials, we cultivate a spirit of creativity, collaboration, and
          sustainability, preparing graduates to break new ground. We are
          committed to transforming visionary ideas into practical solutions for
          a healthier, more sustainable future. Our experienced faculty, with
          academic and research expertise from prestigious institutions like
          NIT, IIT, HCU, IISc, and various state universities, is central to
          this mission. As the field of chemical sciences evolves rapidly, our
          department aims to strengthen its core by integrating with engineering
          disciplines, fostering a robust, interdisciplinary knowledge
          framework. Faculty conclaves, workshops, guest lectures, FDPs,
          conferences, symposiums, and seminars enable us to promote synergy
          between cutting-edge research methodologies.
Our department seamlessly blends the foundational principles of
              chemistry with state-of-the-art technology to address modern
              challenges. From developing green technologies to innovating smart
              materials, we cultivate a spirit of creativity, collaboration, and
              sustainability, preparing graduates to break new ground. We are
              committed to transforming visionary ideas into practical solutions
              for a healthier, more sustainable future. Our experienced faculty,
              with academic and research expertise from prestigious institutions
              like NIT, IIT, HCU, IISc, and various state universities, is
              central to this mission. As the field of chemical sciences evolves
              rapidly, our department aims to strengthen its core by integrating
              with engineering disciplines, fostering a robust,
              interdisciplinary knowledge framework. Faculty conclaves,
              workshops, guest lectures, FDPs, conferences, symposiums, and
              seminars enable us to promote synergy between cutting-edge
              research methodologies.
Our department offers a range of theory and laboratory courses,
              including Chemistry for Engineering (CFE), Engineering Chemistry
              (EC), Environmental Science (ES), Happiness and Well-being (HW),
              and Engineering Chemistry lab for B. Tech students. Additionally,
              we offer open elective courses such as Introduction to Climate
              Change and Sustainable Chemistry for Engineers. Students are
              actively engaged in research through departmental Research
              Interest Groups (RIGs) and course-based projects.
Driving chemical innovation to tackle critical societal

                  responsible chemists dedicated to sustainable development and
                  enhancing human well-being.
