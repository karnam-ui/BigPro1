A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell, & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of September, 2018
I) ED Cell Activities:

talk on “Entrepreneurship Culture” to I Year B.Tech CSE-1 students of VNRVJIET on

Professor, CSE organized the talk.
A talk on “Entrepreneurship Culture” by Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju to I- B.Tech CSE-1
students on 06-09-2018.

B-block on 07-09-2018 between 1.40 PM to 2.40 PM, in which 48 students have
attended. Mr.T.Gnana Prakash , Assistant Professor, CSE organized the talk.
A talk on “My Big Idea Competition and Vignana Jyothi Entrepreneurship Training
Model ” by Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju to I- B.Tech CSE-3 students on 07-09-2018.
