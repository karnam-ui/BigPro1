A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for
the Month of September 2025
I. ED Cell Activities:
1. Entrepreneurship Awareness Program was organized for Department of Automobile
Engineering first year students on 18.09.25 from 01.40 PM to 02.40 PM. Mr. M.
Krishna Prasad, Head - IIE and M. Venkata Ramana, i/c ED Cell created awareness
about Entrepreneurship Development Cell, Start-Ups, Innovation and Incubation. 31
students from first year attended the event.
2. M. Venkata Ramana, i/c ED Cell attended an online session on “Turning Science
into Enterprise: Story of India’s First Protease Inhibitor” on 19.09.2025 from 11.30
AM to 01.00 PM. Dr.Bijayananda Panigrahi, Scientist, ICMR-BRIC and Entrepreneur
- Biopioneer delivered the session on Biopesticides and Clinical trials.
II. MSME & VJTBI Activities:
1. Development of the Two Ideas under MSME Hackathon 2.0 are under progress.
(Dr. K. Ajay Kumar/MED and Dr. D. Ramesh Reddy/ECE). Documents to release of
II instalment were uploaded in MSME portal on 12.08.2025.
2. Development of the One Idea under MSME Hackathon 3.0 is under progress. (Mrs.
K. Bhagya Rekha, Assistant Professor, CSE, VNRVJIET).
Dr. M. Venkata Ramana
Professor of Automobile Engineering &
Incharge, ED Cell, VNRVJIET.
