A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for
the Month of August,2021.
I) ED Cell Activities:
1) Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju has registered and attended an online webinar
through Cisco Webex Events on “Agro Tourism and Agri-
Entrepreneurship” by Panduranga Taware, Director Sales & Marketing in
the AGRI , Kolkata and Sujay Khandge, Founder & Managing Director,
Yashus Digital Marketing Pvt Ltd, Pune on 07/08/2021 between 11 AM to 1
PM organized by National Institute of Agricultural Extension Management,
Rajendra Nagar, Hyderabad.
2) Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju has registered and attended an online webinar
through Zoom Meeting on TiE Sustainable Conference, a roadshow event
of TiE Sustainability Summit 2021.Opening remarks by Subinder Khurana,
Founder Story Process, Chair-Sustainability Conference with a theme of
Entrepreneurial Opportunities in Sustainability-The New Oil“ by
Manohar Reddy, President, TiE Hyderabad and CEO, Feuji Inc on
12/08/2021 between 12.05 PM to 07.00 PM Jointly organized by TiE
Hyderabad and TiE Delhi.
3) Dr.M.Venkata Ramana and Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju Coordinators of
Entrepreneurship Development Cell(ED Cell) have taken initiative in
forming 3 teams from our institution on 04-08-2021 to participate in E-Cell,
Indian Institute of Technology, Madras sponsored Entrepreneurship
Development Drive (EDD) in the month of September 2021.
4) The following student coordinators from our institution were shortlisted to
participate in Entrepreneurship Development Drive (EDD) sponsored by
E-Cell, Indian Institute of Technology, Madras in the month of September
2021.
