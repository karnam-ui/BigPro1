VNR VIGNANA JYOTHI INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING & TECHNOLOGY
DEPARTMENT OF ELECTRONICS & INSTRUMENTATION ENGINEERING
1. Department has fully furnished State of the art laboratories which are catering to program
as per curriculum requirements.
2. Laboratories are equipped with sufficient hardware and licensed software to run program
specific curriculum and beyond.
3. The department creates an atmosphere and feel for learning and knowledge upgradation.
To facilitate this, few labs are identified as project labs. The faculty members form a group
of their interest, to pursue collaborative research work. The project labs facilities to work
with emerged technologies.
4. Charts and Demo models are displayed in the laboratories.
5. Laboratory equipment layout is displayed in every laboratory.
6. The lists of experiments that are being carried out are shown in all laboratories.
7. NI Academy, Virtual Instrumentation laboratory and Analytical Instrumentation laboratory
are air conditioned.
8. Each Laboratory is equipped with writing board, computer, Internet, and other amenities
required for effective presentation.
9. Laboratories and Department library are available for all faculty and students beyond
regular working hours.
10. Five labs are identified as project labs that host the equipment beyond the curriculum for
the execution of the projects.
