A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for
the Month of August 2025
I. ED Cell Activities:
1. ED Cell has organized an Entrepreneurial Talk Show (Entrepreneurship
Awareness Session) on 01.08.2025 from 03.00 PM to 04.30 PM at APJ Abdul
Kalam Hall. Mr. Daisuke Tanji, Founder & CEO of Indobox Inc. (Japan) and Deputy
Director of the Woxsen Japan Centre has informed Entrepreneur opportunities in
Japan in collaboration with Indobox Inc. and India. 77 students from different
programs attended the event.
Mr. Daisuke Tanji, Founder & CEO of Indobox Inc. (Japan) interacting with
students on 01.08.2025
2. ED Cell in Association with VJ-Hub, VNRVJIET has organized World
Entrepreneurship Day on 21.08.2025 for all B.Tech. and M.Tech. students from
10.00 AM to 04.00 PM at APJ Abdul Kalam Hall. Eureka, Characters turned
Entrepreneurs and Battle of brands events were conducted. 84 students and 05
faculty members from different programs attended the event.
World Entrepreneurship Day on 21.08.2025
II. MSME & VJTBI Activities:
1. Development of the Two Ideas under MSME Hackathon 2.0 are under progress.
(Dr. K. Ajay Kumar/MED and Dr. D. Ramesh Reddy/ECE). Documents to release of
II instalment were uploaded in MSME portal on 12.08.2025.
2. Development of the One Idea under MSME Hackathon 3.0 is under progress. (Mrs.
K. Bhagya Rekha, Assistant Professor, CSE, VNRVJIET).
3. Revised documents to release the first instalment of MSME Hackathon 4.0 (Two
Ideas) were uploaded in the MSME portal on 05.08.2025.
4. M. Venkata Ramana, i/c ED Cell attended the review meeting with the Host
Institutions (HIs) organized by MSME-DFO, Balanagar, Hyderabad on 13.08.2025
from 3:00 PM to 05.00 PM on the ideas approved under MSME Innovative Scheme
(Hackathons 1.0 to 4.0). Mr. C.S.S. Rao, Joint Director and Mr. M. Naveen Kumar.
Asst. Director, IEDS, MSME-DFO officers reviewed the status of approved ideas
under the different Hackathons.
Review meeting on the ideas approved under MSME Innovative Scheme with the
Host Institutions (HIs) was organized by MSME-DFO, Hyderabad on 13.08.2025
5. Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSME), New Delhi called for Idea (Project)
Proposals under MSME Idea Hackathon 5.0. The same was communicated through
circular and Institute email to all faculty and students on 01 July 2025. A total of 162
Idea proposals were received. The screening and evaluation committees selected
40 best ideas as per the guidelines given by MSME. These ideas were uploaded in
MSME portal during 18-21 August 2025. The following ideas were uploaded in the
MIS portal.
