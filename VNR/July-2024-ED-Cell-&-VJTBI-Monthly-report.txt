A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME
Vignana Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI)
Activities for the Month of July 2024
I) ED Cell Activities:
1. Entrepreneurship Development Cell and SAE Collegiate Club of VNRVJIET in
association with MSME - Development and Facilitation Office, Hyderabad has
organized One Day Workshop on “Innovation and Entrepreneurship
Ecosystem” at APJ Abdul Kalam Hall on 25.07.2024 from 10.00 AM to 01.00 PM.
Shri. K. C. Choudhury, Assistant Director GR. 1, MSME – DFO, Hyderabad had
been invited as chief guest and delivered a talk on Awareness on Innovation &
Entrepreneurship and various MSME funding schemes. Shri. Sudheer Varma,
Startup Consultant, Academy of Startups delivered an invited talk on
Entrepreneurship Opportunities for Youth & Procedure and the way forward to start
a startup company. 77 faculty members from different departments attended the
workshop.
One Day Workshop on Innovation and Entrepreneurship Ecosystem at APJ Abdul
Kalam Hall
II) MSME & VJTBI Activities:
1. ED Cell coordinator has visited MSME office, Balanagar, Hyderabad on
05.07.2024 to seek advice for the correction in the incubatee name (Sneha
Ramesh) in the MSME Hackathon 3.0 idea project sanctioned to Sneha Ramesh
(Incubatee).
2. The request mail was sent on 05.07.2024 to Dy. Director, Office of the
Development Commissioner (Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises), Ministry Micro,
Small & Medium Enterprises, New Delhi for correction in the incubatee name
(Sneha Ramesh).
3. Utilization certificates for MSME Hackathon 2022 and Hackathon 2.0 are sent to
CNA Bhuvaneshwar on 20.07.2024 to release fund.
4. The first installment of MSME Hackathon 2.0 fund amount Rs. 17,85,000/- is
received to develop the Ideas. The same was communicated to the Incubatees
(Dr. K. Ajay Kumar/ MED and Dr. D. Ramesh Reddy/ECE) on 24.07.2024.
