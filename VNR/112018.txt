A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell, & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of November, 2018
I) ED Cell Activities:
i) As a Part of career vision Sri K.Kodanda Ram , Chief Coordinator, ED Cell has
interacted with the students of I-Year B.Tech, ECE-2&3 on 12-11-2018 during 2.40 PM
to 3.40 PM in Room No :B-203. Sri A.Ramesh Kumar, Associate Professor, ECE
coordinated the activity. The topics covered are ED Cell activities, Incubation and
Ideation, in which 52 students have participated.
(ii) An expert talk was arranged on “An introduction to IPR” by Dr.A.S.Rao, Director,IIEM,
on 14/11/2018 during 1.40 PM to 3.40 PM at B-Block seminar hall to I-Year M.Tech
students of all branches, in which 120 students have attended.
An expert talk on “An introduction to IPR” by Dr.A.S.Rao, Director, IIEM, Vignana Jyothi
iii) An intro session was arranged to the students of I-B.Tech CSE -1,3 & 4 by
Raghunandan Vadlahasa of CII Young Indians, Yuva , Hyderabad on 15/11/2018 at
B-Block seminar hall covering topics on CII-Yi activities during 2 PM to 3 PM for 145
students.
Interaction with the students by Raghunandan Vadlahasa of CII Young Indians, Yuva ,
Hyderabad on 15/11/2018
iv) Sri K.Kodanda Ram, Chief Coordinator, ED Cell and Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju,
Coordinator, ED Cell and CII have attended a one day conference on 14th Edition of
Man’EXE-2018 “Make in Telangana: Driving Manufacturing Excellence through
efficiency and Competitiveness” organized by Confederation of Indian
Industry(CII),Hyderabad at Hotel ITC Kakatiya, Hyderabad on 23-11-2018.
Coordinators of ED Cell at the conference organized by CII, Hyderabad on 23/11/2018
v) A talk on “Technology Challenges with respect to IPR” was organized to I50 students by
Dr.A.S.Rao, Director, IIEM, of I Year M.Tech of all branches on 28-11-2018 at B-Block
seminar hall between 1.40 PM to 3.40 PM.
