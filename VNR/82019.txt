III) MSME VJTBI:
i) Registration of Vallurupalli Nageswara Rao Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering
and Technology (VNRVJIET) as a Host Institution (HI) for setting up of a Business
Incubator (BI) was uploaded on 16-08-2019 by Entrepreneurship Development
Cell(ED Cell) of VNRVJIET through online MIS to avail the benefits under the
scheme of “Support for Entrepreneurial and Managerial Development of MSMEs
through Incubators” under the aegis of Micro Small and Medium Enterprises
(MSME), New Delhi. Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Coordinator of MSME ,Business
Incubator has initiated the process.
Government of India (GoI) Grant in Aid support will be provided to approved Host
Institutions for Entrepreneurship and Incubation related defined activities as
follows:
(a) Grant –in –Aid support for Plant and machinery to HI up to Rs 1.00 Cr (Maximum)
HIs shall be provided grant of Rs 1.00 Cr. for procurement and installation of relevant
Plant and Machines including hardware and software etc.
(b) Grant –in –Aid to HI for developing and nurturing the ideas up to max Rs 15.00
Lakh per Idea.
(c) Seed Capital Support Fund for setting up of Start-ups.
(i) Assistance of upto Rs.1.00 Cr. as grant in aid for the seed capital support to
appropriate Incubatees shall be provided.
(ii) Financial assistance may be given in the form of soft loan, Interest free loan, equity
participation, grant or combination of these.
