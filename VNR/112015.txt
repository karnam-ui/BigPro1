DATE: 27/11/2015
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell , IIPC & Vignana Jyothi
Technology Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of November, 2015
1) Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Professor in the Department of Mechanical
Engineering has delivered a talk on “Challenges & Opportunities for
Mechanical Engineers as Entrepreneurs” on 13-11-2015 during 2.00 PM
to 3 PM for the students of I- B.Tech Mechanical Engineering – I & II as a
part of Career Vision Programme. In which 80 students have attended in
Room No D-304.
2) Sri .Surendra Linga Reddy gave an expert talk on “Needs of present day
startups” on 21-11-2015 to I- B.Tech EIE – I & II students in B-Block
seminar hall during 3 PM to 4.30 PM in which 86 students have
participated as a part of EAC and career vision programme.
3) Sri .Vikram Jit Misra gave an expert talk on “Entrepreneurship and
Achievement Motivation” on 21-11-2015 to I- B.Tech IT students in
Room No C-314 during 1.20 PM to 2.30 PM in which 56 students have
participated as a part of EAC and career vision programme.
4) Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Business Incubator Manager, VJTBI has
attended 17th Selection Committee Members Meeting under the scheme
of "Support for Entrepreneurial and Managerial Development of SMEs
through Incubators" on 24-11-2015 at MSME, New Delhi for presenting
Nine short listed Business Ideas of Vignana Jyothi Technology Business
Incubator(VJTBI).
5) Sri K.Kodanda Ram, Chief Coordinator ,ED Cell gave a talk on Success
stories of recent startups on 27-11-2015 to I - B.Tech Automobile
Engineering students in Room No D-404 during 2.10 PM to 3.30 PM in
which 50 students have participated as a part of EAC and career vision
programme.
6) An incubation centre established by Four B.Tech (Mech) final year
students in VJTBI during this month with an idea of “Air-conditioning
Helmet – Development of a prototype” and is in progress.
ED Cell, IIPC & MSME VJTBI Coordinators
Dr.A.Subba Rao , Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju & Sri.K.Kodanda Ram
