Date: 30/06/2017
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell, IIPC & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of June, 20171
1) Received participation certificates for (2014-2016) Batch TEP/ISB students of
VNRVJIET from Indian School of Business (ISB), Hyderabad and Telangana Academy
for Skill and Knowledge (TASK), Hyderabad on successful completion of their certificate
programme and will be distributed on completion of the vacation.
2) Out of 61 shortlisted students of VNRVJIET for TEP/ISB certificate Course for (2017-2019)
batch, Fourty students have paid their fees.
3) Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju ,Manager,MSME VJTBI has Visited MSME, Balanagar and CITD,
Hyderabad and reviewed the progress made by MSME incubated projects.A tentative
Progress report prepared for the first phase of MSME funded projects for the accounting
year 2016-2017,subjected to the approval of the auditor concerned.
4) Mr.T.Uday,IV B.Tech Mechanical along with another three students have developed an idea
called “Kinetic Supporters”(Relates to a deployable device for stabilizing a two-wheeler if the
rider loses the control of the two-wheeler during the maneuvering) and patent search was
done by K.S.Partners, Hyderabad and found the invention to be patentable.
5) A six member team lead by Mano Sathya Sai,III,B.Tech,EIE have got approval for funding
from MSME,IISc, Bangalore to the tune of Rs 1.4 Lakhs for their innovative idea entitled
“Prosthetic Hand”.
6) ORL Makers Garage 2K17 was being organized by ORL industries in association with
VNRVJIET during 5th June -5 July,2017,in which 120 students from different institutes have
registered for this programme out of which 5 students are from VNRVJIET.
(ED Cell, IIPC & VJTBI Coordinators)
Dr.A.S. Rao, Director, IIE&M, VNRVJIET
Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju,Incubator Manager,VJTBI (MSME)
Sri. K.Kodanda Ram, Coordinator, VJTBI
