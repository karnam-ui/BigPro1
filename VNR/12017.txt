Date: 31/01/2017
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell, IIPC & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of January, 201717
1) VNR VJIET Entrepreneurship Development Cell, in collaboration with E-Cell, IIT-
Bombay has organized a one day Workshop named “Envoyage” at VJIM Auditorium on
09-01-17, in which 350 students from various colleges including Vasavi Engg. College,
CBIT, GRIET, JNTUH, OU etc have attended for the morning session and 300 students
of VNR VJIET have attended in the afternoon session. The morning session was
addressed by Mr. Jai Eapen, CEO @ Britecamp and the afternoon session was
addressed by Mr. Prashant Lingam, Co-Founder of Bamboo House India. Dr. A. S.
Rao, Director(I, I, E & G) has addressed the gathering in both the sessions. The
workshop was monitored by Dr. S.V.S.S. Srinivasa Raju and Sri K. Kodanda Ram.
Student Coordinators G. Goutham, K. Sai Maneesh and B. Dinesh Rishi have played a
key role in organizing the program successfully. Along with the guest lecture, a
competition was also conducted and 40 winners from our college have been invited to
participate in E-Summit at IIT Bombay to be organized on 28th and 29th of January.
2) Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju Professor, Coordinator, VJTBI has attended a 2-day workshop
on "Entrepreneurship Development" and received training in connection with promoting
startups during 10th & 11th ,January,2017 organized by Engineering Staff College of India
(ESCI),Hyderabad in association with The Entrepreneur Zone (TEZ). ZF & TCS delegates
visited ED Cell &VJTBI and interacted with the incubatees on 02-11-2016.
3) ZF & TCS delegates visited ED Cell & VJTBI and interacted with VJTBI Incubatees on
19-01-2017.
4) Third Local Management Committee Meeting of MSME,New Delhi sponsored Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (VJTBI) was held on 25-01-2017 for screening
innovative business ideas under the scheme of “Support for Entrepreneurial and
Management Development of SMEs through Incubators” at Corporate Interaction
Room (P001) from 12.30 PM to 5.30PM in which 18 ideas were presented and the
committed has suggested certain modifications for the refinement of ideas.
5) On the occasion of Republic Day(26-01-2017), the student body has participated in the
Salute Parade describing the activities of ED Cell and Innovation Club.
6) Axis Bank is organizing an online idea competition named “Axis Moves” intended for
the participants in the age group of 18 to 30 years. For the promotion of their event,
they have conducted a one hour info-session on 30-01-17 in our college so as to
explain about the event and to collect registrations for the same.
