Outreach Activities
The Department of CSE (CyS, DS) and AI & DS successfully conducted multiple
outreach activities in the adopted village, Peddachinthakunta. These included
formalities for village adoption, initial discussions with the villagers, a thorough
village survey, and awareness programs for school students. A dental camp was
organized, benefiting 159 individuals, including students and staff. Additionally,
a visit to a nearby old age home was a heart-touching experience, fostering
meaningful interactions and bringing joy to the elderly residents. These
initiatives aimed to enhance health, education, and social well-being within the
community.
