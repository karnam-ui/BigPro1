A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & MSME Vignana
Jyothi Technology Business Incubator (MSME VJTBI) Activities for
the Month of November,2021.
I) ED Cell Activities:
1) Dr.M.Venkata Ramana & Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju have organized ED Cell
Students Coordinators meet at PG Blok Seminar hall on 02-11-2021,
discussed regarding future action plan for organizing ECFICIO ,My Big Idea
Competition and other Entrepreneurship related activities. In which 12
students of 2nd and 3rd Year B.Tech have participated.
2) Dr.M.Venkata Ramana & Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Coordinators of ED Cell
have registered and attended a one day Hybrid mode (virtual and physical)
Conference on “Re-imagining Manufacturing: Technology in driving
Manufacturing Resilience and Excellence” at Manufacturing Excellence
(Man'Exe) 2021 (17th Edition) with the themes of (i)Digital Technologies in
driving Manufacturing Excellence: Future of Manufacturing, (ii)Building
manufacturing resilience: Learnings from Covid and strategies to overcome
future disruptions (iii) Unlock potential of MSMEs for Future growth on 08-11-
2021 at Hotel ITC Kakatiya, Hyderabad and virtual session through Cisco
Webex organized by Confederation of Indian Industry (CII),Telangana,
Hyderabad.
Mr. KT Rama Rao, Hon'ble Minister for Industries, IT and MAUD, Govt of Telangana,
released the report jointly prepared by CII and Ernst & Young, focusing on “Future
of manufacturing in a post-pandemic world”.at the Venue.
3) CII-IITH Masterclass on Electric Vehicles was organized between 10AM to 1
PM on 13 /11/2021 on cisco Webex platform. Three very senior leaders of
Suzuki Corporation based in Japan Mr. Junji Suzuki, Head of Four wheeler
EVs, Mr. Kazushi Furuki, Head- xEV, Battery 3R, Mr. Hirobumi Awakawa,
Head, battery pack development-xEVs, Suzuki Motors have delivered
Keynote address . Prof. Murthy and Prof. Ashok Pandey of IIT-H have also
involved in the programme. This event was jointly organized by CII & IITH,
Hyderabad.Four Faculty and 52 Automobile Enginerring Students have
attended the programme. This programme was attended and monitored by
Dr.M.Venkata Ramana & Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Coordinators of ED
Cell.
4) An idea presentation was given by Mr.B.Hruthik Kumar (Roll No:
18071A12C5),4th Year B.Tech 1st Semester IT-3 student of VNRVJIET on
24-11-2021at PG Block seminar hall for developing an App with the name of
“Eagerpay (Pay smart, live smart)” to Dr.M.Venkata Ramana &
Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Coordinators of ED Cell seeking for
Infrastructural, Finance and mentoring support from the institution
5) Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju & Dr.M.Venkata Ramana Coordinators of ED Cell
have registered and attended a Virtual Session On “Startups: Telangana as
Start-up Destination – Growth Story under Make in Telangana & ICONN
2021”. Inaugural address delivered by Mr Jayesh Ranjan IAS, Principal
Secretary, I & C and IT Government of Telangana on 27-11-2021 from 10.30
AM to 12.30 PM, organized by CII in association with Government of
Telangana, Hyderabad.
