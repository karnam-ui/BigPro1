A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell & Vignana Jyothi Technology

Month of July, 2019
I) TEP / ISB:

III) VJTBI:
1) A proposal for establishing New Generation Innovation and Entrepreneurship
Development Centre (NewGen IEDC) launched by National Science and Technology
Entrepreneurship Development Board (NSTEDB), Department of Science &
Technology (DST), Government of India and implemented by Entrepreneurship
Development Institute of India (EDII), Ahmedabad was uploaded on 15-07-2019.The
objective of this proposal is to support 85 student projects spreading over a span of
5 years’ and worth of Grant is Rs. 2.875 Crores , which includes Recurring and Non-
Recurring expenses. The grant of each student project is limited to Rs. 2.5 Lakhs.
