A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell, & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of February, 2019
I) ED Cell Activities:
1) A talk was arranged by Gade Sasikiran of Million Minds on the topic “Introduction to
Entrepreneurship in Robotics” on 04-02-2019 during 1.40 PM to 3.40 PM in B-Block
seminar hall for the students of III Year B.Tech who have opted for Entrepreneurship
subject as open elective. In which 162 students have participated.
2) The Indus Entrepreneurs (TiE), a non-profit venture devoted to entrepreneurs globally
through Mentoring, Networking, Education, Funding, and Incubation, has conducted a
one-day hands-on workshop pertaining to Entrepreneurship at K.S.Audoitorium,
VNRVJIET on 11-02-2019 between 10.30 AM to 3.30 PM. TiE Steering Committee
Members Mr. Bhanu Prakash Varla and Mr. Subba Raju Pericherla and TiE
Hyderabad Program Champion Mr. J. Vamsi Krishna have attended the event. On this
occasion, a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) was also exchanged between TiE
and VNRVJIET. Over 140 students participated in the event which involved hands-on
activity of creating the Lean Canvas for their business ideas. Dr. C.D. Naidu, Principal-
VNRVJIET, Dr. B. ChennaKesava Rao, Director for Advancement and Dr. A.S. Rao,
Dean-IIEG have participated in the Programme. Mr. K. Kodanda Ram , Dr. S.V.S.S.
Srinivasa Raju, Dr. Chakravarthula Kiran and Mr. Md. Aziz Athani have coordinated
the event.
Exchange of MoU between TiE and VNRVJIET on 11-02-2019 in the presence of Principal,
Director, Dean & Faculty Coordinators of VNRVJIET and the representatives of TiE
Student Participants of TiEGrad Entrepreneurship Education Programme with the Resource
persons & Faculty coordinators at K.S.Auditorium on 11-02-2019
3) Sri T.Mohit and Sri.G.Leela Sainath III Year B.Tech (EEE), Student Coordinators of
ED Cell, VNRVJIET have attended a meeting organized by Telangana State
Innovation Cell (TSIC) on 22-02-2019 between 9 AM to 3.30 PM at Pearl City,
Mr.P.Tarun, TSIC Coordinator Presided over the function. The thrust of the meeting
was on the development of Institute ED Cell.
II) TEP / ISB:

Faculty coordinators of TEP / ISB, VNRVJIET in PG Block Seminar Hall on 13-02-
2019 between 3.45 PM to 4.15 PM to review the progress made by the students of
TEP / ISB. Four students have attended the meeting. Sri Pasupulety Nikhil Kumar ,II
Year B.Tech (Civil Engineering) was nominated as Student Ambassador for (2019-
