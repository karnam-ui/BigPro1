Stentorian club, Founded in the year 2013, the club serves as a place for students to grow, express themselves, let their creativity flow, and fosters a community of wonderful friendships. The club conducts a variety of writing, speaking, and creative events throughout the year.
Stentorian has a monthly magazine called Strident, where students of the college share their writings and art work. MUN Society, a graphic designing wing called Chromoframe, Open Mic Event on the fourth Saturday of every month, Word of the Day display on the college's digital signage are some of the things that the club offers, Stentorian has a lot more!
