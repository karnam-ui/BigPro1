3D PRINTING
➢ Additive manufacturing / Rapid Prototyping / Digital
Fabrication / Digital Manufacturing
➢ Main areas of use:
Prototyping
Specialized parts – aerospace, military, biomedical
engineering, dental
Hobbies and home use
Future applications– medical (body parts), buildings and
cars
➢ 3D Printing uses software that slices the 3D model into
layers (0.01mm thick or less in most cases).
➢ Each layer is then traced onto the build plate by the printer,
once the pattern is completed, the build plate is lowered
and the next layer is added on top of the previous one.
