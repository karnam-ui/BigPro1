EDITOR’S DESK
Greetings to Faculty, Students and Friends!
Primary objective behind this initiation is to strengthen our ties with our students, alumni,
staff, parents, industries and all other stack holders of the institute. The newsletter indents to
act as a communication tool that will not only give information about activities on campus
but also will educate, inform and motivate its readers on various issues of interest. It will also
provide a platform to an individual to disseminate creative ideas. We are overwhelmed by the
response that we received from students, faculties and staff in making this newsletter
possible. In this newsletter, we have reported significant achievements and activities of
departments as well as individual student and staff. Details of recent events held on campus
are also reported. Training and placement statistics for the last pass-out batch have also been
presented. Apart from this some of the wonderful articles submitted by faculty and students
have also been showcased
We would like to also thank head of departments and other staff members for providing
information and valuable suggestions. I hope you will enjoy reading this issue !!
Thanks & Warm regards
Dr. Sujith Bobba
Assistant Professor

MESSAGE FROM HEAD OF THE DEPARTMENT
Dear Faculty and Student Friends,
I am happy to inform you that our department has made a memorandum of understanding
(MoU) on 7th November 2024 between VNR Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering and
Technology and M/s Sams Advanced Climatic Technologies Private Limited. Eminent
personalities have delivered the lectures and shared their knowledge and experience. Nearly
more than hundred participants have benefitted from this FDP. Our faculty members
participated in faculty development programs, NPTEL certification courses to enhance their
learning skills to benefit the students. Some of our faculty members actively participated in
research activities like reviewing the well reputed international SCI journals. I am happy to
share that student have successfully completed short term training programs on Machine
Learning with Python and Introduction to Digital Marketing. Guest lectures and industrial
visits are also regularly organized to give exposure to the present needs of the industry. Few
of our faculty members published research papers in reputed journals which are indexed in
SCI and Scopus.
Let’s all strive a bit more and go beyond our comfort zone to achieve higher peaks.
With best wishes
Dr. B.V.R. Ravi Kumar

STUDENT EDITOR
Dear Faculty and Department Members,
I am deeply grateful for the opportunity to be part of the editorial team for our department's
newsletter. It has been an incredibly rewarding experience to contribute to curating and
presenting the vibrant tapestry of events, accomplishments, and initiatives that define our
department. Working on the newsletter has offered me a unique window into the dynamic
efforts of our faculty and fellow students. From groundbreaking research and academic
achievements to impactful extracurricular activities, it is inspiring to witness the dedication,
passion, and innovation that thrive within our community. This role has not only sharpened
my editorial skills but also deepened my appreciation for the collaborative spirit that drives
our department forward. I feel truly honored to help highlight these efforts and share them
with all of you.
Thank you for the opportunity to be a part of something so meaningful. It is a privilege to
contribute to a platform that celebrates our collective accomplishments and reinforces the
importance of communication within our academic family

Departmental Achievements:
1. A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) was signed on 7th November 2024 between
VNR Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering and Technology and M/s Sams Advanced
Climatic Technologies Private Limited. to establishing a partnership to collaborate in
areas such as skill-based training, education, placements, industrial visits, expert lectures,
research support, and leveraging expertise for design and documentation.
2. A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) was signed on 22nd November 2024 between
VNR Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering and Technology and Integrated Cleanroom
Technologies Pvt Limited (iClean). to establishing a partnership to collaborate in areas
such as Skill Based Training, Education, Placement, Industrial Visits, and Expert
Lectures
3. A hands-on Experience on Modelling & Prototyping using 3D Printing is organized for II
B.Tech Mechanical Engineering Students on 22.10.2024. A total of 58 students has
attended the programme
4. On October 29, 2024, a group of 27 students from III B.Tech (ME-II), accompanied by
2 Faculty members – Shri. K. Krishna Murthy (Assistant Professor, ME) and Dr. V. V.
D. Sahithi (Assistant Professor, ME) – visited the TSRTC Zonal Workshop located in
Venkateswara Colony, Sai Nagar, Peerzadiguda, Hyderabad, Telangana. The industrial
visit provided students with valuable insights into the operational and maintenance
processes of public transportation vehicles, giving them practical exposure to mechanical
engineering applications in the transport sector
Faculty Achievements:
1. Dr. K. Balashowry, Professor of Mechanical Engineering, met with Mr. Gopala
Krishnan, Director of Automotive and EV Telangana, at the Sherton Hotel in Manikonda
on 14th October 2024. The meeting covered plans to establish a Center of Excellence,
schedule a guest lecture, sign an MoU for collaboration, and organize industrial visits.
They also discussed creating internship opportunities for mechanical engineering students
in the automotive and EV sector.
2. Mr. K. Krishna Murthy, Assistant Professor/ME was awarded with doctorate of
Philosophy from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad on 07-12-2024.
3. Dr. Y. Shivraj Narayan, Professor/Mechanical Engineering presenting his research
paper at the ASME 2024 International Mechanical Engineering Congress & Exposition
(IMECE 2024) organized by American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME) at
Oregon Convention Center, Portland, Oregon State, USA during November 17-21, 2024
