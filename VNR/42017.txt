Date: 30/04/2017
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell, IIPC & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of April, 20171
1) Orientation Programme on Entrepreneurship was organized by Sri Vikram Jit Misra,
NEN consultant on 03-04-2017 for the students of all branches, in which 140 students
have participated. Sri .K.Kodanda Ram, Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju and Mrs Priyanka
Singh,Assistant Professor, CSE monitored the programme.
2) Sri. Santhosh Yenamakonda ,M.D, Electronics Technology Ltd, Hyderabad has interacted
with 23 students of Automobile Engineering students on startup funding on 04-04-2017 at
D-block Room No 402,VNRVJIET.Sri .K.Kodanda Ram, Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju and
Mrs B.Rohini Naidu T & P monitored the programme.
3) Orientation Programme on Entrepreneurship of NEN was introduced by Mrs Priyanka
Singh, for I Year B.Tech. students of CSE-I & II on 22-04-2017 at Room No:C-301.
Sri.R.Kranthi kumar,Assistant Professor of CSE, Sri . K.Kodanda Ram over viewed the
programme.
4) World Intellectual Property Day-2017 celebrations were held by VJ Hub at Dr.A.P.J. Abdul
Kalam Auditorium,D-204,Yogaiah Naidu Bhavan on 26-04-2017 during 3 PM to 5 PM,in
which 23 faculty and 30 students of various branches have participated. Dr A.S.Rao has
given a power point presentation on Intellectual Property .Mrs M.S.Devi,Partner ,K&S
Partners has delivered a lecture on the need and importance of Intellectual Property for the
Students and Faculty.On this occasion an essay writing competition was organized by ED
Cell to VNRVJIET students on Patent Search, in which 20 students have participated and
the following three students were selected as prize winners for whom Certificates and cash
awards given.
