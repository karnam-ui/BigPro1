Vignana Jyothi
Hostels
Constructed as per Fire Safety and HMDA norms.
HOSTEL
Location: Conveniently located within one kilometer of the
campus.
Occupancy: Separate hostel buildings accommodate 1,116
boys and 1056 girls.
Elevators: Four high-speed elevators with distinct entry and
exit points for junior and senior students.
Biometric System: Advanced biometric and facial
recognition systems for secure attendance tracking.
Electricity: 24-hour power backup.
Internet: Equipped with a high-speed internet connection.
Student Rooms: Spacious rooms with individual cots, study
tables, study chairs, and a dedicated cabin for every
student.
Medical Facilities: Dedicated medical facilities for boys and
girls.
Geyser & Laundry: Equipped with Geysers and Laundry
facilities.
FOOD
Quality of Food: Nutritious and hygienically prepared food
(Two types of breakfast available daily, including non-veg
options and eggs served twice a week. Special dishes
offered for vegetarians.)
Water Availability: Mineral drinking water dispensers are
available on every floor, providing both cold and warm
options.
Staff: Experienced and well-trained mess staff and stewards.
Dining Area: A spacious dining area designed for comfort.
In-Campus Lunch: Hot lunch is served at the campus on
working days
SECURITY
Security: Security staff are available around the clock.
CCTV Monitoring: Continuous 24/7 CCTV monitoring for
enhanced safety.
Supervision: Resident supervisors are assigned to ensure the
safety and security of the inmates.
SPORTS AND FITNESS
Gym Facilities: Each hostel features a well-equipped gym
facility.
Indoor Games: Equipped with indoor games such as table
tennis, foosball, carrom, and chess.
Outdoor Games: Outdoor courts are available for volleyball,
badminton, basketball, and more.
YOGA: A dedicated space for YOGA sessions.
ENTERTAINMENT
Recreation Room: A recreation room equipped with a smart
TV and a projector for entertainment.
Celebrations: Regular celebrations and spiritual
observances for various festivals.
Internal Competitions: Periodic internal competitions will be
organized to help discover and nurture the hidden talents
of the students.
TRAINING AND LEARNING CENTRES
Discussion Rooms: Dedicated discussion rooms for
collaborative brainsto rming and knowledge sharing.
In-house Training: Separate training is provided for
students.
Lab: A dedicated lab with a projector. Research:
Workspace for research work in the hostel.
Synergy Activities: Collaborative synergy activities between
seniors and juniors to foster learning and development.
MISCELLANEOUS
Food Court: A food court and a stationery store are
conveniently located within the hostel premises.
Transportation Services: Reliable transportation services are
available between the hostel and the college.
Hygiene: Trained housekeeping staff to maintain the hostel
hygiene.
Pest Control: Periodic spraying will be carried out in and
around the hostel premises to control pest infestation.
