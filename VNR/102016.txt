Date: 31/10/2016
A Report on Entrepreneurship Development Cell, IIPC & Vignana Jyothi Technology
Business Incubator (VJTBI) Activities for the
Month of October, 2016
1) VNR VJIET ED Cell has organized Entrepreneurship Awareness Drive (EAD-16) in
collaboration with E-Cell, IIT Kharagpur in K.S. Auditorium on 4th October,2016 between
10AM and 2 PM. The program is a grand success in which 1000+ students have attended
and got benefitted out of the lectures, talks, open house session, panel discussion and
workshop. The key note address “General Approach toward Entrepreneurship and his
journey,” was given by Sri B.V.R. Mohan Reddy, Chairman, CYIENT. Then, Dr.C.D. Naidu,
Principal, VNR VJIET gave a welcome address to the participants. Then Dr. A.S.Rao,
Director, IIE & G gave his views on entrepreneurship. Sri B.Sarat Gopal, General
Secretary, VNR VJIET addressed the gathering. Sri Narsi Reddy Posham, CEO, Smartron
addressed the gathering and shared his views. Sri Bharat Masetty, Student Associate
Member, E-Cell, IIT Kharagpur gave presentation on IIT Kharghpur E-cell initiatives.
2) On the eve of Entrepreneurship Awareness Drive (EAD-16) an open house was conducted
by VNR VJIET ED Cell in collaboration with E-Cell, IIT Kharagpur on 4th October between
11AM and 12 Noon on the topic “Innovation on Health Care Start-Ups: what counts &why?”
by Mr.Mohammed Abubakr,founder and CEO, Book MEDS and Mr.Neehar Cherabuddi
(co-founder &CEO, DoctorC).
3) On the eve of Entrepreneurship Awareness Drive (EAD-16) a panel discussion was
organized on 4th October between 12 Noon and 1PM on the topic “The Missing Bits in the
start-up Ecosystem in India”. Mr.Kiran Gali,founder and CEO, Number Mall, Mr.Raghu
Babu Gunturu,(partner R&A Associates,co-founder & CEO, Fresh Chops) with
Mr.Lakshman Papineni(Co-founder & CEO, App Virality),Mr.Prashanth Garapati(Co-
founder & CEO, Commut)have participated in the panel discussion. The students posed
several questions to the panel members and obtained suitable answers from the panel
members out of their experience.
4) On the eve of Entrepreneurship Awareness Drive (EAD-16) a work shop on IBM BLUE MIX
APP development was held at A.P.J.Abdul Kalam Auditorium in VNR VJIET from 11 AM to
1 PM . The representatives from IBM gave their presentation on App development using
BLUE MIX to 150 students of CSE and IT branches from VNR VJIET, GRIET, GITAM.
5) On the eve of Entrepreneurship Awareness Drive (EAD-16) a work shop was organized in
K.S.Auditorium on the topic “Working your way through stock market using a DMAT
account” between 1 PM to 2PM. Mr.Y.Santhosh Reddy,Representative from National
Securities Depository Limited (NSDL) gave a power point presentation on how to invest in
stock markets, in which 130 students have attended.

9) As a part of Career Vision programme Dr S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, Coordinator, ED Cell

11) As a part of Career Vision Programme Sri.K.Kodanda Ram, Chief Coordinator, ED Cell,

which 50 students have attended.
12) Dr.A.S.Rao ,Director, IIE & G, Sri.K.Kodanda Ram, Chief Coordinator, ED Cell and
Sri K.Vijaya Chandar ,Assistant Professor, EIE have interacted with the students of I Year,
B.Tech, EIE -1 & 2 about Innovations in the Health Care Sector as a part of Career Vision
programme in B-Block Seminar hall and invited ideas from the students instantaneously on
26-10-2016 in which 100 students have attended and 68 of them have submitted their
ideas. It has been decided to encourage five best innovative ideas with a cash prize of Rs
1,000/-each.
13) VNRVJIET ED Cell has organized My Big Idea Competition (MBIC) with a open theme on
27-10-2016 at PG Block Seminar hall between 2 PM to 4.30 PM, in which Dr.A.S.Rao,
Dr.S.V.S.S.Srinivasa Raju, & Sri. K.Kodanda Ram have acted as Judges to screen the
ideas. Students Innovation Club Members, G.Goutham, K.Sai Maneesh Reddy, V.Anusha,
B Dinesh Rishi and R.Rishitha have organized the event. Fourteen students of VNRVJIET
have presented their ideas in the competition.

14) Jarsh Advanced Safety Systems(Temperature Controlled Helmet):
This project is in the verge of completing the prototype product development and is
presently testing penultimate designs.
Road map after completion of product-
1) Internal testing of final prototype
2) Production of 5 prototypes for intense field test
3) Production of 30 samples for sample testing to B2B customers
4) Acquire orders from B2B customers based on sample testing results
The team along with Mr. Narayanara Rao SVN, Manager, VJ- Hub has presented a pitch
at IISc Banglore for collaboration of VJ-Hub with MSME. The overall response was
positive and results are awaited. They will be released by next week tentatively.
Sales talks with B2B customers are in progress and a detailed 5 year vision and road map
is in process of preparation. A detailed concept view of future has been prepared.
